Diaspora Education Collaboration: UPI Cibiru Conducts PPL at SB PERMAI Pulau Pinang

 

The Overseas Field Experience Practice Program (PPL) for students of the Primary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education, Cibiru Campus, officially opened on Monday, April 13 2026. This activity was carried out in the Indonesian Community Organization Guidance Studio (PERMAI) Pulau Pinang, Malaysia.

The opening ceremony was attended by representatives   of SB Permai Pulau Pinang, field supervisor, namely Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., MH, PPL student participants consisting of Intan Samrotul Fuadah, Rhaudya Zihan Esa Maghfira, Dela Lestari, Siti Khoerunnisa, and Hanum Eliza, as well as some fourth grade students. This program is part of an effort to strengthen students' international experience in the context of Indonesian diaspora community education.

One of the main activities in this event was the signing of a stamped Cooperation Agreement and Implementation Agreement between the Indonesian Education University (UI) Cibiru Campus and SB PERMAI Pulau Pinang. This signing serves as a formal foundation for the implementation of the Overseas PPL program and affirms both parties' commitment to supporting the improvement of the quality of education for Indonesian children abroad.

In their remarks, representatives from PERMAI Pulau Pinang expressed their hope that students would actively contribute to learning activities and contribute to strengthening the identity and character of diaspora students. Meanwhile, the supervising lecturer emphasized that this experience focuses not only on teaching practice but also on social adaptability and cross-cultural understanding.

Through this activity, UPI Cibiru PGSD students are expected to be able to develop pedagogical and professional competencies contextually, while broadening their global perspectives and strengthening their concern for the education of Indonesian children in the diaspora community.
