Validation of Foreign Language Proficiency by the Language Center as a Requirement for Registration for the Trial Examination

Cibiru, June 3, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru announced that validating foreign language proficiency is now a primary requirement for registering for the final exam . Students are required to take a foreign language proficiency test administered by the Language Center to fulfill their academic qualifications.

This policy aims to ensure that students have adequate foreign language skills, particularly in understanding international references and literature relevant to their field of study. A foreign language validation certificate from the Language Center will be an administrative requirement before students can register for the thesis defense.

Students can register to take a foreign language proficiency test through the system provided by the UPI Language Center. The exam covers reading, writing, listening , and speaking skills , meeting international standards.

With this policy, it is hoped that students will be better prepared to face academic and professional challenges, and will be able to access more scientific resources in foreign languages ​​that support the preparation of their scientific works.

Further information regarding registration procedures and the schedule for foreign language proficiency tests can be accessed through the official website of the UPI Language Center or by contacting the academic section of the study program.

Selamat kepada Tim Inaster atas Lolosnya Pendanaan Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa (PKM) 2024!

Cibiru, 22 Juni 2024 — Program Studi Pendidikan Multimedia Kampus UPI di Cibiru mengucapkan selamat kepada Tim Inaster yang berhasil lolos dalam pendanaan Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa (PKM) 2024. Keberhasilan ini merupakan hasil kerja keras dan dedikasi tim dalam merancang proposal yang inovatif dan kreatif.

Tim Inaster terdiri dari mahasiswa-mahasiswa berbakat yang memiliki komitmen tinggi untuk mengembangkan ide-ide yang bermanfaat bagi masyarakat. Dengan lolosnya pendanaan PKM, mereka akan memiliki kesempatan untuk mewujudkan proyek mereka dan memberikan dampak positif bagi lingkungan sekitar.

Pendanaan PKM adalah program yang diadakan oleh Kementerian Pendidikan, Kebudayaan, Riset, dan Teknologi untuk mendorong kreativitas dan inovasi di kalangan mahasiswa. Tim Inaster diharapkan dapat menjalankan proyek mereka dengan baik dan menghasilkan karya yang berkualitas.

Sekali lagi, selamat kepada Tim Inaster! Semoga keberhasilan ini menjadi langkah awal menuju prestasi yang lebih besar di masa depan.

Selamat kepada Wisudawan dan Wisudawati Gelombang 2 Tahun 2024

We hope the graduates will carry on the knowledge and experience they gained at UPI Cibiru and continue to contribute to society and the professional world. Congratulations on your new journey, and may success always accompany you in your future endeavors!
