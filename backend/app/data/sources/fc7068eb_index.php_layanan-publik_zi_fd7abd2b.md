POLICY

The UPI Integrity Zone Program Policy aims to:

Realizing clean governance that is free from corruption, collusion and nepotism (KKN).
Improve accountability and transparency of organizational performance through measurable and accountable planning, implementation, and reporting.
Building a work culture of integrity and professionalism within the academic community and educational staff.
Strengthening internal monitoring and control systems to prevent abuse of authority and conflicts of interest.
Improving the quality of public services that are fast, accurate, transparent, and oriented towards user satisfaction.
Integrating the principles of bureaucratic reform into academic, administrative, research and community service policies.
