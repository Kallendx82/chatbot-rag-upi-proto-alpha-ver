Program Internasionalisasi

Serangkaian aktifitas internasionalisasi UPI Tasikmalaya yang terdiri dari academic mobility, partnership, projects, dan penelitian bagi mahasiswa dan dosen.

Directorate of International Affairs (DIA) is the university division dedicated to support the internationalization goals of the university and to handle international mobility involving our and international civitas academica. Our goal is to assist the international students and scholars handle their academic-related matters at Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia and to bridge Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia’s civitas academica with overseas universities.
