UPI Launches Complaint Hotline for Cadre Development Activities

Bandung, October 18, 2024 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) has taken decisive steps to ensure that its cadre development activities adhere to ethical values ​​and campus regulations. In an official circular issued on October 18, 2024, UPI announced the opening of a complaint hotline accessible to all students.

This hotline aims to provide a safe and reliable channel for students who wish to report irregularities in cadre development activities. Reports can be made via the official telephone number or email address provided by the university.

Students who wish to report a problem simply need to provide basic information such as a description of the incident, the time and location of the incident, and the parties involved (if known). The university guarantees complete confidentiality for those reporting the incident.

The university emphasizes that any form of violation, including physical violence, psychological pressure, or discrimination, will not be tolerated in cadre development activities. With the complaint hotline , UPI hopes to create a more positive cadre development culture that benefits student character development.
