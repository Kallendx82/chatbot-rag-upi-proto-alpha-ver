UPI Cibiru Students Implement PKP 2 PKM Towards Strengthening Innovation Capacity (SDGs 4 & 9)

The journey to achievement is inseparable from passion, discipline, and continuous learning. Students at the Cibiru Campus of the Indonesian Education University (UIPI) are currently participating in the Student Creativity Program (PKM) phase 2 , as part of their efforts to develop competency, innovation, and collaboration in research and community service.

The implementation of PKP 2 PKM contributes to the achievement of:

SDGs 4 – Quality Education:
through strengthening students' critical thinking, creativity, and research skills as part of a quality higher education process.

SDGs 9 – Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure:
by encouraging the birth of innovative ideas, prototypes, and solutions that have an impact on society and technological development.

The enthusiasm and hard work of students at this stage are expected to be a strong foundation to go further until they reach the National Student Scientific Week (PIMNAS) .

Keep moving, keep innovating, and give your best — the future of innovation starts with today's steps.
