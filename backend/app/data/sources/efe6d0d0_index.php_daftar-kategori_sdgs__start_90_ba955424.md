Implementation of Requests for Audit Supporting Documents in the RKAT Monitoring for the Fourth Quarter of Fiscal Year 2025

Friday, January 9, 2026

The implementation of the request for supporting documents for this audit is part of the RKAT escort audit activity for Quarter IV of Fiscal Year 2025 which was carried out in the period October to December 2025. This activity aims to ensure the conformity of program and budget implementation with predetermined plans, as well as supporting the realization of accountable and transparent governance within the Indonesian University of Education.

In its implementation, the Internal Control Unit (SPI) of the Indonesian Education University (UII) requested all work unit leaders, including regional security units, to submit complete data for the October 2025 Accountability Report (LPJ) along with supporting documents determined in accordance with the RKAT oversight audit provisions. The requested documents will serve as evaluation and verification materials for activity implementation and budget realization in the fourth quarter.

The document request and submission activities were represented by the Deputy Director of Academic, Student Affairs, and Quality Assurance, Eng. Munawir, MT, and the Head of the General Administration Section (Kasie Adum), Didi Warsidi, SAP.  All submitted documents were then used as the basis for the implementation of the RKAT escort audit for the Fourth Quarter of the 2025 Fiscal Year by the UPI Internal Control Unit.

Implementation of the Qualification Exam as an Academic Evaluation Stage for the Master's Study Program

Thursday, January 8, 2026

A total of 38 Master's Program (S2) students from the UPI Cibiru Campus participated in the Qualification Exam, held as part of the academic evaluation phase. The exam was held in the 2nd Floor Classroom and was open to students who had met the academic requirements for the   qualification exam.

The qualifying exam is a crucial stage in a master's student's academic journey before entering the thesis research process. This exam tests students' academic readiness, scientific understanding, and analytical skills relevant to their chosen field of study.

The exam proceeded smoothly and in accordance with applicable academic regulations. The students took each stage of the exam seriously, while the examining team conducted the evaluation process objectively and professionally.

Program Studi PGSD UPI Cibiru Resmi Membuka Sidang Proposal sebagai Tahap Awal Penelitian Mahasiswa

Kamis, 08 Januari 2025

Program Studi Pendidikan Guru Sekolah Dasar (PGSD) UPI Cibiru secara resmi membuka pelaksanaan Sidang Proposal sebagai tahap awal penelitian mahasiswa. Kegiatan ini diikuti oleh 117 mahasiswa Program Studi PGSD yang telah memenuhi persyaratan akademik untuk melanjutkan proses penelitian tugas akhir.

Pelaksanaan sidang proposal akan berlangsung di Gedung Baru Lantai 5, Kampus UPI Cibiru. Dalam kegiatan ini, mahasiswa dibagi ke dalam dua jalur pelaksanaan, yaitu jalur sidang skripsi dan jalur jurnal, sesuai dengan skema penyelesaian tugas akhir yang dipilih oleh masing-masing mahasiswa.

Pembagian jalur tersebut bertujuan untuk memberikan fleksibilitas sekaligus memastikan proses evaluasi akademik berjalan secara optimal. Mahasiswa pada jalur sidang skripsi akan mempresentasikan rancangan penelitian yang akan dikembangkan menjadi skripsi, sementara mahasiswa pada jalur jurnal diarahkan untuk menyusun penelitian yang berorientasi pada publikasi ilmiah.

Melalui pelaksanaan sidang proposal ini, mahasiswa diharapkan memperoleh masukan konstruktif dari dosen penguji guna menyempurnakan perencanaan penelitian, baik dari aspek substansi maupun metodologi. Kegiatan ini juga menjadi bagian dari komitmen Program Studi PGSD UPI Cibiru dalam menjaga kualitas akademik serta mendukung penguatan budaya riset di lingkungan kampus.

Kolaborasi Strategis BNN dan UPI Cibiru Bersama Mahasiswa: Membangun Kesadaran sekaligus Rencana Edukasi dan Pencegahan Penyalahgunaan Narkoba

Rabu, 07 Januari 2025

Kegiatan ini merupakan langkah awal kolaborasi strategis antara Badan Narkotika Nasional (BNN) Kota Cimahi dan Kampus UPI Cibiru dalam upaya membangun kesadaran serta menyusun rencana edukasi dan pencegahan penyalahgunaan narkoba di lingkungan kampus. Kegiatan ini bertujuan untuk memperkuat sinergi antar lembaga dalam menciptakan lingkungan pendidikan yang sehat, aman, dan bebas dari penyalahgunaan narkoba.

Pada kesempatan ini, empat orang perwakilan dari BNN Kota Cimahi melakukan kunjungan ke Kampus UPI Cibiru. Kunjungan tersebut dilaksanakan dalam rangka menjajaki  rencana kerja sama dan membahas kolaborasi yang berfokus pada pencegahan penyalahgunaan narkoba serta penanganan mahasiswa/siswa yang berpotensi terdampak atau terindikasi penyalahgunaan narkoba. Diskusi meliputi rencana pelaksanaan kegiatan edukasi, sosialisasi, pendampingan, serta mekanisme rujukan dan penanganan yang sesuai dengan ketentuan yang berlaku.

This activity is expected to foster a shared commitment between the National Narcotics Agency (BNN) and UPI Cibiru to implement sustainable drug education and prevention programs, engage students as agents of change, and strengthen student support and treatment systems. This collaboration is a crucial part of realizing a campus that cares about the health, safety, and future of the younger generation.

Ornamental Plant Care and Planting: Supporting Greening Programs as an Environmental Conservation Effort

Wednesday, January 7, 2025

The ornamental plant care and planting activities are carried out as part of the institution's commitment to supporting greening programs and preserving and enhancing the beauty of the environment. These activities aim to create a green, beautiful, and comfortable environment while also improving the quality of open spaces within the institution's premises.

During this activity, several types of ornamental plants were planted, including five jawer kotok plants, six lipstick plants, five ivy plants, and five green kribo plants. These plant species were selected based on environmental conditions and aesthetic value to enhance the beauty of the surrounding area.

The ornamental plant planting took place in the rear area, near the theater garden, which is a strategic area for green space development. This activity is expected to positively impact environmental quality, beautify the surrounding area, and be part of ongoing efforts to maintain the environment and implement greening programs.
