Academic Information: UKT Payments, IRS Submissions, and Online Trusteeship for Odd Semester 2025/2026

The following is news compiled based on the contents of Circular Letter Number 35 of 2025 from the Indonesian University of Education:

Bandung, June 2, 2025 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) officially issued Circular Letter Number 35 of 2025 concerning the technical implementation of UKT/BPP payments , IRS filling , and online guardianship for the Odd Semester of the 2025/2026 Academic Year. This letter was addressed to all deans, regional campus directors, SPs directors, study program heads, and students at UPI.

In the circular signed by the Vice Chancellor for Education and Student Affairs, Prof. Didi Sukyadi , several important points were mentioned as follows:

A. UKT/BPP Payment

UKT/BPP payments for existing students are scheduled from June 22 to July 31, 2025 .

For students who will apply for academic leave, applications and payments can be made from June 7 to August 31, 2025 .

B. IRS Filing and Online Guardianship

The IRS and online guardianship filling period will take place from August 1–27, 2025 , through the page https://student.upi.edu for students and https://siak.upi.edu/perwalian for academic advisors.

Using a SIAKKu account for students and a SInNDo account for lecturers is a primary requirement for this online process. Students or lecturers who do not yet have an account are asked to register immediately with the STI Directorate.

The IRS filing and guardianship schedule is divided by year of birth:

Class Year	IRS & Trust Filing Schedule
2019/2020 – 2020/2021	August 3 – 8, 2025
2021/2022 – 2022/2023	August 9 – 14, 2025
2023/2024 – 2024/2025	August 15 – 20, 2025
2025/2026	August 21 – 26, 2025

Students who do not complete the IRS within the specified time are required to make improvements to their study contract through the PRS (Change of Study Plan) period on September 15-22, 2025 .

Academic supervisors who have not yet approved (acc) the student's IRS are also required to complete it during the PRS period.

This circular serves as an official guideline for conducting online academic administration within UPI to support the effectiveness and efficiency of technology-based academic services.
