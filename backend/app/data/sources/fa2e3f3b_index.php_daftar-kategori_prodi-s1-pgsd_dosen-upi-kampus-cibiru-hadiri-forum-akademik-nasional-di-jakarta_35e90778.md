UPI Cibiru Campus Lecturers Attend the National Academic Forum in Jakarta

One of the PGSD lecturers at UPI Cibiru Campus was seen attending an academic activity held at a star-rated hotel in Jakarta on Tuesday, August 12, 2025. The event was held in Ballroom A. The event was attended by academics, practitioners, and education policymakers from various universities in Indonesia. This forum served as a strategic platform for formulating steps to strengthen the quality of education and the professionalism of educators.

The presence of UPI Cibiru Campus lecturers in this forum demonstrates the institution's commitment to continue playing an active role in the development of national education, particularly in strengthening teacher capacity.
