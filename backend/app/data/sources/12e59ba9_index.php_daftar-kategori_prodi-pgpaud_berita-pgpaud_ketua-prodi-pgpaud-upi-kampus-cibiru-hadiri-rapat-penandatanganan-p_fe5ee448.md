Head of the PGPAUD Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus Attends the Performance Agreement Signing Meeting

Cibiru, September 30, 2025 — The Head of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) of the Indonesian University of Education, Cibiru Campus, attended the Performance Agreement Signing Meeting which was held as a strategic step in strengthening governance and performance accountability within the campus environment.

This activity was attended by heads of work units, including heads of study programs at UPI Cibiru Campus, to ensure alignment between each unit's performance targets and the institution's overall goals. By signing this performance document, each leader affirmed their commitment to implementing work programs effectively, measurably, and oriented towards improving the quality of academic services.

During the meeting, the Head of the Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Program emphasized the importance of collaboration, sustainable planning, and an evaluation-based work culture as the foundation for achieving the Tri Dharma of Higher Education. The signing also served as a momentum to strengthen integrity, transparency, and a shared commitment to advancing the quality of early childhood education through various academic programs, research, and community service.

Through this activity, PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Campus emphasized its role as a study program that is oriented towards improving quality, professionalism, and making real contributions to educational development in Indonesia.

The signing of this performance agreement aligns with SDG 4: Quality Education, particularly in terms of improving transparent, accountable, and quality-oriented education governance. Through systematic performance planning, PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Campus strives to ensure that all academic and non-academic activities support the creation of quality, inclusive, and sustainable education. This activity also reflects the institution's commitment to strengthening education management as a crucial foundation for achieving equity and improving the quality of early childhood education services in Indonesia.
