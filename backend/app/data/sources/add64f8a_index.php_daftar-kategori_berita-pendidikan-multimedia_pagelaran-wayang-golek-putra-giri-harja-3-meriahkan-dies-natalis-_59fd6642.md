Putra Giri Harja 3 Wayang Golek Performance Enlivens UPI's 70th Anniversary and 14th Lustrum

Bandung, October 25, 2024 — As part of the 70th anniversary celebrations and the 14th Lustrum of the Indonesian Education University (UPI), a Wayang Golek performance by Putra Giri Harja 3 was held on October 25, 2024, at the UPI ATM Center Parking Lot. This event aims to preserve traditional West Javanese arts and culture and strengthen cultural identity among the academic community.

The event was attended by lecturers, students, alumni, and the general public, who enthusiastically watched the performance until the very end. Besides providing entertainment, this activity also served as a means of cultural education for the younger generation.

In the spirit of preserving culture, UPI continues to strive to integrate traditional values ​​into modern education. This Wayang Golek performance demonstrates UPI's commitment to promoting harmony between education, culture, and tradition.
