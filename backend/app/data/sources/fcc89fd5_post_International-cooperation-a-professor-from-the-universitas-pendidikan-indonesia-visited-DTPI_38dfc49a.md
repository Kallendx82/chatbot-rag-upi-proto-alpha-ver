Penyunting Berita : Mulki Anaz Aliza      Waktu Posting : 01-11-2024 06:01:28      Kategori : Intenasional      Dilihat : 190

On November 1 of this year, Professor J. Julia from Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia visited the Denov Institute of Entrepreneurship and Pedagogy (DTPI). The Denov Institute of Entrepreneurship and Pedagogy (DTPI) is a pioneering educational institution in Uzbekistan, established in 2018 as a branch of Termiz State University. Initially starting with two faculties, the institute has since expanded to include four faculties and 16 departments, currently serving 8,212 students across 24 bachelor’s programs and 12 master’s specialties. ✅ The purpose of the visit was to establish cooperative relations with the Department of Primary Education Methodology. Today, on November 2, the rector of DTPI, O.A. Ruziyev, welcomed the esteemed professor, introduced him to our institute, and discussed future cooperation plans. ⚡️ During the meeting, agreements were reached on establishing a student exchange program starting in the first quarter of 2025, scientific supervision of master's degree students, and the organization of professional development programs for professors and teachers. Additionally, cooperation agreements were signed between the Faculty of Pedagogy and the Department of Primary Education Methodology. ❇️ This visit and cooperation will play an important role in improving the quality of education in the future and will create opportunities for mutual exchange of experience.
