Campus Environmental Care: Cleaning and Mowing of Open Area Grass to optimize cleanliness and beauty

UPI Cibiru Campus conducted a cleaning and grass-cutting activity in open areas around the campus, carried out by campus janitors. This activity demonstrates the campus's commitment to maintaining a clean, beautiful, and comfortable environment to optimally support learning, work, and interaction among the academic community.

As an educational institution, UPI Cibiru Campus views environmental care as a crucial element in supporting the quality of educational services. A clean and well-maintained campus area directly impacts the comfort of students, faculty, and educational staff in carrying out academic and non-academic activities. A beautiful environment also strengthens the campus's image as a humanistic, healthy, and welcoming learning space for all members of the campus community.

The impact of these activities is increasingly felt through the preservation of the campus's aesthetics, the reduction of potentially disruptive undergrowth, and the improvement of open spaces as a means of interaction and informal learning. This demonstrates the role of UPI Cibiru Campus in sustainably managing campus facilities to support a more optimal learning experience.

This activity also supports SDG 3 (Healthy and Prosperous Lives) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities) through the management of clean and sustainable green spaces. As a strategic recommendation, regular campus maintenance and a shared awareness among the entire academic community is essential for maintaining a clean environment.
