The Managers of the Eduhumaniora and Cakrawala Dini Journals Receive Awards from UPI

Bandung, June 13, 2025 — Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) held an Incentive Award Ceremony for SINTA Indexed Journal Managers in 2025 on Friday, June 13, 2025. This event took place in the PPG Auditorium on the 6th floor, as a form of appreciation for the contribution of scientific journal managers within UPI in supporting the improvement of the quality of national scientific publications.

In this activity, journal managers from various faculties and regional campuses of UPI whose journals have been indexed in the SINTA (Science and Technology Index) Information System received awards in the form of incentives. One that received attention was the Elementary Education Journal (EduHumaniora) managed by lecturers from UPI Cibiru Campus, namely Yunus Abidin, Hana Yunansah, and Fully Rakhmayanti, which was included in the SINTA 3 category and received an incentive of Rp 15,000,000. In addition, the Cakrawala Dini Journal under the auspices of the PGPAUD study program also received the same award. The Cakrawala Dini Journal is managed by Kuswanto and Endah Silawati.

The symbolic handover of the incentives was carried out directly by the university leadership, demonstrating UPI's concrete support for the management of quality scientific journals. This event also served as a forum for fostering camaraderie and strengthening the shared commitment among journal managers to continuously improve the quality of scientific governance and publications within UPI.

UPI Chancellor, Prof. M. Solehuddin, in his remarks stated that the provision of this incentive is not only a form of appreciation, but also as motivation for journals at UPI to continue to develop, increase their rankings in national and international indexes, and make a real contribution to the institution's academic reputation.

With the spirit of "Impactful Diktisaintek", UPI hopes that this step will encourage journal managers to continue maintaining quality, publication ethics, and innovation in managing scientific journals in the digital era.
