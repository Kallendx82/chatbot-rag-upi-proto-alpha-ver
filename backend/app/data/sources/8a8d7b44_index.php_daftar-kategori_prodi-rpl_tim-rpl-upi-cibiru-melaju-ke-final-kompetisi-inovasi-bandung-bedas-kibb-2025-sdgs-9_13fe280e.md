The UPI Cibiru RPL Team Advances to the Finals of the 2025 Bandung Bedas Innovation Competition (KIBB) (SDGs 9)

The Software Engineering (RPL) Study Program at the Cibiru Campus of the Indonesia University of Education (UIPI) has once again achieved a remarkable achievement.
The RPL student team advanced to the final round of the 2025 Bandung Bedas Innovation Competition (KIBB) , an event designed to encourage creativity and technological innovation to support the development of the Bandung City region.

The UPI Cibiru RPL team's participation in this competition demonstrates the students' commitment to developing innovative, technology-based solutions relevant to community needs. This initiative aligns with Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 9—Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure —which emphasizes the importance of strengthening technological and innovation capacity to accelerate sustainable development.

Through this competition, RPL students not only hone their technical skills and creative thinking, but also contribute significantly by presenting innovative ideas for the advancement of smart and sustainable cities.

Congratulations to the RPL team on their achievement! May this success inspire other students to continue innovating and making a positive impact on their communities and the environment.
