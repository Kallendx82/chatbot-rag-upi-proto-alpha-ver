The PGPAUD Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus Holds an End-of-Semester Evaluation Meeting and Preparations for the Start of the Odd Semester of 2025/2026

Cibiru, July 8, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru held an end-of-semester evaluation meeting and an early-semester meeting on Tuesday, July 8, 2025. This activity took place offline and was attended by all lecturers in the PGPAUD Study Program. This meeting was led directly by the Head of the PGPAUD Study Program, who directed the discussion and coordination. The main focus of the meeting included the evaluation of lecturer performance assessments during the even semester of the 2024/2025 academic year, lecture preparation for the odd semester of 2025/2026, and the preparation and adjustment of the lecture schedule. In this meeting, lecturers shared reflections on the implementation of learning, the achievements of course outputs, and challenges faced during the past semester. In addition, several strategies for strengthening the quality of learning that will be implemented in the upcoming semester were also agreed upon. This activity is an important moment for the PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Study Program to maintain the continuity of academic quality, increase collaboration between lecturers, and align the lecture agenda with institutional policies and student needs.
