Educational Synergy: Labschool Elementary, Middle, and High School Coordination Meeting with Campuses to Strengthen Academic Collaboration

UPI Cibiru Campus continues to demonstrate its commitment to strengthening the integrated education ecosystem through coordination meetings with elementary, middle, and high schools (SD, SMP, and SMA Labschool). This activity is a strategic step in building synergy between elementary, secondary, and higher education levels to ensure sustainable learning quality and student development.

UPI Cibiru Campus serves not only as a center for higher education but also as a driving force for academic collaboration, fostering learning innovation within the Labschool environment. This synergy is expected to deliver an educational model that is adaptive and relevant to current developments.

The involvement of study programs also contributes to strengthening the curriculum, developing teacher competencies, and implementing best practices in learning as a concrete manifestation of improving the quality of education in partner schools.

The coordination meeting was held in the Director's Room at the UPI Cibiru Campus and attended by campus leaders and the principals of Labschool elementary, middle, and high schools. This forum served as a forum for aligning work programs, strengthening communication, and fostering ongoing collaboration between the campus and schools.

This activity also supports the achievement of SDGs 4: Quality Education , through efforts to realize inclusive, equitable, and sustainable education in producing superior generations.
