The Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus also established a strategic partnership with UI and PT IKB to develop Edubot, the first collaborative educational AI.

Bandung, May 15, 2025 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Cibiru Campus signed a strategic Cooperation Agreement (PKS) with the Institute of Applied Sciences (LST) of the Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences, University of Indonesia , and PT. Artificial Intelligence Installation (PT IKB) . One of the Study Programs involved in the collaboration is the Elementary School Teacher Education study program, both at the Bachelor's and Master's levels. The signing ceremony, which took place in the UPI Cibiru Campus Hall, marked the start of the collaboration in the development of Edubot , an artificial intelligence (AI) engine specifically for education.

The event, which took place warmly and filled with collaborative enthusiasm, was attended directly by the Director of UPI Cibiru Campus, Prof. Dr. Deni Darmawan, M.Kom., MCE , Head of LST FMIPA UI Eko Waludi, M.Si. , and Director of PT IKB Ismail, S.Kom . The three appeared in an official photo session while holding the PKS document, witnessed by the development team, staff, and institutional representatives.

In this collaboration, the three parties are committed to:

Building the Edubot engine as an AI virtual assistant for learning needs.

Conducting coding and super memory training for teachers, students, and college students.

Developing AI-based digital learning content for all levels of education (elementary to tertiary).

Develop a fair and collaborative profit sharing and IPR protection system.

The Director of UPI's Cibiru Campus, Prof. Deni Darmawan, stated that this collaboration represents a concrete step toward integrating cutting-edge technology into the education system. "Edubot will be a new milestone for Indonesian education in utilizing artificial intelligence as an interactive and adaptive learning and teaching tool," he said.

Edubot is designed not only as a passive AI, but also capable of providing AI-based training services, including deep learning platforms , super memory , and foreign language learning guided by AI and human instructors.

In addition, the parties agree that the IPR (Intellectual Property Rights) of this product will be jointly owned, and this collaboration will last for 5 years , and can be extended as agreed.

This collaboration is expected to strengthen the synergy between the world of higher education, the technology industry, and the real needs of the Indonesian education world, making Edubot a national flagship product in the field of educational technology.
