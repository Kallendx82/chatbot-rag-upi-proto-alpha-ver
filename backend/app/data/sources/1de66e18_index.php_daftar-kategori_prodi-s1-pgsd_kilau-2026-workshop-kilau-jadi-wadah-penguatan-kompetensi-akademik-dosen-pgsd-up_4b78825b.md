*KILAU 2026: KILAU Workshop Becomes a Forum for Strengthening the Academic Competence of UPI Cibiru PGSD Lecturers *

The Library of the University of Education of Indonesia, Cibiru Regional Campus, held an Advanced Information Literacy Class Workshop (KILAU) on Monday, April 6, 2026, in a campus classroom attended by 17 lecturers , especially from the PGSD UPI Cibiru Campus environment. This activity is a strategic step for the campus in strengthening lecturers' competencies in the fields of information literacy, scientific publications, and the use of academic technology to improve the quality of learning, research, and the output of the tridharma of higher education.

The workshop presented practical materials on utilizing Scopus for literature searches, scientific publication strategies, and optimizing Turnitin Feedback Studio . The impact of the activity was evident in the increased ability of lecturers to access, evaluate, and utilize scientific sources critically and responsibly, thus supporting more innovative learning quality and improved publication productivity.

In line with the "impact" program , this activity encourages the development of learning innovations and scientific work that provide tangible benefits to students, the campus, and the community. The workshop also supports SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 9 (Innovation and Infrastructure) by strengthening lecturer competencies and utilizing digital platforms in a modern academic culture.

As a strategic recommendation, KILAU needs to be implemented regularly with continuing themes such as reputable journal publications, AI for research, and reference management to further its impact. The series of interactive activities, including presentations, practical exercises, discussions, and questions and answers, further emphasizes the library's role as an innovative and adaptive learning resource center.
