Vision

To become a study program that has excellence and pioneering expertise in the field of Computer Engineering in Indonesia by 2030.

Mission

Providing educational and teaching services in the field of Computer Engineering expertise to produce graduates with high academic competence, creative, innovative and professional abilities, and an entrepreneurial spirit.

Implementing research programs to support high-quality learning processes and contributing to the development of science, applications, and products based on information and communication technology, as well as publishing research results.

Utilizing knowledge and products in the field of Computer Engineering expertise in carrying out community service based on research results that can contribute to advancing society in increasing knowledge and utilization of software and hardware from modern computing systems, robotics, game technology, computer-controlled equipment, and smart device networks.

Developing, implementing, and disseminating technology in the field of Computer Engineering based on community needs and building networking with the business and industrial world (DUDI).

What is Computer Engineering?

Computer engineering is a discipline encompassing computer science, computer design, construction technology, implementation, and maintenance of modern computer hardware and software, as well as computer-controlled equipment. In the past, computer engineering was often referred to as a combination of computer science and electrical engineering. Today, computer engineering has developed into a distinct discipline, though it is related to both electrical engineering and computer science. Computer engineering draws on computing, mathematics, and engineering.

EXPERTISE GROUP
Intelligence Device Development

Studying the development of Device Design and Construction including Hardware & Software that applies the principles of Automation, Control & Non-configuration and is implemented in fields such as industry, biomedicine, agriculture, electronics, mechatronics, & robotics.

Embedded System Development

Mempelajari Arsitektur & Organisasi Komputer, melakukan implementasi Embedded System & pengembangan prototype teknologi High Performance Computing, serta mengkaji teknologi Real Time System, Computer Graphic Image Processing, AR & VR.

Network & Security Development

Mempelajari struktur & penerapan Jaringan Komputer, analisis Jaringan Komputer, jenis Protokol & Interface Jaringan. Mengimplementasikan Keamanan Jaringan serta mengetahui konsep Vulnerability, Serangan Siber, Kriptografi, & Etichal Hacking.
