UPI Cibiru Campus Establishes International Collaboration with Chirchik State Pedagogical University, Uzbekistan, Hosting a Foreign Guest Lecturer

Bandung, February 27, 2025 – Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Cibiru Campus, through the Computer Engineering Study Program, has established an international collaboration with Chirchik State Pedagogical University, Uzbekistan. This collaboration is implemented through the presence of a foreign guest lecturer, Prof. Dr. Usarov Jabbor Eshbekovich, DSc, who delivered a lecture on Design Thinking

n his lecture, Prof. Dr. Usarov Jabbor Eshbekovich shared insights into the concepts and applications of Design Thinking in the fields of technology and innovation. His presence provided students of the Computer Engineering Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus with new perspectives on how creative approaches can be applied to the development of innovative technological solutions.

As part of this collaboration, Prof. Usarov was accompanied by Muhammad Taufik Dwi Putra, S.Kom., M.T., who served as the co-lecturer. This partnership aims to enrich students' learning experiences and broaden their understanding of international academic and industry standards.

This international collaboration marks a strategic step in expanding UPI’s academic network with foreign universities, while also opening up broader opportunities for students and faculty members to gain educational experiences aligned with global standards. The Computer Engineering Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus continues to strive for educational excellence through innovative and impactful international collaborations.

Through this lecture, students are expected not only to grasp the theoretical aspects of Design Thinking but also to apply these concepts in developing technology-based products and solutions. Moving forward, the collaboration between UPI Cibiru Campus and Chirchik State Pedagogical University is expected to grow further through various academic programs and joint research initiatives.
