The Master of Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru Holds Phase I Thesis Defense Examination

Bandung, August 13–14, 2025 — The Master of Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru held the Phase I Exam for students in the Smart Class Room and Cawalaloka Room, Building A, 2nd Floor. This activity lasted for two days, from 08.00 to 13.00 WIB, involving a number of examiners who had been officially assigned by the Director of the UPI Campus in Cibiru, Prof. Dr. Deni Darmawan, S.Pd., M.Si., M.Kom., MCE.

Eleven students presented their research findings and thesis plans to a panel of examiners. The topics covered varied, ranging from strategies for implementing the Independent Curriculum, developing technology-based teaching materials, digital leadership for school principals, and STEM-SDG-based learning.

This activity serves as a crucial step for Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Master's students to gain constructive feedback before moving on to the thesis development and refinement stage. With a serious yet supportive academic atmosphere, this exam is expected to improve the quality of student research and make a tangible contribution to the development of elementary education in Indonesia.
