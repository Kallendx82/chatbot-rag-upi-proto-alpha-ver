UPI Cibiru Campus Receives Visit from DelegationHiroshimaUniversity

Friday, January 24, 2025, the Indonesian Education University (UPI) Cibiru Campus received an official visit fromHiroshimaUniversity, Japan. The visit aimed to discuss exploring a collaboration agreement between the Graduate School for International Development and Cooperation (IDEC) and the University of Japan.HiroshimaUniversity with UPI Cibiru Campus. Delegates fromHiroshimaThe University is led by Takamichi Asakura, PhD, and accompanied by Nissa Aulia Belistiana Utami, M.Pd., who was warmly welcomed by the Director of the UPI Cibiru Campus, Prof. Dr. Deni Darmawan, S.Pd., M.Si., M.Kom., MCE.

In his remarks, Prof. Dr. Deni Darmawan expressed his gratitude for the visit, which served as a significant opportunity to strengthen international relations in education. He emphasized the importance of cross-border collaboration in improving academic quality, research, and community service. "We hope this collaboration will provide tangible benefits for both institutions, particularly in developing the capacity of students and lecturers," he said.HiroshimaThe University also expressed its appreciation for the warm welcome and enthusiasm shown by UPI Cibiru Campus.

This visit marks the first step in building stronger synergies in education, research, and cultural exchange programs. The purpose of this collaboration is to create opportunities for academic collaboration that will bring long-term benefits to both universities. Looking ahead, IDEC hopes toHiroshimaUniversity and UPI Cibiru Campus can be strategic partners in supporting the development of superior and globally competitive human resources. Ibud/24/1/2025.
