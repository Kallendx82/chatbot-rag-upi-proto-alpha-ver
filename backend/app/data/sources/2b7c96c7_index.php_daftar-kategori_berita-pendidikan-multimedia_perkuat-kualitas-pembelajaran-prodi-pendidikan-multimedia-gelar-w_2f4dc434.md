To Strengthen Learning Quality, the Multimedia Education Study Program Holds a Workshop on Preparing OBE-Based Lesson Plans

CIBIRU (17/09/2025) – In an effort to improve the quality standards of education and the relevance of graduates to the needs of the world of work, the Multimedia Education Study Program, Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Campus in Cibiru, held a Workshop on Preparing Semester Learning Plans (RPS) Based on Outcome-Based Education (OBE) .

This activity took place on Tuesday, September 16, 2025 , in the Multimedia Education Study Program Room. This strategic event was attended by all lecturers in the Multimedia Education Study Program.

Transformation Towards Results-Oriented Education

The implementation of an OBE-based curriculum is now a mandatory standard in higher education institutions to ensure superior accreditation and international recognition. The workshop's primary focus was on aligning teaching materials, learning methods, and assessment instruments with established Graduate Learning Outcomes (CPL).

In this activity, lecturers conducted a curriculum review and a comprehensive revision of the RPS document. Intensive discussions were held to ensure that each course focused not only on delivering material ( content-based ), but also on the skills students would master after completing the course ( outcome-based ).

Post-Workshop Expectations

The outcome of this one-day workshop was a standardized RPS document ready for implementation in the current semester. With this updated learning tool, it is hoped that the learning process in the Multimedia Education Study Program will be more structured, measurable, and adaptive to developments in educational technology and the creative industry.

The activity concluded with a session to standardize assignment formats and assessment rubrics, in order to provide transparency and objectivity in evaluating student learning outcomes in the future.
