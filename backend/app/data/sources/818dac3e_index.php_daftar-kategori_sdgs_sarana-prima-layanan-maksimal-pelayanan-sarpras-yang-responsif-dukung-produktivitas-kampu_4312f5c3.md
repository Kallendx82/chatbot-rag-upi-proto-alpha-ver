Prime Facilities, Maximum Service: Responsive Infrastructure Supports UPI Cibiru Campus Productivity

UPI Cibiru Campus continues to strengthen its service quality through responsive facilities and infrastructure (sarpras) to support the smooth running of academic and institutional activities. These services are centralized in the facilities and infrastructure room located in the PGPAUD hallway, behind the Blue Building, making it easily accessible to lecturers, educational staff, and students who need campus support. The presence of these organized services is an essential part of the campus's commitment to creating a comfortable and productive learning environment.

Furthermore, the strategic infrastructure space strengthens inter-unit coordination in campus facility management. This results in faster service delivery, more targeted needs management, and the maintenance of the quality of supporting learning facilities.

The series of services takes place every working day through receiving facility requests, verifying needs, distributing facilities, monitoring usage, and following up on maintenance.

This activity also relates to SDG 4 (Quality Education) through the provision of adequate learning facilities, and SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure) through the strengthening of efficient and sustainable campus infrastructure governance. Thus, infrastructure services are a crucial part of developing a modern and adaptive campus.
