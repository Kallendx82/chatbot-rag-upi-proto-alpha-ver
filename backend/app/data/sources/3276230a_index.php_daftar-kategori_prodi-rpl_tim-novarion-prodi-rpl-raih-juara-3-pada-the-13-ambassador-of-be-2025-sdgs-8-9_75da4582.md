The NOVARION RPL Study Program Team Won 3rd Place at The 13 Ambassador of BE 2025 (SDGs 8 & 9)

The Software Engineering (RPL) Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus has once again achieved national recognition. The NOVARION
team won third place in the Best Creative and Innovative Business Model in SDGs category at the 13 Ambassadors of Business and Economics (BE) 2025 event .

This competition assesses participants' ability to design innovative, creative business solutions that have real implementation potential, particularly in supporting sustainable development.

This achievement contributes to the achievement of:

SDGs 8 – Decent Work and Economic Growth , by encouraging entrepreneurial ideas and sustainability-based business models.

SDGs 9 – Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure , through the application of technology and innovation in designing future business models.

Congratulations to the NOVARION Team on this prestigious achievement. May this success motivate them to continue their work and bring digital innovations that benefit society.
