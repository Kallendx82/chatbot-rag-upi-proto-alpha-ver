UPI Leadership Documentation: Momentum to Strengthen the Institution's Image and Togetherness

 

The photo documentation of campus leaders was held on the 3rd floor of Building A/Blue Building, in the Photography Laboratory, as part of strengthening the institution's visual identity and image. This activity is a strategic step for the institution to provide professional, representative leadership documentation that is ready to be used for various official campus publications.

This activity also strengthens togetherness and synergy among leaders as a symbol of organizational solidarity. The uniform and professional appearance of the leadership represents collaboration in implementing the university's vision, mission, and strategic programs. This demonstrates how UPI utilizes documentation as part of effective information governance.

This activity relates to SDG 4 (Quality Education) by strengthening education information governance, and SDG 16 (Resilient Institutions) by supporting transparency, professionalism, and strengthening organizational identity. Therefore, this documentation session is a tangible contribution to the development of strong and sustainable educational institutions.

As a strategic recommendation, leadership documentation should be conducted periodically to reflect the dynamics of the organizational structure and the university's branding needs. Standardizing visual concepts, backgrounds, and publication formats is also crucial to maintaining UPI's identity consistency across various platforms.
