"UPI Cibiru Campus Tutorial Cadre Development: Building Superior and Religious Mentors"

UPI Cibiru - The UPI Cibiru Campus Tutorial Cadre Development activity which took place on February 1-2, 2025 at Villa Saung Qolbu, Kp. Jati RW 6, Pasirbiru Village, Cibiru District, was a strategic step in forming superior cadres in the tutorial field. This event was opened by the Head of the UPI Cibiru Campus Tutorial Center, Mr. Dr. Asep Rudi Nurjaman, M.Pd.I., who emphasized the importance of the role of tutorials in improving the academic quality and character of students. A total of 85 participants consisting of prospective administrators and tutorial mentors were present, representing various study programs, namely PGSD, PGPAUD, RPL, Communication Technology (Tekkom), and Multicultural Community Education (PMM).

This activity is designed to equip participants with insights, skills, and leadership values ​​in the tutoring field. A variety of materials are presented, including effective teaching techniques, tutorial group management, and the internalization of Islamic values ​​in the learning process. Through this training, it is hoped that participants will become mentors who are not only competent in academic guidance but also act as agents of change, bringing positive values ​​to the campus environment.

As part of the vision to realize a religious UPI Cibiru Campus, this Tutorial Cadre Development program also emphasizes the importance of integrating science and spiritual values. With continuous development, it is hoped that the tutorial program can become a learning tool that not only focuses on academics, but also forms student character based on morals and ethics. Going forward, the tutorial cadres who have been developed in this activity are expected to actively contribute to creating a more conducive academic environment, oriented towards intellectual development and the cultivation of noble morals.
