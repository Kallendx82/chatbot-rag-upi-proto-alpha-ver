UPI Cibiru PGPAUD Lecturers Involved in Student RPL Activities in Collaboration with the Adiluhung Nusantara Foundation

Cibiru, July 15, 2025 — Three lecturers from the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) in Cibiru participated in the coordination and assessment of Prior Learning Recognition (RPL) for PGPAUD students in collaboration with the Adiluhung Nusantara Foundation. This activity was held for two days, on July 14 and 15, 2025, as part of the process of recognizing learning achievements obtained by students through non-formal channels and previous work experience. The lecturers were responsible for coordinating and assessing student portfolio documents in accordance with established standards and procedures. This activity is a form of real support from the PGPAUD Study Program at UPI Cibiru in expanding access to higher education through the RPL scheme and strengthening strategic partnerships with institutions outside the campus. It is hoped that the implementation of this RPL can provide real benefits for students in continuing their studies in a more structured and efficient manner.
