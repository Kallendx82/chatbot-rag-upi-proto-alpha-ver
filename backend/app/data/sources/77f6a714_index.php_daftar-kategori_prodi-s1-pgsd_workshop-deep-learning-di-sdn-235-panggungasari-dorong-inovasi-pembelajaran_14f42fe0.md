Deep Learning Workshop at SDN 235 Panggungasari Promotes Learning Innovation

In order to improve the quality of learning in elementary schools, Dr. Yona Wahyuningsih, M.Pd., conducted a deep learning workshop at SDN 235 Panggungasari, Bandung City on Thursday, August 14, 2025.

This workshop aims to introduce and develop in-depth learning strategies that emphasize holistic, critical, and applicable understanding. This method is expected to enable students to master the material more comprehensively and relate it to everyday life.

The activity, attended by teachers from SDN 235 Panggungasari, was enthusiastic. Participants gained not only a theoretical understanding of the concept of deep learning but also hands-on practice in implementing the strategy in the classroom.

Dr. Yona Wahyuningsih stated that in-depth learning is crucial for preparing a generation that is adaptive, creative, and able to think critically to face the challenges of the times. "With deep learning , students don't just memorize, but are able to understand, analyze, and apply the knowledge they possess," she said.

Through this workshop, it is hoped that teachers can continue to innovate in teaching methods so as to create meaningful learning and have a positive impact on student development.
