Congratulations and Success! Ms. Desiani Natalina Muliasari Earns a Ph.D.

Cibiru, April 18, 2025 — The extended family of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) UPI Campus in Cibiru conveys its congratulations and highest appreciation to Mrs. Desiani Natalina Muliasari, M.Pd., Ph.D. for her success in obtaining her doctoral degree (Ph.D.).

This achievement is a testament to her dedication, perseverance, and strong enthusiasm for learning, both in developing herself and contributing to the world of education, particularly in the field of early childhood education. Ms. Desiani's doctoral degree is expected to inspire the entire academic community of PGPAUD UPI Cibiru, while also strengthening the quality of teaching resources in carrying out the Tri Dharma of Higher Education.

May the knowledge gained bring blessings and benefits to himself, his family, his community, and the institution where he serves. Great hopes are also placed on his continued contribution to the advancement of early childhood education in Indonesia.

We're proud of you! Your dedication and persistence have paid off. Congratulations on your Ph.D., and best wishes for your continued success. 🎓✨
