The UPI Cibiru TANAMI RPL Team Qualifies for the 13th SETIA KEI Final (SDGs 9)

The Software Engineering (RPL) Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus has once again achieved a brilliant achievement. The TANAMI RPL
team advanced to the final round of the 13th KEI Sharia Economics Triumph Activities (SETIA) , a national competition focusing on ideas and innovations in sharia economics and its supporting technologies.

This achievement demonstrates the quality of RPL students, who are not only competent in the technical aspects of software development but also capable of delivering relevant and valuable technological solutions to society.
This achievement aligns with Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 9—Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure —which encourages the strengthening of innovation and technological development as the foundation for sustainable development.

Hopefully, this success will motivate all RPL students to continue working, innovating, and making positive contributions to the development of technology that benefits the wider community.

Congratulations to the RPL TANAMI Team, and good luck on your journey to the finals! 🏆
