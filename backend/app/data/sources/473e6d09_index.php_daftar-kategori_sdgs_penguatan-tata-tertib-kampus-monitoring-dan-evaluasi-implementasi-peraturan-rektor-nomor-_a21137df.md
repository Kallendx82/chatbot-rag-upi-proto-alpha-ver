Strengthening Campus Regulations: Monitoring and Evaluation of the Implementation of Rector's Regulation Number 60 of 2025 concerning Student Discipline

Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia Cibiru Campus continues to strengthen its commitment to creating an orderly, safe, and character-based academic environment through the Monitoring and Evaluation of the Implementation of Rector's Regulation Number 60 of 2025 concerning Student Discipline activities held in the Hall of Building E, 1st Floor. This activity is an effort by the campus to build a culture of discipline and increase student awareness of the importance of ethics and order in the university environment.

The event featured special staff from the Campus Directorate of the Indonesian University of Education in Cibiru as resource persons and policy evaluation facilitators. Participants gained an understanding of the implementation of student disciplinary regulations, oversight mechanisms, and strategies for creating a conducive and professional academic climate.

This activity demonstrates UPI Cibiru Campus' active role in supporting the development of student character through discipline, responsibility, and integrity. This program also aligns with the campus's commitment to impact through character building and quality higher education governance.

From the perspective of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), this activity supports SDGs 4 on Quality Education and SDGs 16 on Peace, Justice, and Resilient Institutions by strengthening a culture of discipline and an inclusive and harmonious educational environment.
