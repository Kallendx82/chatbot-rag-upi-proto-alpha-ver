UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Program Holds Outstanding Student Selection Socialization

The Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education, Cibiru Campus, held a Socialization event for the Selection of Outstanding Students (Pilmapres) as part of its efforts to foster and develop student potential. This activity aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of the selection mechanism, general requirements, and assessment components in the Pilmapres event, especially regarding outstanding student achievements.
Through this socialization, PGSD students were provided with information about the importance of a track record of academic and non-academic achievements, including involvement in intracurricular, co-curricular, and extracurricular activities. In addition, students were also directed to document various outstanding achievements as a form of recognition of competence, leadership, and real contributions during their education at university.

This socialization activity for the Outstanding Student Selection is in line with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) point 4, namely Quality Education, which emphasizes the importance of developing superior, competitive, and character-based human resources. The development of outstanding students also supports the creation of an academic environment that encourages a culture of achievement, innovation, and lifelong learning. In addition, this activity also contributes to SDGs point 16, by strengthening the values ​​of integrity, responsibility, and fairness in a transparent and accountable selection process.

Through this activity, the UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program affirms its commitment to supporting students to develop their best potential and prepare a future generation of educators who are not only academically superior, but also able to actively contribute to sustainable educational development.
