HISTORY OF UPI CAMPUS IN CIBIRU

The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) is a multi-campus university because it has campuses in addition to the main campus (Bumi Siliwangi Campus), also has campuses in five regions: Cibiru, Bandung Regency, Tasikmalaya City, Sumedang Regency, Purwakarta Regency, and Serang Regency, Banten Province. These campuses officially became part of UPI in 1990/1991, originally from the State Teacher Training School (SPG). The campuses in these regions became an integral part of UPI in the framework of UPI's future development.

The multi-campus concept adopted by UPI refers to Government Regulation Number 15 of 2014 concerning the UPI Statutes, which was later clarified through UPI MWA Regulation Number 3 of 2015 concerning the Implementation of the UPI Statutes. According to the regulation, UPI organizes Tridharma activities and other activities in an integrated, harmonious, and sustainable manner both inside and outside the UPI domicile. Outside the domicile is interpreted as organizing Tridharma activities and other activities at the main campus of Bumi Siliwangi and UPI Campuses in the regions, including the UPI Campus in Cibiru, Bandung Regency.

The UPI campus in Cibiru is located on Jalan Raya Cibiru KM 15 Bandung 40393, geographically within the administrative area of ​​Cibiru Wetan Village, Cileunyi District, Bandung Regency, West Java Province. This campus was born on January 15,   1990,   at which   time   there   was   a   handover of the implementation of elementary school teacher education by SPG/SGO from the Director General of Elementary and Secondary Education to the Director General of Higher Education, Ministry of Education and Culture. In line with the closure of SPG/SGO, all assets belonging to SPG/SGO, in this case SPG Negeri I Bandung were handed over to IKIP Bandung (now changed to UPI) to be continued as a Primary School Teacher Education (PGSD) study program at diploma II level (DII).

After 11 years (1990-2001) of organizing PGSD D-II, on January 1, 2001, the UPI Campus in Cibiru began organizing undergraduate education for the PGSD Study Program. The PGSD S1 program began by accepting prospective students with a DII PGSD background. The implementation of the S1 program was limited to students who came from elementary school teachers who had become civil servants or had worked so that it had not accepted students who came from high school graduates or equivalent. Until 2005, the PGSD Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru accepted students for the PGSD S1 program from high school graduates and equivalent. And, in 2006 opened the Dual Modes PGSD S1 Program. The Dual Modes PGSD S1 Program developed a learning concept by integrating face-to-face learning with independent learning using modules.

In addition to providing PGSD, the UPI Campus in Cibiru also offers a Kindergarten Teacher Education Study Program (PGTK) at the DII level. This program was officially implemented in the 2004/2005 academic year. In line with the demands of needs and various government policies in the field of education, the PGTK Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru was developed into a PGTK undergraduate education program. The PGTK S1 program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru began to be realized in the 2009/2010 academic year. The PGTK Study Program has now changed to the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PG PAUD).

Along with the rapid and widespread development of science and technology, the UPI Campus in Cibiru began developing other disciplines based on the development of information technology. In 2018, the UPI Campus in Cibiru opened a Bachelor's degree in Multimedia Education. In 2019, it opened a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering (non-educational). In 2020, it opened a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering (non-educational). In addition to providing undergraduate programs, starting in late 2018, the UPI Campus in Cibiru was assigned to organize the Teacher Professional Education Program (PPG) for Elementary School teachers (PPG Daljab). In 2021, it opened a Master's degree program (S2) in Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD). The status of the study programs at the UPI Campus in Cibiru has been recognized and accredited by BAN-PT, and two study programs have received superior qualifications (A), namely the PGSD and PGPAUD Study Programs since 2018.
