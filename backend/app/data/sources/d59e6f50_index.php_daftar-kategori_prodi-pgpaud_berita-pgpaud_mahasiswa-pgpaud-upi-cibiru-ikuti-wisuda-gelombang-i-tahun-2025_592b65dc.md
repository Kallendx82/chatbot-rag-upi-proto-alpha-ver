UPI Cibiru PGPAUD Students to Participate in Graduation Wave I in 2025

Cibiru, February 19, 2025 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) successfully held its first graduation ceremony for the 2025 class on Wednesday, February 19, 2025, at the UPI Gymnasium on the Bumi Siliwangi Campus in Bandung. Hundreds of graduates from various faculties and regional campuses participated in the event, including those from the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) program at the UPI Cibiru campus.

Several PGPAUD students participated in the solemn and joyful graduation ceremony. This moment marked the completion of their academic journey and the beginning of their new roles as professional educators in society.

The UPI Cibiru Early Childhood Education Program (PGPAUD) would like to congratulate all students who have completed their studies and participated in the graduation ceremony. We hope the knowledge and experience gained during their studies will be invaluable in achieving quality early childhood education.

Congratulations to the graduates of UPI Cibiru's PGPAUD. Continue to create and inspire future generations of educators!
