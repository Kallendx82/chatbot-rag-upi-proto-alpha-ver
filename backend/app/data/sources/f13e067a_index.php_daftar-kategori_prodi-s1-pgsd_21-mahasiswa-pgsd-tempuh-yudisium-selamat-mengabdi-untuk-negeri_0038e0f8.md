21 Elementary School Teacher Education Students Graduate. Congratulations on Serving the Nation!

Cibiru, April 27, 2025 – Twenty-one undergraduate students of the Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus have officially graduated during a graduation ceremony held on Monday, April 27, 2025, in the Smartclass room of the PGSD Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus. The event began at 4:00 PM WIB and continued until completion.

The event was attended by the Head of the Undergraduate Primary School Teacher Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus, Dr. Tita Mulyati, M.Pd., who delivered a speech and led the graduation ceremony. Also present was the Secretary of the Primary School Teacher Education Study Program, Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., who also congratulated the students on successfully completing their studies.

In her remarks, Dr. Tita Mulyati expressed her appreciation for the students' achievements and expressed her hope that they would apply their knowledge to contribute to the world of education. "Hopefully, this graduation will be the beginning of your long journey in education, to produce a better generation for the future," said Dr. Tita.

With the completion of this activity, 21 PGSD S1 students from UPI Cibiru Campus have now officially entered the professional world and are ready to contribute to the world of education in Indonesia.
