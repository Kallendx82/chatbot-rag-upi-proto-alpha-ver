UPI Job Fair 2024: A Platform for Synergy Between Education and the World of Work

The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) once again held its annual Job Fair , this time in 2024. This event took place at the Achmad Sanusi Building, on October 10-11, 2024, and was attended by dozens of national and multinational companies from various sectors.

This event aims to bridge UPI students and alumni, including students of the Multimedia Education Study Program at the UPI Cibiru Campus, with career opportunities that match their competencies.

At the 2024 UPI Job Fair, the Multimedia Education Study Program also made a unique contribution by presenting digital student portfolios showcasing their innovative work. This became one of the attractions for the technology companies in attendance.

In addition to the job fair, this event also features career seminars, personal branding workshops , and CV consultations, all designed to strengthen students' and alumni's readiness to face job selection.

The 2024 UPI Job Fair demonstrates the university's commitment to making the campus a hub for collaboration between education and the workplace.
