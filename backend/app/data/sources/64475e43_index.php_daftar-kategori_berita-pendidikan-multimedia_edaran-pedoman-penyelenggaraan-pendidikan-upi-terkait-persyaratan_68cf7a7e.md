Circular on UPI Education Implementation Guidelines Regarding TOEFL Requirements

Bandung, October 16, 2024 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) has issued an official circular regarding educational implementation guidelines , which stipulates TOEFL requirements as an academic requirement for students. This circular aims to improve students' English language skills and prepare them for global competition.

Students are expected to immediately prepare to meet these requirements by utilizing the various facilities provided by UPI. Further information can be accessed through the official UPI Language Center website or through the academic departments of each faculty.
