UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Students Participate in Diploy Activities

On Wednesday, October 22, 2025, a Diploy Socialization activity was held by the Center for Human Resources Development and Communication and Informatics Research (BPSDMP Kominfo) which was attended by Mrs. Nur Azizah, S.Si., M.Si. as the Head of BPSDMP Kominfo Bandung and Muhammad Fauzan, S.Kom. along with the team which took place at the Kimaya Braga Hotel, Bandung. In this activity, five students from the UPI Campus in Cibiru were selected as participants who were accompanied by the SI Lecturer of Elementary School Teacher Education at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, Mrs. Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd. MH. The students are:

  Andreas Parlin Roberto Sihombing (S1 Computer Engineering)
  Faza Aghniya Husnan R (Bachelor of Elementary School Teacher Education)
  Siti Fatimah (Bachelor of Elementary School Teacher Education)
  Siti Hawa (Bachelor of Elementary School Teacher Education)
  Zona Zahrana (Bachelor's Degree in Elementary School Teacher Education)

This Diploy Socialization activity aims to improve students' understanding and skills in the field of communication and informatics, as well as encourage their active participation in the development of quality human resources. This socialization aligns with the fourth goal of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), namely " Quality Education." Through this activity, it is hoped that students will gain relevant knowledge and skills to face challenges in the world of work and contribute to community development.

The event started at 09.00 WIB and ended at 16.00 WIB. During the activity, participants were given material regarding the use of the DIploy.id platform, Virtual Internship socialization, interview procedures and CV preparation.
