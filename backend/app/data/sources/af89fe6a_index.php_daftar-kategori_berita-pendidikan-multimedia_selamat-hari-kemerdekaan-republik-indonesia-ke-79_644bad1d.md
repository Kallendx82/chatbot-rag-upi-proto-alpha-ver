Happy 79th Independence Day of the Republic of Indonesia

Cibiru, August 17, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru wishes you a Happy 79th Independence Day of the Republic of Indonesia ! On August 17, 2024, we celebrate a historic day that marks the nation's struggle for independence.

Independence Day is a crucial moment for all Indonesians to reflect on the values ​​of struggle, unity, and oneness forged by our heroes. In the spirit of independence, let us strengthen our love for our country and our commitment to contributing to national development, particularly through education and technology.

As a form of celebration, the Multimedia Education Study Program invites all students and academics to participate in various activities that foster a spirit of nationalism, such as competitions, seminars, and social services.

Happy Independence Day! May the spirit of independence always burn within us and motivate us to continue contributing to the nation's progress.
