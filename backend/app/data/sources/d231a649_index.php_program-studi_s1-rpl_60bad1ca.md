Vision

The vision of the Software Engineering Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus is to become a pioneering and superior study program in the development of software engineering science by 2029.

Mission

The mission of the Software Engineering Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus is an elaboration of the implementation of educational and teaching activities, research, and community service carried out in an effort to realize UPI's vision. The mission of the Software Engineering Study Program is as follows.

Providing educational and teaching services in the field of software engineering expertise to produce graduates with high academic competence, creative and innovative abilities, and professionalism, as well as an entrepreneurial spirit;

Implementing research programs to support high-quality learning processes and contributing to the development of scientific knowledge, applications and software engineering-based products, as well as publishing research results;

Utilizing software engineering knowledge and products in carrying out community service based on research results that can contribute to advancing society in increasing knowledge and utilization of information technology; and

Developing, implementing, and disseminating technology in the field of software engineering based on the needs of society, the business world, and industry.

What is Software Engineering?

The Software Engineering Undergraduate Program is one of the study programs at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, located on Jl. Raya Cibiru KM 15, Bandung Regency. In the Software Engineering Study Program, students will study fields related to Software Engineering (software development, software management, engineering processes, engineering tools and methodologies), Data Engineering (data modeling, knowledge-based data management, data management implementation), Multimedia & Game Programming (Game Development, 2D & 3D animation), and Ubiquitous Computing (Internet of Things, Augmented Reality, Cloud Computing).

EXPERTISE GROUP
Data Engineering and Business Intl.

Studying data processing techniques, the process of searching for patterns and relationships from various perspectives in a dataset by utilizing computational intelligence methods whose results can be used to support decision making.

Multimedia and Games

Mempelajari pemahaman tentang prinsip, konsep dan teknik animasi baik 2D maupun 3D, selain itu mempelajari cara pembuatan game, proses pengembangan game, theory of fun dan nilai edukasi dalam game.

Ubiquitous Computing

Mempelajari konsep ubiquitous computing dimana sistem atau aplikasi dapat dijalankan bukan hanya pada komputer namun pada beragam alat yang digunakan sehari-hari seperti smartphone, jam tangan, dan peralatan elektronik lainya yang bersistem cerdas.
