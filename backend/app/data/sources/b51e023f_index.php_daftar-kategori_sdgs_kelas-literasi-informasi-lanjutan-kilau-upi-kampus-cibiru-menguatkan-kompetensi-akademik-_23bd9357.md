KILAU UPI Cibiru Campus Advanced Information Literacy Class: Strengthening Academic Competence in the Digital Era

UPI Cibiru Campus held an Advanced Information Literacy Class (KILAU) on the 3rd, 4th, and E floors of the New Building , attended by lecturers and dozens of students . This activity is a strategic step for the campus to strengthen the competence of the academic community in the areas of scientific source search, digital database utilization, citation management, and technology-based academic information management. Through this program, the campus emphasizes its role in improving the quality of learning, research, and scientific publications in the digital era.

The event featured speakers from the Indonesian University of Education , along with Dr. Muh. Asriadi AM, S.Pd., M.Pd. , a lecturer from the Cibiru Campus , who provided material reinforcement and hands-on practice. The event's impact was evident in the increased capacity of lecturers and students to manage information critically, effectively, and ethically, thus supporting more innovative learning quality and improved research productivity.

This activity also supports SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 9 (Innovation and Infrastructure) by strengthening academic competencies and utilizing digital technology in scientific information management. The interactive series of events featured presentations, practical exercises, discussions, and a question-and-answer session, further strengthening the academic culture at UPI Cibiru Campus.
