Our Activity —

Exciting Activities for You

Virtual Opening Ceremony

The Virtual Opening Ceremony marks the commencement of the event, setting the tone and officially inaugurating the proceedings. It often includes speeches, performances, and ceremonial elements.

Virtual General Lecture

The Virtual General Lecture involves a keynote presentation or address delivered by a distinguished speaker on a significant topic relevant to the event's theme or purpose.

FGD and Research Collaboration

Focused Group Discussions (FGDs) provide a platform for in-depth conversations among participants on specific topics, fostering collaboration and the exchange of ideas. Research collaboration involves joint efforts among participants to explore and address research-related challenges or opportunities.

Dissemination & Virtual Closing Ceremony

The Dissemination session involves sharing outcomes, insights, or findings resulting from discussions or research conducted during the event. The Virtual Closing Ceremony concludes the event, summarizing key takeaways, acknowledging contributions, and bidding farewell.
