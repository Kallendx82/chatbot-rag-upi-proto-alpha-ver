Appreciation for PGSD Student Achievements: Qualifying as a Finalist in the Teacher's Cakra Competition

UPI Cibiru Campus has once again produced outstanding future educators through the achievements of its Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) students. Four students have successfully qualified as finalists in the Cakra Guru Competition, a competition that tests prospective teachers' innovation and competence in designing creative and impactful learning.

This success was supported by intensive mentoring, strengthening of pedagogical competencies, and hands-on, hands-on coaching. Throughout the process, students were mentored by their supervisor, Ranu Sudarmansyah, who provided strategic guidance in developing innovative learning ideas.

The finalists underwent a rigorous selection process, from administration and proposal assessment to presentations before a panel of judges. This participation is part of the implementation of an "impactful" learning program that encourages the creation of tangible work in the field of education.

This achievement is also in line with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) point 4, namely Quality Education, through the development of inclusive, creative, and adaptive learning methods at the elementary school level.
