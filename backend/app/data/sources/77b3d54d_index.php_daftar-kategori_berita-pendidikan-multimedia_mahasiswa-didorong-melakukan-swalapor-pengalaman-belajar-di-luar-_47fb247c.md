Students are encouraged to self-report their off-campus learning experiences.

Cibiru, September 30, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru encourages all students to actively self-report their off-campus learning experiences through the official website kemahasiswaan.upi.edu . This appeal aims to document learning activities conducted off-campus, such as internships, student exchange programs, competitions, and MBKM (Merdeka Belajar Kampus Merdeka) activities.

Through self-reporting, students can record various achievements, both academic and non-academic. This data will become an important part of the student's track record and demonstrate the Multimedia Education Study Program's active support for the holistic development of student competencies.

By self-reporting, students also contribute to strengthening UPI's institutional achievement data, which can then be used in various national-level evaluations and reporting. The study program hopes that all students will make optimal use of this feature to document every valuable experience they have.
