Opening a New Era of Parking Management: YOLO and CNN-Based Intelligent Systems
Parking management issues in modern cities often pose unique challenges, ranging from congestion at entrances to difficulty finding available parking spaces. To address these challenges, artificial intelligence (AI) technology, particularly  deep learning methods , offers innovative solutions. One cutting-edge approach under intensive development is a license plate detection-based automated parking system using a combination of the  You Only Look Once (YOLO) algorithm  and  a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) .
This intelligent system transforms the conventional parking process that relies on manual input or physical tickets into a seamless and efficient experience.
 
The Central Role of YOLO and CNN
The development of this system involved two main image processing stages:
License Plate Location Detection with YOLO:  YOLO is a  real-time object detection algorithm  known for its high speed and accuracy. In this context, YOLO is used to instantly scan images or  video feeds  from CCTV cameras and identify the exact location of vehicle license plates within the image frame. Its speed allows the system to process moving vehicles without significant delay.
Character Recognition with CNN:  After the license plate area is successfully detected and extracted by YOLO, the image is passed to a CNN model. CNN, the backbone of many image recognition applications, is responsible for recognizing and classifying each character (letters and numbers) on the license plate. This process is often integrated with  Optical Character Recognition  (OCR) technology, such as EasyOCR, to convert image pixels into computer-readable text data.
 
Integrated Working Mechanism
In summary, the workflow of this system runs automatically:
Image Acquisition:  Cameras installed at the parking area entrance/exit capture images of vehicles.
Object Detection:  The YOLO model processes images to find license plates.
Segmentation and Recognition:  The license plate area is isolated, and the CNN model reads the characters within it.
Verification and Management:  The generated license plate text data is then matched against an existing database. If valid, the parking barrier opens automatically, and parking space availability information is updated in  real time .
Benefits and Future Prospects
The implementation of this technology brings significant benefits:
Time Efficiency:  Reduce long queues at parking entrances and exits by eliminating manual interactions.
Enhanced Security:  Allows accurate monitoring of incoming and outgoing vehicles, facilitating tracing in the event of an incident.
Space Optimization: Real-time  data   on parking occupancy helps managers maximize parking capacity.
The development of this YOLO and CNN-based parking system represents a promising step forward in creating smarter and more efficient urban environments. Research is ongoing to improve accuracy, especially in challenging lighting conditions or inclement weather.
