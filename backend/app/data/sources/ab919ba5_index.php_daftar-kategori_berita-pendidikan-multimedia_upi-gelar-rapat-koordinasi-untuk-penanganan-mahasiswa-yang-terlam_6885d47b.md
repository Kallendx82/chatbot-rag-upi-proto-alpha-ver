UPI Holds Coordination Meeting to Handle Students Who Are Late in Completing Their Studies

Bandung, October 16, 2024 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) held a special coordination meeting to discuss strategies for handling students experiencing delays in completing their studies. The meeting was attended by university leaders, heads of study programs, academic advisors, and the Directorate of Academic Administration and Student Affairs team.

The main focus of this meeting includes identifying obstacles faced by students, evaluating the effectiveness of academic guidance, and designing intervention programs such as remedial classes , time management training, and psychological support.

This meeting is expected to produce more integrated and positive policies, so that the number of students who are late in completing their studies can be minimized.

Through this effort, UPI once again demonstrates its commitment to creating an academic environment that supports the success of every student.
