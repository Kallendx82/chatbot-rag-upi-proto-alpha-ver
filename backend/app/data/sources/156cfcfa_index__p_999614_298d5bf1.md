Adzkar Muhamad AR Goes Global : ASEAN Student Mobility Journey
February 14, 2025
Berita UPI Tasikmalaya, Desain Produk Industri

Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI), through the Industrial Product Design program, proudly delegate Adzkar Muhammad A R as a representative in the ASEAN Student Mobility 2025. This program will be held from February 11–25, 2025, in a hybrid,  combining on-site activities in Kuala Lumpur and online sessions.

This program provides opportunity to broaden students’ academic perspectives, understand the cultural diversity within the ASEAN region, and build international connections. Additionally, it serves as a platform for participants to engage in discussions, exchange ideas, and foster cross-border collaborations in various academic and social fields. By participating in this multicultural environment, students are expected to develop critical thinking, leadership skills, and adaptability in an increasingly interconnected world.

Participation in programs like ASEAN Student Mobility 2025 is a testament to UPI students’ readiness to compete and contribute on an international level. In addition to enriching their academic experience, this program also opens doors to building professional networks that can become valuable assets for the future. With a broader perspective and a deeper understanding of global dynamics, students can gain greater confidence in facing challenges in both professional and academic fields.

Therefore, we encourage all students to stay motivated in their personal development and take part in similar international programs. Do not hesitate to explore new opportunities, enhance your competencies, and bring UPI’s name to the global stage. Together, we can create change and make a positive impact on society! (Klaus Wonner Purba)
