Welcoming Graduates: “Stepping from the Blue: Saying Goodbye with Joy to Making History”

Blue Sadap (Welcome Graduates of PGSD UPI Cibiru Campus) is an activity carried out by the Advocacy Department and the PSDO Department of HIMA PGSD to welcome and appreciate the graduates of PGSD UPI Cibiru Campus who have graduated and strengthen the relationship between UPI Cibiru Campus students, HIMA PGSD and graduates who have graduated. This activity was held in one day on Wednesday, June 11, 2025, at UPI Bumi Siliwangi. The series of activities were filled with giving speeches, congratulations, and also giving gifts or graduation tokens by the committee to the graduates to appreciate the results of their hard work so far. This Blue Sadap activity is the right forum to provide a good final impression for graduates. With this Blue Sadap, it is hoped that the graduates will have memorable memories before leaving campus.

The event began with preparations for departure to the event location at UPI Bumi Siliwangi. After all committee members had gathered, a briefing and final committee check-up was conducted, led by the event division. Next, the entire committee traveled from UPI Cibiru Campus to UPI Bumi Siliwangi. Upon arrival at UPI Bumi Siliwangi, the committee gathered at the Ahmad Sanusi Building for breakfast and prepared to join the parade with the graduates. The entire committee moved in an organized manner from the Ahmad Sanusi Building to the UPI Bumi Siliwangi Gymnasium.

Upon arrival at the UPI Bumi Siliwangi Gymnasium, the committee welcomed the graduates. The graduates, accompanied by the committee and the LO, followed a procession to the UPI Bumi Siliwangi Bareti Park as a form of respect and celebration of their graduation. After arriving at Bareti Park, all PGSD UPI Cibiru Campus graduates took documentation together with other UPI Cibiru Campus graduates. After the documentation was completed, the PGSD UPI Cibiru Campus graduates were directed to gather at one point. Next, the event began informally with a warm welcome from the MC to welcome the participants and graduates who had attended, then remarks by the Chairperson of the Executive Committee, Chairperson of the HIMA PGSD Executive Board, and Chairperson of the HIMA PGSD Legislative Council.

After the speech, the event continued with representatives of the graduates delivering a foreword containing their impressions, messages, and gratitude for the support throughout their studies. The committee presented souvenirs to the graduates as a form of appreciation and memento of their achievements. Finally, the MC officially closed the series of activities, marking the end of the parade and celebration for the graduates. All graduates left the location and continued with their respective activities. Thus, the 2025 Blue Tap activity ran smoothly, orderly, and full of enthusiasm. It is hoped that this activity will provide memorable memories for the graduates before leaving campus.
