Dr. Agil Nanggala, M.Pd., Receives 27 Invitations as an Official Speaker in 2025

Dr. Agil Nanggala, M.Pd., a lecturer in Elementary School Teacher Education at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, has been invited to speak or be a resource person 27 times in 2025 at various official forums, generally related to leadership, science, youth, and motivation. Dr. Agil Nanggala's consistency in being willing and involved as a speaker or resource person is certainly closely related to the Tri Dharma of Higher Education, namely education, research, and community service.

This activity is seen by Dr. Agil Nanggala, M.Pd., as a strategic vehicle to increase literacy, change the paradigm of thinking and become a shared vehicle to train leadership attitudes, competencies and character that are in line with Pancasila values.

Her commitment to being an accessible, inclusive, free-of-charge, and, God willing, competent speaker or resource person stems from her past experience as an activist. She recalled once searching for and contacting a qualified speaker or resource person, but was hampered by fees beyond the student committee's ability to afford.

Substantively, the active participation of Dr. Agil Nanggala, M.Pd., as a speaker or resource person in 2025, certainly reflects the substance of the SDGs or Sustainable Development Goals, especially points: 4) Quality Education, 10) Reduced Inequalities, and 17) Partnerships for the Goals.

Hopefully, the commitment and consistency of Dr. Agil Nanggala, M.Pd., to be an inclusive speaker or resource person, without dowry and God willing competent, will contribute to improving the civic literacy of students and the community, elevate the good name of PGSD UPI Campus in Cibiru, and have a positive impact on the realization of Indonesia's golden generation in 2045.
