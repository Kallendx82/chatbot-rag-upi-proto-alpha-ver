Friday, January 9, 2026

The preparation activity for the Evaluation Worksheet (LKE) and the Integrity Zone (ZI) Work Plan for 2026 was carried out as an initial step in supporting the development of the Integrity Zone within the Indonesian University of Education. This activity aims to align perceptions, strengthen coordination, and prepare the necessary documents for the 2026 ZI development.

The event took place in the Hall of Building E, 1st Floor, and was attended by leaders, members of the Integrity Zone team, and the Head of Quality Assurance. The presence of leaders and quality assurance staff is crucial in ensuring that the LKE and ZI work plan preparation process is directed, systematic, and aligned with institutional policies.

Through this activity, it is hoped that all parties involved can understand their respective roles and responsibilities in the preparation of the LKE and the planning of the 2026 Integrity Zone work program, so that ZI development can be implemented optimally, sustainably, and support the realization of clean, transparent, and accountable governance.
