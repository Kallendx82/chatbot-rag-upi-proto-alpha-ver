UPI Cibiru RPL Students Win International Awards at the ASEAN Youth Conference Malaysia (SDGs 4 & 17)

The Software Engineering (RPL) Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus has once again achieved impressive international success.
Fatra Al Khawarizmi , an RPL student, was selected as:

Fully Funded Delegate ASEAN Youth Conference Malaysia , and

The Most Outstanding Scholar ASEAN Youth Organization Academy .

This international event brings together young people from various ASEAN countries to discuss, collaborate, and formulate ideas on strategic regional issues. Fatra's success is a testament to the quality of RPL students in competing globally, while also demonstrating strong diplomatic skills, leadership skills, and global literacy.

This achievement contributes to:

SDGs 4 – Quality Education:
through strengthening academic capacity, soft skills, and international learning experiences that improve the quality of student learning.

SDGs 17 – Partnerships for the Goals:
by building cross-country collaboration and ASEAN youth networks to support the sustainable development agenda.

Congratulations to Fatra Al Khawarizmi on this extraordinary achievement. May this international achievement inspire other RPL UPI Cibiru students to continue developing and bringing the institution's reputation to the global stage.
