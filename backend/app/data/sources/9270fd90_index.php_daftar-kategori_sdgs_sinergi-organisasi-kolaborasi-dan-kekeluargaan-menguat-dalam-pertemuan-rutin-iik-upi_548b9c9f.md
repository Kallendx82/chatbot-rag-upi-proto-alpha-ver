Organizational Synergy: Collaboration and Family Strengthened in UPI IIK Routine Meetings

To celebrate the joy of Eid al-Fitr 1447 H and strengthen the bonds of brotherhood, IIK UPI Cibiru Campus together with IIK Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia held a routine meeting and gathering in the BPPU Meeting Room of UPI Dormitory . This activity serves as a strategic forum to strengthen organizational collaboration, build togetherness, and foster a spirit of kinship among IIK members within the Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia environment.

The impact of the activity was further enhanced by the presence of a space for reflection and learning through a lecture entitled "The Secret of Forgiveness for Health from a Scientific Perspective" delivered by Prof. Dr. Mumu Komaro, ST, MT . This material provided an understanding that the value of forgiveness not only strengthens social relationships, but also impacts mental health, emotional well-being, and harmony in organizational life.

This activity also relates to SDG 3 (Healthy and Well-Being) by strengthening mental and emotional health, and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals) through collaboration and synergy between organizations. Thus, this activity is a real contribution to creating a harmonious and sustainable campus community.
