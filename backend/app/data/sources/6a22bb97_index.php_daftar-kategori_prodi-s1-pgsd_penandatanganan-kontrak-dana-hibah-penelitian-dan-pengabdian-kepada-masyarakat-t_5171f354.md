Signing of the 2025 Research and Community Service Grant Contract at the UPI Cibiru Campus

Cibiru, May 27, 2025 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Cibiru Campus held a Research and Community Service Grant Fund Contract Signing event on Tuesday, May 27, 2025, at 10:00 WIB in the Meeting Room, 1st Floor, Hall, Building E. This activity is part of an effort to strengthen academic culture and improve the quality of the tridharma of higher education, especially in the fields of research and community service.

The contract signing ceremony was attended by lecturers receiving internal grants from UPI's Cibiru Campus. According to data, this year, 20 research projects and 13 community service projects received grant funding from the university. Of these, the Elementary School Teacher Education Program (PGSD) was the largest contributor, with 10 lecturers receiving research and 7 receiving community service.

The event was also attended by the leadership of UPI Cibiru Campus, including the heads of study programs and the lecturers who received the grants. In their remarks, the campus leaders expressed their appreciation to all participating lecturers and emphasized the importance of continuing the research and community service programs as a tangible manifestation of UPI's contribution to the development of science and improving community welfare.

The event took place solemnly and full of collaborative spirit, closing with a group photo session as a symbol of shared commitment to carrying out the mandate of the tridharma of higher education in a professional and broad-based manner.
