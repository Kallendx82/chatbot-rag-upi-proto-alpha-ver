We are delighted to announce that we will be welcoming international students again this year! The Exploring Indonesian Culture Summer Course (EICSC) – BIPA program serves as an opportunity or provision for international students interested in:

Enrolling in PGSD UPI Campus in Purwakarta.
Beginning their journey in learning Indonesian.
Familiarizing themselves with Indonesian culture.

PGSD Study Program at the UPI Campus in Purwakarta has an Agreement and Collaboration Program with various Elementary Teacher Education study programs abroad, including those in Cambodia, Malaysia, and Bangladesh.

Through this collaboration, the Elementary Teacher Education study programs at partner universities can send their students to participate in the EICSC 2024. This program could also serve as a pathway to establishing international student exchange partnerships.
