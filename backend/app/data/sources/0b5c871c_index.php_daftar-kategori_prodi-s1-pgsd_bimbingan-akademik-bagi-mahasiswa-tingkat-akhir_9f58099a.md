Academic Guidance for Final Year Students

Cibiru, (30/7). In an effort to encourage students from the 2018 to 2020 intake who have not yet completed their final assignments, the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru held a meeting on Clarification of Academic Status and Further Study Plans led by Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., MH held a meeting aimed at encouraging and providing motivation and guidance to students so they can complete their theses before the end of this academic year.

Students are given the opportunity to discuss directly how they face challenges while completing their final assignments. One of the main challenges faced by students is not yet having a competency certificate. To address this, the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program offers a solution by participating in the IC3 (Internet and Computing Core Certification) training , which will be held this August. Furthermore, many students also face difficulties in taking the PTESOL test. As a solution, the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program encourages students to submit a letter of application directly to the language center.

With the meetings and solutions provided by the Study Program, it is hoped that students will be motivated and able to overcome the obstacles that occur so that they can continue their studies well.
