Welcome New Students! PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Congratulates the 2025 SNBT Participants Who Passed the Selection

Cibiru, May 28, 2025 — The extended family of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education Campus in Cibiru would like to congratulate and welcome the prospective new students who have passed the 2025 National Selection Based on Tests (SNBT) and have been officially accepted into the PGPAUD Study Program. This achievement is the result of hard work, perseverance, and a high fighting spirit. We are proud to welcome you as part of the extended family of PGPAUD UPI Cibiru, a study program committed to producing professional and inspiring educators in the field of early childhood education. The PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Study Program has been accredited with UNGGUL and is supported by experienced lecturers, a curriculum that is relevant to future needs, and various complete and innovative learning facilities. May this success be the beginning of a proud academic journey. Let's grow, learn, and contribute together in creating a future generation that is intelligent, characterful, and competitive. Welcome to PGPAUD UPI Cibiru — a learning home for prospective early childhood educators who are strong and dedicated.
