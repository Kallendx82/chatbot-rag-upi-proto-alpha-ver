The UPI Cibiru campus now appears greener and more beautiful thanks to the planting and landscaping of the front garden. Janitor Yusuf Nurdiansyah and his colleagues have worked hard to create a beautiful and tidy garden. They planted a variety of plants that not only enhance the campus's appearance but also help preserve the environment.

Furthermore, Yusuf and his team are committed to continuously maintaining and ensuring the cleanliness of the entire environment, especially the newly planted front garden. They ensure that the garden remains well-maintained and provides a comfortable space for the academic community to conduct activities.

The cleaning team and the UPI Cibiru Campus community play an important role in maintaining the beauty and cleanliness of the campus, so that the academic community can enjoy a comfortable and healthy environment.
