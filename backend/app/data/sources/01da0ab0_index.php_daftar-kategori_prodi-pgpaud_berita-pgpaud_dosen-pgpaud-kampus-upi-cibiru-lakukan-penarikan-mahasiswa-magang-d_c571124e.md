UPI Cibiru Campus PGPAUD Lecturers Withdraw Students from Internships at Mekar Arum Kindergarten

Cibiru, December 10, 2024 – Lecturers of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Campus in Cibiru held a student intern withdrawal event at Mekar Arum Kindergarten. This event marked the end of the students' internship period, which had lasted for several months. The withdrawal event was attended by the supervising lecturer, Mekar Arum Kindergarten teachers, and student interns. The event took place in a warm atmosphere, considering that the interns' contributions during their time at the institution had provided valuable experience for the teachers and students. In his remarks, the supervising lecturer expressed his appreciation to Mekar Arum Kindergarten for the opportunity given to the students to learn and practice directly. "We are very grateful for the support and guidance given to our students during their internship. This experience will be a valuable provision for them in becoming professional educators in the future," he said. The Mekar Arum Kindergarten teachers also expressed their gratitude to the students for their contributions in supporting various learning activities at the school. One teacher stated, “The presence of the interns was very helpful, especially in presenting new, creative ideas in teaching and learning activities.” The students who attended expressed their gratitude and thanks for the guidance received during the internship. “The internship experience at Mekar Arum Kindergarten provided many lessons, both in teaching, interacting with children, and understanding the world of early childhood education in more depth,” said one of the students. The withdrawal of these students was also a touching moment, both for the students and the school. With the end of the internship period, the PGPAUD Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru hopes that the students can apply the experience they have gained in their careers as educators.
