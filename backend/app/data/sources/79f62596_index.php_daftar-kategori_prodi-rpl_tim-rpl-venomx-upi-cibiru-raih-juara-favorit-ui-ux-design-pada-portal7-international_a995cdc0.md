The UPI Cibiru VenomX RPL Team Won the Favorite UI/UX Design Award at the Portal7 International Competition (SDGs 4 & 9)

The Software Engineering (RPL) Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus has once again achieved impressive international success. The VenomX RPL
team won the Favorite Champion in the UI/UX Design category at the Portal7 International Competition , an event showcasing digital work and innovations from various countries.

This achievement demonstrates the RPL students' ability to design creative, functional, and user-oriented interfaces and experiences that meet the needs of modern users.
This success also contributes to the following achievements:

SDGs 4 – Quality Education:
through strengthening students' digital literacy, creativity, and technological skills.

SDGs 9 – Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure:
by encouraging the development of innovative design and technology-based solutions that support a sustainable digital ecosystem.

Congratulations to the RPL VenomX Team on this achievement. May this international achievement motivate them to continue working, innovating, and bringing RPL UPI Cibiru's reputation to the global stage.
