Warmly Welcoming Prof. Dr. Mohamad Hariri Abdullah as an Adjunct Professor at Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia Tasikmalaya Campus
October 24, 2024
Berita UPI Tasikmalaya, Desain Produk Industri
October 14, 2024, We are delighted to announce the arrival of Prof. Dr. Mohamad Hariri Abdullah as an esteemed adjunct professor at Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia, Tasikmalaya Campus. He will share his knowledge, experience, and classroom practices with the students of the Industrial Product Design Program at UPI Tasikmalaya, offering invaluable insights into product design development.

Prof. Dr. Hariri’s presence is an extraordinary opportunity for our students to gain firsthand knowledge from an expert with deep experience in his field. With a focus on innovation and sustainable practices, he will equip students with practical skills and insights relevant to the challenges of today’s industry.

We are excited to welcome him, as his presence will enrich the classroom learning experience and open new opportunities for students to apply creative and innovative design concepts. We look forward to an inspiring learning journey with him! (Dilardi Ramadhan)
