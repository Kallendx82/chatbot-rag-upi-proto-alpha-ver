UPIISM Odd Semester 2024/2025 Student Graduation Ceremony: Going Global with the Indonesian Education University

Bandung, October 2, 2024 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) held a farewell ceremony for students participating in the 2024/2025 Odd Semester UPI International Student Mobility (UPIISM) Program . The event was attended by students from various study programs, including representatives from the Multimedia Education Study Program, who will continue their studies at international partner universities.

This year, UPIISM students will be placed in various leading universities, including the National University of Malaysia, Universiti Malaya Kelantan, and a number of educational institutions in Asia, Europe, and Australia.

The farewell event also included a sharing session from UPIISM alumni who had completed previous programs. They shared their experiences and offered tips to new participants on maximizing their study abroad opportunities.

With this program, UPI further strengthens its position as a university that supports international mobility, making students the main actors in realizing the vision of global education.
