Happy National Education Day 2025

Bandung, May 2, 2025 – The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) campus in Cibiru held a ceremony to commemorate National Education Day (Hardiknas) 2025 on Friday morning, May 2, 2025, at the UPI Cibiru Campus Ceremony Field. The event was solemn, lively, and meaningful, carrying the national theme "Universal Participation Achieves Quality Education for All."

The ceremony began at 7:30 a.m. WIB and was attended by various members of the academic community, including lecturers, educational staff, university students, and students from the UPI Cibiru Laboratory Kindergarten, Elementary School, Middle School, and High School. The UPI Family Mothers Association (IIK) also participated. All participants wore traditional attire as a symbol of the nation's rich culture and the spirit of diversity in education.

In his remarks, the Director of the UPI Cibiru Campus, Dr. Deni Darmawan, emphasized the importance of cross-sector collaboration in advancing education. "This year's theme invites all of us, without exception, to participate in efforts to improve the quality of education. Education cannot work alone; it requires the participation of all parties: teachers, parents, the government, the community, and the students themselves," he said.

The National Education Day commemoration at the UPI Cibiru Campus also serves as a momentum to reaffirm the noble values ​​of education as inherited by Ki Hadjar Dewantara, a pioneering figure in Indonesian education, whose birthday is commemorated as National Education Day.

Through this commemoration, UPI Cibiru hopes that the collaborative and mutual cooperation spirit in the world of education can continue to grow and inspire all levels of society to work together to build an intelligent and character-based future for Indonesia.
