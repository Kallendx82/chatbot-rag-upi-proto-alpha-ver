PGPAUD Students Win Second Place in the 2025 UPI Cibiru Outstanding Student Selection

Cibiru, February 6, 2025 — Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia Cibiru Campus once again held the 2025 Faculty Level Outstanding Student Selection (Pilmapres) on Thursday, February 6, 2025. This activity is an annual event that aims to select the best students who have superior achievements both academically and non-academically.

Congratulations and appreciation are given to Arij Fathi Bauzir (2204035), a student of the PGPAUD Study Program, who succeeded in winning 2nd Place in the Outstanding Student at the UPI Campus Level in Cibiru 2025. This achievement is a real proof of Arij's dedication, enthusiasm, and commitment in developing his potential and making a positive contribution to the campus.

The Pilmapres (Pilmapres) assesses not only academic achievement but also student involvement in organizational activities, scientific papers, innovation, and community service. Through this activity, UPI Cibiru Campus directly contributes to SDG 4 (Quality Education) because the Pilmapres encourages the creation of a quality, competitive educational environment, and is oriented towards developing a generation of students who are superior, creative, and ready to contribute to the development of education in Indonesia. This event also strengthens a healthy and sustainable academic culture as part of efforts to improve the quality of higher education.

This achievement is expected to inspire other students to continue their work and bring honor to UPI Cibiru Campus at the regional and national levels. The Early Childhood Education Program (PGPAUD) expresses its pride and highest appreciation to Arij for his accomplishments. May this achievement be the first step in a journey of even more glorious achievements in the future.
