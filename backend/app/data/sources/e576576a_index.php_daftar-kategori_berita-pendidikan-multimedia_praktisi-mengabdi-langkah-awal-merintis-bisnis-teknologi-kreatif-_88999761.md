Practitioners Serve: Initial Steps to Pioneering a Creative Technology Business at UPI Cibiru Campus

Novi Setia Nurviat , Founder & Owner of PT. Inovindo Digital Media, in the Chatting About Technopreneurship seminar , Thursday (11/13/2025) in Bandung, West Java.

 

Bandung, November 13, 2025 - The Multimedia Education Study Program, Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Cibiru Campus, once again presents an inspiring activity entitled "Practitioners Serve: Technopreneurship." This event carries the theme "Chatting About Technopreneurship: The First Steps to Pioneering a Creative Technology Business" and takes place in the Multimedia Computer Laboratory on Thursday, November 13, 2025, from 1:00 PM onwards.

The event featured Novi Setia Nurviat, Founder & Owner of PT. Inovindo Digital Media and founder of the Daarul Adzkar Foundation, as the keynote speaker. With extensive experience in digital media and technology business, Novi shared insights on how students can start and develop technology-based creative businesses in today's digital era.

This event was hosted by Nurhidayatulloh, S.Pd., MT, as moderator and Head of the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus. In his remarks, he emphasized the importance of the technopreneurship spirit for multimedia students so that they not only become users of technology, but also creators and developers of innovative solutions that have an impact. In addition to strengthening students' interest in technopreneurship skills , this Practitioners Serving activity also supports the achievement of Sustainable Development Goals point 4, namely Quality Education. Through this activity, students are encouraged to develop 21st-century competencies such as creativity, critical thinking, and technology-based innovation in line with UPI's mission to produce superior, adaptive, and globally competitive graduates.

The Practitioners Serve activity represents a tangible synergy between academia and industry practitioners , providing students with a platform to learn directly from the experiences of creative business owners. Participant enthusiasm was high, with many students attending and actively participating in the interactive discussion sessions. This event is expected to foster a technology-based entrepreneurial spirit among students and serve as the first step in creating a generation of innovative and competitive young technopreneurs.

 

For further information regarding Multimedia Study Program activities , you can visit the official Instagram account @multimedia.upi or go directly to UPI Cibiru Campus .
