Thesis Defense Examination for the S1 PGSD Study Program, UPI Cibiru, Period 20 August 2025

The Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program (PGSD) of the UPI Campus in Cibiru held a Thesis Defense Examination for the period Wednesday, August 20, 2025, at Building B, 5th Floor. The defense began at 08.00 WIB until finished and was attended by 38 students as defense participants.

The implementation of this trial involved a number of examining lecturers from various fields of expertise, in accordance with the official letter of assignment signed by the Director of the UPI Campus in Cibiru, Prof. Dr. Deni Darmawan, S.Pd., M.Si., M.Kom., MCE.

In her remarks, the Chair of the Thesis Council, Winti Ananthia, S.Pd., M.Ed., emphasized the importance of punctuality in completing post-defense administrative processes leading up to the graduation ceremony. She emphasized that the smoothness of this process is crucial for students' readiness for the upcoming graduation ceremony.

Meanwhile, the Secretary of the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program, Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., provided motivational encouragement to the participants. She stated that the thesis defense is not only an academic exam, but also a demonstration of students' readiness as future professional educators capable of contributing to the world of elementary education in Indonesia.

By holding this trial, it is hoped that the PGSD UPI Cibiru students will be able to complete their studies well and bring a spirit of innovation in developing learning in elementary schools.

The UPI Cibiru Campus Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program and other study programs held a Disaster Mitigation and Management Workshop in collaboration with Kitakyushu University, Japan.

Bandung – The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Cibiru Campus held a Disaster Mitigation and Management Workshop on Tuesday, August 19, 2025. This activity is part of a research collaboration program between UPI Cibiru and The University of Kitakyushu, Japan.

The workshop presented speakers from both universities, including Prof. Kodama Yayoi, Asst. Prof. Murae Fumitoshi, and Prof. Hiroyuki Miyake from Kitakyushu University, as well as Dr. Indriani Rachman, Ph.D from UPI Cibiru.

Approximately 50 students from the Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Program at UPI Cibiru attended the event. Participants were briefed on applicable disaster mitigation and response strategies, aiming to build student awareness and preparedness for potential disasters in their local environment.

Melalui kegiatan ini, diharapkan terjalin hubungan kerjasama akademik yang lebih erat antara UPI Cibiru dan Universitas Kitakyushu, khususnya dalam bidang penelitian kebencanaan. Selain itu, mahasiswa juga dapat memperluas wawasan global sekaligus mengimplementasikan ilmu yang diperoleh ke dalam kehidupan nyata.

Perwujudan Pendidikan Nilai melalui Model Resolusi Konflik berbasis Cartoon Digital (PRK-CD) dalam Mendukung Tujuan 4 SDGs: Pendidikan Berkualitas

Tim dosen Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Kampus Cibiru melaksanakan kegiatan Pengabdian kepada Masyarakat di Universitas Wiralodra, Indramayu, pada Rabu, 13 Agustus 2025. Kegiatan ini mengusung tema “Perwujudan Pendidikan Nilai melalui Model Resolusi Konflik berbasis Cartoon Digital (PRK-CD)” sebagai kontribusi nyata dalam mendukung Tujuan 4 SDGs: Pendidikan Berkualitas.

Adapun tim dosen yang terlibat dalam kegiatan ini adalah Dr. Jenuri, S.Ag., M.Pd., Dr. Yona Wahyuningsih, S.Pd., M.Pd., dan Mega Laeni, S.Pd., M.Si. Selain itu, kegiatan ini juga melibatkan Daffa Wahid Prasetyo, mahasiswa S1 Program Studi Pendidikan Guru Sekolah Dasar (PGSD), yang turut mendukung pelaksanaan kegiatan di lapangan.

Dalam pelaksanaannya, tim memperkenalkan Model Resolusi Konflik berbasis Cartoon Digital (PRK-CD) sebagai inovasi pembelajaran yang memadukan pendidikan karakter dengan pemanfaatan media digital. Model ini dirancang untuk membantu peserta didik memahami cara penyelesaian konflik secara kreatif, damai, dan mendidik, sehingga mampu menumbuhkan karakter positif di lingkungan pendidikan.

Melalui seminar, diskusi, dan pendampingan interaktif, para dosen bersama mahasiswa berkolaborasi dengan civitas akademika Universitas Wiralodra. Kegiatan ini menegaskan pentingnya integrasi pendidikan nilai dalam pembelajaran sebagai bagian dari upaya peningkatan mutu pendidikan di Indonesia.

Kegiatan pengabdian ini menjadi bukti nyata peran UPI dalam mendukung pencapaian Tujuan 4 SDGs: Pendidikan Berkualitas, dengan menghadirkan inovasi pembelajaran berbasis digital yang relevan dengan kebutuhan zaman dan berorientasi pada pembentukan karakter generasi muda.

Dari Kami untuk Negeri: Merdeka!

Civitas akademika Program Studi Pendidikan Guru Sekolah Dasar (PGSD) Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Kampus Cibiru turut memperingati Hari Ulang Tahun (HUT) Kemerdekaan Republik Indonesia ke-80 dengan penuh khidmat dan kebersamaan.

Dalam kegiatan yang digelar pada Ahad (17/8/2025), para dosen dan tenaga kependidikan mengikuti rangkaian peringatan dengan penuh semangat nasionalisme. Acara ini menjadi momentum penting untuk meneguhkan kembali komitmen dalam mendidik generasi bangsa yang berkarakter, berdaya saing, dan cinta tanah air.

The spirit of independence inherited by the nation's heroes is expected to continue to be an inspiration for UPI Cibiru Campus, especially the PGSD Study Program, in producing professional and integrated educators.

"Hopefully, this spirit of independence will always be an encouragement for us in carrying out the noble task of enlightening the nation's life," said one of the representatives of the PGSD lecturers at UPI Cibiru.

With gratitude, this commemoration is not only interpreted as a ceremony, but also as a reflection to continue contributing to Indonesian education by strengthening the character and competence of students.
