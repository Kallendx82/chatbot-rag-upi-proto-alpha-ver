"Cultivating Student Character and Ethics" Held at SDN Tebet Timur 07 Jakarta

In an effort to strengthen character education in elementary schools, the "Developing Student Character and Ethics" seminar was successfully held at SDN Tebet Timur 07 Jakarta as a hybrid session . Seventeen elementary school teachers enthusiastically participated in the series of materials and discussions.

This seminar carries the theme: "Developing Student Character and Ethics with Creative Methods Through Habituation" , which emphasizes the importance of forming student attitudes and behavior from an early age through a creative, consistent, and fun approach.

The keynote speaker, Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, S.Pd., M.Pd., MH , a lecturer and education practitioner, delivered the material. In her presentation, she emphasized that positive habits in schools are a crucial foundation for developing ethical, disciplined, and responsible students.

The seminar was well-received by the teachers in attendance. They hoped that similar activities could continue regularly as part of improving educator capacity, particularly in strengthening character education at the elementary school level.

Through this activity, it is hoped that teachers can implement creative and meaningful habituation methods in daily learning, so that they can produce a generation that is not only intellectually intelligent, but also has noble morals.
