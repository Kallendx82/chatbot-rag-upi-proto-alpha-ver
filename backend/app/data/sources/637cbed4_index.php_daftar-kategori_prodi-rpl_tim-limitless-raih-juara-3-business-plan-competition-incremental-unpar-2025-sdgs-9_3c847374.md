The LIMITLESS Team Won 3rd Place in the 2025 UNPAR INCREMENTAL Business Plan Competition (SDGs 9)

The LIMITLESS Team from the Software Engineering Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus also achieved a proud achievement  .
This team won third place in the 2025 UNPAR INCREMENTAL Business Plan Competition , an event focused on innovative business strategies based on research and development of digital solutions.

This achievement confirms the ability of RPL students to identify market opportunities, develop implementation strategies, and integrate technology in designing business models.

This achievement is in line with:

SDGs 9 – Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure , by encouraging the birth of technology-based business ideas and designs that are oriented towards sustainability and long-term impact.

Congratulations to the LIMITLESS Team on this remarkable achievement. May this be the first step towards innovative work and further development opportunities in the future.
