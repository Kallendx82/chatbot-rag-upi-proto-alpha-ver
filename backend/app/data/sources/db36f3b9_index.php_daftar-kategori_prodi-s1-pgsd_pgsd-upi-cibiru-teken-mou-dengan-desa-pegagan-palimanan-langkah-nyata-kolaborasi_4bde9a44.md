UPI Cibiru's Elementary School Teacher Education Department Signs MoU with Pegagan Village, Palimanan: A Concrete Step in Collaborating on Education and Community Empowerment
 Cibiru, July 2025 – The Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus officially signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Pegagan Village Government, Palimanan District, Cirebon Regency. The signing of this MoU marks the beginning of a multi-stakeholder collaboration that covers basic education, student character development, and the involvement of PGSD students in community service activities in Pegagan Village. This collaboration aims to strengthen the connection between the campus and the community, particularly through direct programs relevant to the village's needs.
Dr. Yona Wahyuningsih, M.Pd., representing the UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Department, and the Pegagan village head shook hands as a symbol of support for this shared commitment. The event also marked the readiness of both parties to begin mentoring programs, such as teacher training, literacy mentoring, and community-based learning practices.
Through this initiative, the UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Department demonstrates its commitment to upholding the "independent campus" principle and the Tri Dharma of Higher Education, balancing education, research, and community service. Going forward, this collaboration is expected to enrich students' experiences while improving the quality of basic education in Pegagan Village.
