Optimizing Sustainable Programs: UPI Cibiru Campus Director Reviews GreenMetric Implementation and Catfish Cultivation

Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia Cibiru Campus continues to strengthen its commitment to supporting sustainable campuses through the implementation of the GreenMetric program and education-based catfish cultivation. As a form of support for the program, the Director of UPI Cibiru Campus, Prof. Karim Suryadi, directly reviewed the program that has been and is being implemented by Dr. Yusuf Tri Herlambang in the back area near the UPI Cibiru Campus female dormitory on Tuesday (May 12). The review was carried out to see the progress of the program and discuss development plans to support the optimization of GreenMetric on campus.

This program aligns with the campus's commitment to impact and supports SDGs 4, 12, and 13 by strengthening education, environmental management, and campus sustainability. Strategic recommendations include strengthening inter-unit synergy, optimizing supporting facilities, and engaging students to maximize GreenMetric implementation. During the event, the Director and staff visited the catfish cultivation area and discussed the development of facilities to support sustainable programs on campus.
