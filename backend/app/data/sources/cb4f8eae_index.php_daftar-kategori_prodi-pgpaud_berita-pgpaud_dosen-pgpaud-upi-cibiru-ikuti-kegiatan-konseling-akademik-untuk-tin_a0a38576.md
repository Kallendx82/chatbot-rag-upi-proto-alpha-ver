UPI Cibiru PGPAUD Lecturers Participate in Academic Counseling Activities to Improve Student Guidance Services

Cibiru, June 20, 2025 — Lecturers of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus participated in the Academic Counseling activity held on Friday, June 20, 2025. This activity is part of an effort to improve the quality of academic services and student guidance on campus. Academic counseling is an important component in assisting students during the lecture process, not only in academic aspects, but also to help them face various challenges in their studies and self-development. In this activity, lecturers received provisions related to effective communication strategies in guidance, mapping student academic problems, as well as relevant and empathetic approaches in assisting students towards optimal academic achievement. Through this academic counseling activity, the PGPAUD Study Program hopes that lecturers will be increasingly prepared to be supportive and solution-oriented companions, so that students feel more guided in undergoing the learning and lecture process as a whole.
