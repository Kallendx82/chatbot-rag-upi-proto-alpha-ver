Four Multimedia Education Students Undertake Proposal Seminar Exam

Cibiru, September 19, 2024 — On September 18, 2024 , four students from the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru took a proposal seminar exam as part of the academic requirements before proceeding to the thesis stage. This activity is a crucial step for students to present their research ideas to the examining lecturers.

The proposal seminar exam was attended by lecturers from the Multimedia Education Study Program, who served as examiners and provided feedback to the students. Each student presented their research plan, which included the background, objectives, methodology, and expected contributions of the research.

This proposal seminar exam serves not only as an evaluation but also as an opportunity for students to practice public speaking and hone their presentation skills. It is hoped that students will use this experience to strengthen their future research.

With high enthusiasm, students are expected to be able to complete their thesis process and make a positive contribution to science in the field of multimedia education.
