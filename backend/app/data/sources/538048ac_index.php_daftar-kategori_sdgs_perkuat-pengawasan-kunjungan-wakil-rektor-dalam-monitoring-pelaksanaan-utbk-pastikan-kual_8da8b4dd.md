Strengthening Oversight: The Vice Chancellor's Visit to Monitor the Implementation of the UTBK Ensures Quality of Service at UPI's Cibiru Campus

UPI Cibiru Campus continues to strengthen its commitment to maintaining the quality of service and integrity of the Computer-Based Written Exam (UTBK) through ongoing monitoring. This effort is crucial in ensuring the entire selection process meets standards and provides optimal service to exam participants.

The strategic role of UPI Cibiru Campus extends beyond technical implementation to ensuring a transparent and accountable quality assurance system within the higher education selection system. This initiative is expected to boost public trust and create a safe, orderly, and comfortable exam experience for all participants.

This activity was held at the UPI Cibiru Campus, with Prof. Dr. Vanessa Gaffar, SE., Ak., MBA, as Vice Rector, in attendance. During her visit, she personally inspected various campus facilities and services to ensure that the entire UTBK implementation process ran smoothly and in accordance with established standards.

This activity is also in line with the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) , especially SDGs 4: Quality Education , through efforts to ensure an inclusive, fair, and quality higher education selection system for all prospective students.
