The Elementary and Early Childhood Education (PGSD) and Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Programs at UPI Cibiru Campus Undergo Online ACQUIN International Accreditation Visitation.

Cibiru, July 3, 2025 — Two flagship study programs at the Indonesian University of Education, Cibiru Campus, namely the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program (PGSD) and the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD), have undergone an online international accreditation visitation by the ACQUIN (Accreditation, Certification and Quality Assurance Institute) on July 3 and 4, 2025.

This activity is part of the effort to ensure the quality of higher education based on international standards, by assessing various aspects such as curriculum, learning process, governance, resources, and stakeholder involvement in the development of study programs.

Despite being conducted online, the assessment proceeded smoothly and professionally. The ACQUIN assessment team reviewed documents and conducted interviews with campus leaders, program heads, lecturers, students, alumni, and business and industry partners via video conferencing.

The active participation of all elements, from lecturers, students, educational staff to external partners, is proof of UPI Cibiru's commitment to building superior study programs that meet international standards.

With the implementation of this visitation, it is hoped that the PGSD and PGPAUD Study Programs at the UPI Cibiru Campus will receive international recognition from ACQUIN, while also opening up opportunities for broader collaboration with global institutions in the future.
