Notice: IRS Guardianship and Filing Period August 1 - 27, 2024

Cibiru, August 1, 2024 — It is announced to all students of the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru that the guardianship period and Study Plan Entry (IRS) will be held from August 1 to 27, 2024 .

The advisory period is a crucial time for students to consult with their academic advisors regarding course selection and study plans for the upcoming semester. During advisory sessions, students can discuss their academic progress and receive advice on courses that align with their interests and career goals.

Completing the IRS is a crucial step every student must take to ensure all courses taken are officially recorded. Students are expected to complete and submit their IRS before the specified deadline to ensure a smooth administrative process.

All students are expected to adhere to the specified schedule and go through the guardianship process and IRS filling out as well as possible.
