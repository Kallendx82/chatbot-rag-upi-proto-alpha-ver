Optimizing the Library's Function as a Space for Study, Discussion, and Knowledge Seeking

UPI Cibiru Campus continues to improve the quality of academic services by optimizing the library on the 4th floor of the New Building as a study, discussion, and knowledge-seeking space for students. This facility is designed to create a comfortable and conducive academic environment and support student competency development.

As a higher education institution, UPI Cibiru Campus provides a library as a supporting facility that promotes a culture of literacy and academic development. Students use the library to access references, broaden their horizons, support research, prepare academic assignments, and collaboratively discuss and exchange ideas.

The existence of this library has a positive impact on the development of a learning culture in the campus environment and supports the creation of an active and productive academic atmosphere.

This activity is also in line with the achievement of SDGs 4: Quality Education , through providing access to inclusive, quality, and sustainable educational facilities and learning resources for the entire academic community.
