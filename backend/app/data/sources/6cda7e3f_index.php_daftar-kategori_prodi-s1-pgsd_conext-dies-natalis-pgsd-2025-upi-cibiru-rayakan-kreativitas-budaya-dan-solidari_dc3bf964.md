CONEXT & PGSD Dies Natalis 2025 UPI Cibiru: Celebrating Student Creativity, Culture, and Solidarity

Bandung, September 13, 2025 – The Elementary School Teacher Education Student Association (PGSD) of the Indonesian Education University (UPI) Cibiru Campus successfully held the peak event of CONEXT (Contest and Exhibition) & PGSD Dies Natalis 2025 in the front yard of the new UPI Cibiru building. Carrying the theme "Educreafest (Education, Creativity and Festival)" , this event presented a cultural celebration, a space for expression, and collaboration that combined the richness of Nusantara culture with the spirit of competition and educational innovation.

The committee chairman said that this activity was designed as a concrete manifestation of PGSD's commitment to fostering a young generation that is creative, cares about culture, and is ready to contribute to the nation.

The 2025 PGSD CONEXT & Anniversary series has been underway since last July. On July 31, 2025, the committee held a Technical Meeting , attended by participants from the Greater Bandung elementary school futsal competition, the West Java elementary school math olympiad, the national student essay competition, and the band competition for youth aged 15–25. This meeting ensured participant readiness and established the competition mechanism.

The competition began with a futsal competition on August 2–3, 2025. This tournament was attended by a number of elementary schools in Greater Bandung, and was won by the SDT Krida Nusantara futsal team after defeating MI Abdurrahman in the final.

Next, on August 7, 2025, a national online student essay competition was held, with nine participants from various universities. On the same day, a West Java elementary school mathematics olympiad was attended by 41 students from various schools, with Izzqiyan Adelard Dwindra from UPI Labschool Elementary School taking first place.

The highlight of the event on September 13, 2025, included a tumpeng (rice cone) cutting ceremony, performances by UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) lecturers, an Indonesian culinary competition, a mini fashion show , a student festival, and band performances by the contestants. Awards were also presented to the winners.

"Through this event, we want to present more than just a celebration. This is a moment to strengthen togetherness, solidarity, and foster a positive spirit among students," said the committee.

With the spirit of Cultureverse , the 2025 PGSD Anniversary will not only be an entertainment event, but also a forum to hone creativity, sportsmanship, and strengthen collaboration between students, lecturers, and the educational community.
