The Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru Congratulates the Vice Chancellors of UPI for the 2025-2030 Period

Bandung, July 2025 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) officially inaugurated five Vice Rectors for the 2025–2030 term. This inauguration is a crucial part of the university's leadership restructuring to strengthen institutional governance in addressing the challenges of education in the digital and global era.

The five Vice Chancellors who were inaugurated and their areas of responsibility are as follows:

Prof. Dr. Vannesa Gaffar, SE, Ak., MBA.
Vice Rector for Education and Quality Assurance
Prof. Vannesa will lead the strategy for improving academic quality, accreditation, and curriculum development across all UPI educational units.

Prof. Dr. Tri Indri Hardini, M.Pd.
Vice Rector for Human Resources and Information Systems
She is responsible for human resource management, information system strengthening, and campus digital governance.

Prof. Dr. Agus Setiabudi, M.Si.
Vice Rector for Research, Community Service, and Partnerships
Focuses on improving research performance, community service, and establishing synergies with national and international strategic partners.

Prof. Dr. Rudi Susilana, M.Si.,
Vice Rector for Planning and Finance.
He is responsible for designing and overseeing the university's strategic planning and financial management policies.

Prof. Dr. Phill. Yudi Sukmayadi, M.Pd.
Vice Rector for Student Affairs, Alumni, and Business
Responsible for developing student services, strengthening alumni networks, and managing campus business units based on entrepreneurship.

The inauguration was enthusiastically received by the entire UPI academic community, including those at regional campuses such as Cibiru, Sumedang, and others. It is hoped that this new leadership will further propel UPI as a pioneering university and a leader in education, research, and social contribution.
