UPI Cibiru PGPAUD Participates in UPI's 2025 Open Visit, Showcasing the Excellence of Its Flagship Programs

Cibiru, February 11, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru participated in the Open Visit UPI 2025, an annual event organized by the Indonesian University of Education as an effort to introduce the campus profile, study programs, and important information regarding new student admissions to the public. The event, which was open to prospective students, teachers, parents, and the general public, was a strategic moment for the PGPAUD Study Program to demonstrate its commitment and quality of education. In this activity, visitors had the opportunity to interact directly with PGPAUD lecturers and students, see campus facilities, and obtain in-depth information about the excellence of the study program. PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Campus has now been accredited as UNGGUL by the Independent Education Accreditation Institute (LAMDIK), indicating that its educational quality has met the highest national standards. Supported by experienced and highly qualified professional teachers in the field of early childhood education, PGPAUD is also equipped with complete learning support facilities, such as modern classrooms, PAUD Laboratory, Computer Laboratory, library, and other supporting facilities. Through this Open Visit, PGPAUD not only promotes its flagship program, but also invites prospective students to learn more about the world of early childhood education which is now increasingly developing and needed. With quality, facilities, and a supportive academic atmosphere, the UPI Campus in Cibiru is the right choice for young people who want to pursue a career in education with strong competencies and superior character.
