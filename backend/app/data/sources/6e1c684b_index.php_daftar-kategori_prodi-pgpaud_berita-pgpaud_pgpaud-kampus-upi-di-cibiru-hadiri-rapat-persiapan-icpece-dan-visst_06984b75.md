UPI Campus PGPAUD in Cibiru Attends Preparatory Meeting for ICPECE and VISSTE 2025

Cibiru, November 4, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Program (PGPAUD) at the UPI Cibiru Campus attended the ICPECE (International Conference on Primary and Early Childhood Education) and VISSTE Preparatory Meeting held in the Deputy Director's Room at the UPI Cibiru Campus. The meeting was attended by the Head of the PGPAUD Study Program, members of the implementation team, and the Deputy Director of the UPI Cibiru Campus. The meeting discussed the preparation of the conference agenda, the division of committee tasks, coordination with UPI's central office, and strategies for publication and documentation of the event.

The Head of the Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Program emphasized the importance of strong collaboration between committees to ensure the success of both international academic events. The Deputy Director also provided guidance on strengthening technical and administrative preparations to ensure a more effective and professional conference. The meeting proceeded smoothly and resulted in several strategic decisions to support the implementation of ICPECE and VISSTE in 2025.

The implementation of this meeting also reflects the commitment of PGPAUD UPI Cibiru in supporting the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), in particular:

SDG 4: Quality Education, through strengthening international academic activities that encourage knowledge exchange, improving the quality of education, and developing the competencies of educators and prospective educators.

SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals, by establishing collaboration between study programs, campus leaders, and various national and international partners in organizing scientific conferences.

This conducive meeting resulted in several important agreements to ensure that ICPECE and VISSTE 2025 run effectively, professionally, and provide a real contribution to the development of early childhood education and global educational innovation.
