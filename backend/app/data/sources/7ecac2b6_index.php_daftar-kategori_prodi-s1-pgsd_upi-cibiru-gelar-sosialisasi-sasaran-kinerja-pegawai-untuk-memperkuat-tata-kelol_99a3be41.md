UPI Cibiru Holds Employee Performance Target Socialization to Strengthen Campus Governance

The Cibiru Campus of the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) held an Employee Performance Targets (SKP) Socialization event to strengthen the employee performance management system on campus. Lecturers and educational staff from various work units participated in the event, demonstrating the institution's commitment to achieving professional, accountable, and sustainable higher education governance.

The SKP socialization aims to improve employee understanding of measurable performance planning, implementation, and evaluation that aligns with their respective main duties and functions. Through this activity, participants received an explanation of the performance-based SKP preparation mechanism, as well as its relationship to organizational performance achievements and improvements in the quality of academic and non-academic services at UPI Cibiru Campus.
The implementation of this activity is in line with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), specifically SDG Goal 16, namely Peace, Justice, and Resilient Institutions, through strengthening transparent, effective, and accountable institutions. In addition, optimal employee performance management also supports SDG Goal 4, Quality Education, by ensuring the delivery of quality and competitive higher education services.

Through the socialization of this SKP, UPI Cibiru Campus is expected to be able to strengthen a performance-based work culture, improve the professionalism of human resources, and encourage the realization of superior campus governance in supporting the achievement of the vision and mission of the Indonesian Education University.
