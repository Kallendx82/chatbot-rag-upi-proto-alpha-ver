UPI Cibiru's Elementary School Teacher Education Department Holds Initial Preparatory Meeting for the 2025/2026 Even Semester

The Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Study Program at the Indonesian Education University (UPI) Cibiru Campus held an Initial Preparatory Meeting for the Even Semester as a strategic step in ensuring academic and institutional readiness for the even semester learning process of the 2025/2026 Academic Year. This activity was held on Monday, January 26, 2026, at the 1st floor of Building E and was attended by lecturers of the PGSD Study Program as part of an effort to strengthen coordination, planning, and synchronization of academic programs.

This meeting serves as a crucial forum for building quality, sustainable, and adaptive educational governance to address future educational challenges. Through structured and collaborative planning, the UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Department is committed to supporting the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly SDG 4 (Quality Education), by providing an inclusive, effective learning system that focuses on developing the competencies of prospective elementary school educators. This activity also reflects the institution's commitment to strengthening a professional, accountable, and sustainable academic culture to achieve high-quality and globally competitive elementary education.
