Effective RKAT Preparation: Strategy to Improve Performance, as well as Targeted Efficiency and Effectiveness, in Realizing SDGS 9, 16

Saturday, October 4, 2025

To improve performance and efficiency, campus leaders undertake external assignments and prepare Annual Activity Plans and Budgets (RKAT) outside the campus. These activities aim to improve the quality and effectiveness of the RKAT and strengthen collaboration and communication between leaders.

By undertaking external assignments and preparing the RKAT (Work Plan and Budget) off-campus, leaders can focus and concentrate on developing appropriate strategic plans to improve performance and efficiency. This activity also provides an opportunity for leaders to discuss and share experiences, thereby improving the quality and effectiveness of the RKAT.

In the RKAT preparation process, leaders carry out analysis and evaluation of performance and activities that have been carried out previously. They also identify strengths and weaknesses, as well as opportunities and challenges faced by the campus. In this way, leaders can prepare a more effective and targeted RKAT, as well as improve campus performance and efficiency.

This off-campus RKAT preparation activity is expected to yield positive results and impact campus performance and efficiency. With an effective and targeted RKAT, the campus can improve its quality and reputation, and achieve its stated goals and objectives.
