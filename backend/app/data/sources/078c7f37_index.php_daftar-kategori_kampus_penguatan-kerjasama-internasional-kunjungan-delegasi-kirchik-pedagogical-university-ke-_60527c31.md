STRENGTHENING INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION: VISIT OF CHIRCHIK PEDAGOGICAL UNIVERSITY DELEGATION TO UPI CAMPUS IN CIBIRU

UPI Cibiru - On Thursday, November 28, 2024, the Cibiru Campus of the Indonesian Education University (UPI) received an official visit from a delegation of approximately 10 people from Chirchik Pedagogical University, Uzbekistan. This visit was part of an effort to strengthen international relations and establish cooperation in the field of education. During the event, the delegation was warmly welcomed by the leaders and Heads of Study Programs at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, demonstrating UPI Cibiru's commitment to establishing global collaborations to improve the quality of education at the international level.

The purpose of this visit is to introduce the academic potential and facilities of UPI Cibiru Campus to Chirchik Pedagogical University, as well as to open up opportunities for collaboration in various fields, especially in student exchange programs, joint research, and curriculum development that is more oriented to global needs. Through this collaboration, both parties are expected to be able to exchange knowledge and experience in the world of education, enrich academic insight, and support the improvement of the quality of graduates who have international competitiveness. Furthermore, in 2025 will send students to Uzbekistan as well as from Uzbekistan will send their students to UPI Cibiru, while lecturers from UPI Cibiru will teach in Uzbekistan and vice versa, Uzbekistan lecturers will teach at UPI Cibiru

The goal of this collaboration is to build a long-term, mutually beneficial relationship, with various collaborative programs that can expand the scope of education in both countries. It is hoped that this collaboration will strengthen the friendship between Indonesia and Uzbekistan, as well as have a positive impact on the development of higher education at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, by enhancing the reputation and opportunities for students and lecturers to develop at the international level.
