UIN BANDUNG STUDENTS VISIT UPI LIBRARY ON CIBIRU CAMPUS

Cibiru, UPI Cibiru Campus Library received an Observation Visit of 3 (three) Students of the Department of Islamic Library and Information Science, UIN Sunan Gunung Djati Bandung and were received directly by the library coordinator, Mrs. Harti Annisa, S.Pd. This observation activity was carried out to fulfill the Advanced Information Organization Course with the title "Information Management and Collection Classification at the UPI Cibiru Campus Library. 

During the observation visit, the library team explained the scope of the UPI Cibiru Campus Library, including library management, services, infrastructure, and how the collections are classified and organized. Through this observation visit, students are expected to gain a deep and practical understanding of the information management process, particularly collection classification, as applied in a university library environment.
