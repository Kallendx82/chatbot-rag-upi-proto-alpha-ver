Optimizing Drinking Water Facilities: Regular Filter Maintenance as a Support for Campus Prime Services in Maintaining Drinking Water Quality

UPI Cibiru Campus continues to improve the quality of campus facilities through regular maintenance of the Fure dispenser located in the Blue Building Lobby, 1st Floor. This maintenance includes replacing the water filter to maintain the quality, cleanliness, and suitability of drinking water used by the academic community. This step demonstrates the campus' commitment to providing healthy, comfortable facilities that support daily academic activities.

Water filter maintenance is carried out as part of efforts to maintain the cleanliness standards of public facilities on campus. Regular filter replacement ensures that drinking water quality is maintained, allowing students, lecturers, and educational staff to safely and comfortably use the facilities. This activity also demonstrates UPI's commitment to the health and well-being of its academic community through the provision of optimal campus services.

By optimizing these drinking water facilities, UPI continues to promote a clean, healthy campus environment that positively impacts all service users. The availability of hygienic drinking water is one form of excellent campus service in supporting UPI's "impactful" programs, particularly Goal 3 on Good Health and Well-being and Goal 6 on Clean Water and Sanitation by providing access to clean and safe drinking water.
