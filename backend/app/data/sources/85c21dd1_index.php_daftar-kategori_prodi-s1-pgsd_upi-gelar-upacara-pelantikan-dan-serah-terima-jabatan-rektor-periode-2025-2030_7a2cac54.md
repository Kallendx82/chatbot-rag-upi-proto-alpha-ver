UPI Holds Inauguration and Handover Ceremony for Rector for the 2025–2030 Period

Bandung, June 16, 2025 — The Indonesian Education University (UPI) held the Inauguration and Handover of the UPI Rector for the 2025–2030 Period on Monday, June 16, 2025. The event was held solemnly at the Achmad Sanusi Building, UPI Bandung Campus, starting at 09.00 WIB until finished.

This activity marked the transition of leadership from the previous Chancellor, Prof. Dr. M. Solehuddin, M.Pd., MA, to the elected Chancellor for the 2025–2030 period, Prof. Dr. Didi Sukyadi, MA. Also present at the event were university leaders, faculty deans, directors of regional campuses, heads of study programs, lecturers, and student elements.

Embracing the spirit of "Values ​​for Value, Full Commitment, No Conspiracy," the inauguration ceremony marked a significant milestone in the sustainability of campus governance and development. In his remarks, UPI Rector Prof. Dr. Didi Sukyadi, MA, affirmed his commitment to leading UPI to greater excellence and impact at the national and global levels, through strengthening the Tri Dharma of Higher Education and professional and inclusive governance.

A warm atmosphere was evident during the handover ceremony and congratulatory messages from the UPI academic community to the new rector. The Head of the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program, the Secretary of the Study Program, and the Vice Director of Academic and Student Affairs at the UPI Campus in Cibiru also personally congratulated UPI Rector Prof. Dr. Didi Sukyadi, MA. Documentation of the event shows the enthusiasm of the participants who attended in person, filled with a spirit of togetherness and new hopes.

With new leadership, UPI looks to the future with optimism to continue to be a pioneer and implementer of quality education, globally competitive, and firmly rooted in national and cultural values.
