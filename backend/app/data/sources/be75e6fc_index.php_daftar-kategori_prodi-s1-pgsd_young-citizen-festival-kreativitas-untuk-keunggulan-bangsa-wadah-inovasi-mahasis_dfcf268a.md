Young Citizen Festival: Creativity for National Excellence, a Platform for Civics Students Innovation at UPI Cibiru Campus

 

 

Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Cibiru Campus held the Young Citizen Festival: Creativity for National Excellence as part of the Final Project of the Civics Education (PKn) Course on Wednesday, December 17, 2025. This activity was attended by 7th semester PKn Deepening class students and became a space for actualizing student creativity and innovation in the field of Civics Education with lecturers Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., MH and Dr. Agil Nanggala, M.Pd.

The Young Citizen Festival is designed to showcase students' innovative work focused on strengthening civics values. In this event, students showcase various forms of learning innovation, including educational websites , interactive digital media, visual media, and other creative learning tools aimed at fostering a contextual and engaging understanding of civics values.

A total of 24 student groups showcased their best work. Each group presented their ideas, concepts, and the excellence of their innovations, emphasizing the relevance of civics values ​​such as democracy, nationality, integrity, citizen participation, and social responsibility to everyday life. The works displayed reflected the students' ability to integrate civics theory with the needs of learning in the digital age.

Through this activity, students are not only required to understand the theoretical concepts of Civics but also to package these values ​​in creative, communicative media that are appropriate to their characteristics. The Young Citizen Festival serves as an authentic learning platform that encourages students to think critically, collaboratively, and innovatively as future educators and competitive citizens.

The event was met with high enthusiasm from all participants. Students took turns presenting their innovations, accompanied by discussions and appreciation of each piece. The interactive atmosphere of the festival reflected the spirit of collaboration and creativity in developing Civics Education relevant to the challenges of the times.

Through the Young Citizen Festival: Creativity for National Excellence, UPI Cibiru Campus hopes that students will develop professional competencies as prospective civics educators who are innovative, adaptive, and have character. This activity is expected to inspire the development of meaningful civics learning and contribute to shaping a young generation that is intelligent, critical, and has integrity for the nation's excellence.
