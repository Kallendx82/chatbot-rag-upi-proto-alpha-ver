The withdrawal of student interns from Bhinekas Kindergarten was filled with emotion and appreciation.

Bandung, December 6, 2024 – Bhinekas Kindergarten hosted a farewell ceremony for interns from the Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Program who had completed their internship at the educational institution. The event, held on Friday, was attended by supervising lecturers, teachers, and the interns. A warm and emotional atmosphere enveloped the farewell, reflecting the strong bonds forged during the internship.

In his remarks, the supervising lecturer expressed his appreciation to Bhinekas Kindergarten for the opportunity and guidance provided to the students during their internship. He also emphasized the importance of field experience as a foundation for students to become professional and competent educators.

"We are very grateful to everyone at Bhinekas Kindergarten who has supported our students. The experience here has not only enriched their knowledge but also shaped their character and prepared them to become future educators," he said.

Bhinekas Kindergarten teachers also shared their impressions and messages at the event. They praised the contributions of the student interns, who have helped with various classroom activities, from teaching to assisting the children in play and learning. "The presence of the student interns brings a new dimension to the children. They are very enthusiastic about every activity they do with the senior students," said one teacher.

The farewell moment became even more emotional as the students expressed their gratitude to their teachers for the guidance and support they received during their internship. They also shared their experiences and valuable lessons learned during their time at Bhinekas Kindergarten.

One of the interns said, "We've learned a lot here, not just about teaching, but also about understanding children in all their unique ways. Thank you for everything you've given us."

In a touching atmosphere, the event concluded with the presentation of souvenirs from the students to Bhinekas Kindergarten. The teachers and students exchanged farewells, hoping that their good relationship would continue in the future.

The withdrawal of the interns from Bhinekas Kindergarten marked a sweet conclusion to their fieldwork journey. This moment not only provided unforgettable memories but also marked the students' first step in applying the knowledge they've gained to improve early childhood education.
