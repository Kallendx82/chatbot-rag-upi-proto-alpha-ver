Information on the Submission of Grades for Odd Semester 2024/2025 for PGPAUD Lecturers at UPI Cibiru

Cibiru, January 9, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) of the Indonesian Education University Campus in Cibiru informs all lecturers that the submission of student grades for the Odd Semester of the 2024/2025 Academic Year has been scheduled for January 8 to 16, 2025.

In accordance with the Indonesian University of Education academic calendar, lecturers are requested to promptly process and upload students' final grades through the online academic system at https://siak.upi.edu/sinndo . Timely grade submission is crucial for smooth academic administration, including transcript recapitulation and student study evaluation.

The study program also reminded all lecturers to ensure:

The attendance and assessment recapitulation is complete

The final grade has gone through an objective and transparent assessment process.

There are no pending or unentered values ​​by the specified deadline.

The PGPAUD Study Program Coordinator emphasized the importance of accuracy and responsibility in the grade input process, as part of the academic commitment to the quality of education and student services.

If there are technical problems or questions regarding the grade input process, lecturers can contact the study program's academic operator team or campus administration staff.

Let us maintain the quality of academic governance through accuracy and integrity in managing student grade data.
