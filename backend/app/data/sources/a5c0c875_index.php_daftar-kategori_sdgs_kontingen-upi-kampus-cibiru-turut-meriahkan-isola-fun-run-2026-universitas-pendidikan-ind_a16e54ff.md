The UPI Cibiru Campus Contingent Participates in the 2026 Isola Fun Run. The Indonesian Education University supports the togetherness of its academic community in sportsmanship.

UPI Cibiru Campus participated in the 2026 Isola Fun Run of the Indonesian Education University as a form of support for a healthy lifestyle, sportsmanship, and strengthening togetherness of the academic community. This activity was held on Saturday, April 11, 2026, with a gathering point in the field area of ​​the FPSD Building, and was opened directly by Prof. Didi Sukyadi. The UPI Cibiru Campus contingent was attended by leaders, representatives of lecturers, and educational staff, who came carrying a spirit of solidarity and pride as part of the big UPI family.

Besides being a sporting event, this event also strengthened the image of UPI Cibiru Campus as a regional campus unit that consistently supports university programs. The impact was seen in an increased sense of belonging to the institution, a growing collective spirit, and an active and healthy campus culture. This aligns with the Isola Fun Run's goal of fostering cross-generational camaraderie among UPI's academics and alumni.

This activity also relates to SDG 3 (Healthy and Well-Being) through the promotion of physical activity, and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals) by strengthening synergy between units and alumni. Thus, participation in the Isola Fun Run is a tangible contribution from the campus to building a healthy and strong academic community.
