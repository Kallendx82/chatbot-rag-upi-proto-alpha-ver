The UPI Cibiru ABG RPL Team Becomes a GEMASTIK 2025 Finalist with its Smart ATS Innovation

The Software Engineering Study Program, Indonesia University of Education (RPL UPI) is proud to announce the achievement of the ABG Team who successfully qualified as a Finalist for GEMASTIK 2025 , one of the most prestigious national technology competitions for Indonesian students.

In this competition, the ABG Team presented an innovation titled SMART ATS (Automatic Trash Sorter) , an automatic waste sorting system based on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision developed to support the circular economy ecosystem through the implementation of Smart Bin . This technology is designed to improve the effectiveness of waste management and encourage environmentally friendly practices in the community.

This success demonstrates the ability of UPI's RPL students to integrate software engineering knowledge with applicable and socially impactful technological solutions. This achievement also reflects UPI's commitment to developing superior and innovative human resources in the field of information technology.

The UPI RPL Study Program expresses its highest appreciation to the team for their dedication and hard work. It is hoped that this achievement will motivate other students to continue creating, innovating, and actively participating in developing technological solutions that benefit society and the environment.
