UPI Campus PGPAUD in Cibiru Holds 2025 SKP Filling Meeting, Encourages Lecturer Professionalism

Cibiru, October 16, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru held a meeting to fill out the 2025 Employee Performance Targets (SKP). This meeting took place in the PGPAUD Study Program room. The event was attended by the Head of the Study Program, permanent lecturers, and education staff as part of an effort to ensure accountable and measurable performance governance.

This SKP preparation meeting serves as a crucial agenda for mapping each lecturer's performance targets in accordance with the latest regulations, while also aligning the Tridharma activities of higher education with the study program's work program for the coming year. Strengthening professionalism and improving the quality of educational services are the primary focuses of the SKP preparation.

The Head of the Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Program emphasized that the preparation of the SKP is not merely an administrative obligation but also a strategic tool for improving individual and institutional performance. "The SKP helps us ensure that every lecturer's activities align with the direction of the study program's development, student needs, and the demands of early childhood education," he said.

Aligned with the SDGs: Strengthening Governance and Quality of Education

This activity also has relevance to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), in particular:

SDG 4: Quality Education, through improving educator competency and strengthening performance planning oriented towards quality education services.

SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions, by encouraging transparent, accountable, and integrity-based higher education governance through the preparation of systematic and measurable SKP.

With the completion of this meeting, it is hoped that all PGPAUD lecturers can complete the preparation of the 2025 SKP on time and according to standards, and support the achievement of the Study Program's vision as an excellent institution in PAUD teacher education.

The activity took place smoothly, productively, and full of collaborative spirit to strengthen the performance and quality of educational services in the PGPAUD environment of the UPI Campus in Cibiru.
