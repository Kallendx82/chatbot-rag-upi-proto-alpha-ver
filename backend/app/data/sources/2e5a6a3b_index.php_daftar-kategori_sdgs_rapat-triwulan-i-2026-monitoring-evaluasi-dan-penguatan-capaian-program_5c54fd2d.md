First Quarterly Meeting 2026: Monitoring, Evaluation, and Strengthening Program Achievements

UPI Cibiru Campus held the 2026 First Quarter Meeting: Monitoring, Evaluation, and Strengthening of Program Achievements in the Hall of Building E, 1st Floor, attended by campus leaders and managers of the Integrity Zone (ZI) area. This event demonstrated UPI's commitment to strengthening governance, improving service quality, and ensuring the achievement of work programs in the first quarter of 2026.

Quarterly meetings are held as a forum for monitoring and evaluating program achievements, challenges, and follow-up actions in each work area. During these meetings, campus leaders provide guidance on strengthening inter-unit synergy and optimizing the implementation of the Integrity Zone to support effective, transparent, and accountable campus governance.

Through this monitoring and evaluation, UPI continues to foster a professional work culture that impacts the improvement of academic and non-academic services. This activity also aligns with SDG Goal 16 on Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions by strengthening high-quality, transparent, and sustainable institutions.
