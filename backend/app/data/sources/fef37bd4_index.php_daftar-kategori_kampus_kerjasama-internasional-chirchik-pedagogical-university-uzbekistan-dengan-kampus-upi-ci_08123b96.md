INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION BETWEEN CHIRCHIK PEDAGOGICAL UNIVERSITY, UZBEKISTAN AND UPI CIBIRU CAMPUS

UPI Cibiru - On Thursday, November 28, 2024, the Cibiru Campus of the Indonesian University of Education (UII) received an official visit from a delegation of approximately 10 people from Chirchik Pedagogical University, Uzbekistan. This visit was part of an effort to strengthen international relations and establish cooperation in the field of education. During the event, the delegation was warmly welcomed by the leaders and Heads of Study Programs at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, demonstrating UPI Cibiru's commitment to establishing global collaborations to improve the quality of education at the international level.

 

The purpose of this visit is to introduce the academic potential and facilities owned by UPI Cibiru Campus to Chirchik Pedagogical University, as well as to open opportunities for collaboration in various fields, especially in student exchange programs, joint research, and curriculum development that is more oriented to global needs. Through this collaboration, both parties are expected to be able to exchange knowledge and experience in the world of education, enrich academic insight, and support the improvement of the quality of graduates who have international competitiveness. Furthermore, in 2025 will send students to Uzbekistan as well as from Uzbekistan will send their students to UPI Cibiru, while lecturers from UPI Cibiru will teach in Uzbekistan and vice versa Uzbek lecturers will teach at UPI Cibiru Campus.

 

The goal of this collaboration is to build a long-term, mutually beneficial relationship, with various collaborative programs that can expand the scope of education in both countries. It is hoped that this cooperation will strengthen the friendship between Indonesia and Uzbekistan and positively impact the development of higher education at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, by enhancing the reputation and opportunities for students and faculty to develop internationally.

 

On that occasion, the Director of UPI Cibiru Campus presented souvenirs in the form of books produced by his research along with the lecturers, namely a book on Artificial Intelligence in a Bio-communication Perspective and a book on Dynamics of VCDLN. For teaching materials, a book on research methodology entitled "The Dynamics of Qualitative Research" was also handed over. Both books are in Indonesian and will be translated by the delegation from Chirchik Pedagogical University. This information regarding UPI Cibiru Campus' efforts to promote its campus towards a World Class University as mandated by UPI. (Ibud, 11/28/2024)
