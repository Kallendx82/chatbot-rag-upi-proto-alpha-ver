What is PGPAUD?

The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) study program is one of the study programs at the UPI Campus in Cibiru which was established on April 2, 2007 with a decree from the Director General of Higher Education, Ministry of National Education. The PGPAUD study program has been accredited as UNGGUL by the Independent Education Accreditation Institute (LAMDIK) since July 18, 2023-July 17, 2028, with Decree Number: 621/SK/LAMDIK/Ak/S/VIII/2023. In line with the vision of the PGPAUD Study Program that reaches far into the future, the profile of PGPAUD graduates is oriented as teachers who have additional competencies in service management and entrepreneurship in the field of PAUD and are oriented towards continuous learning.
