PT Inovindo Digital Media Officially Partners with UPI's Multimedia Education Study Program

Novi Setia Nurviat, Nurhidayatulloh, S.Pd. , MT, and Dian Rinjani, S.Pd. , M.Ds in the signing of the collaboration between PT Inovendo Digital Media and the Multimedia Education Study Program of UPI, Thursday (11/13/2025) in Bandung, West Java.

 

Bandung, November 13, 2025 – The Multimedia Education Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) has officially established a partnership with PT Inovindo Digital Media, a company specializing in digital-era business solutions. The signing ceremony took place on Friday, November 13, 2025, in the Multimedia Education Study Program meeting room.

During the event, representatives from PT Inovindo Digital Media were seen handing over the cooperation document directly to the Multimedia Education Study Program. This signing represents a concrete step in strengthening the relationship between education and the digital creative industry.

Through this collaboration, both parties are committed to developing strategic programs, including internships for students, collaboration in developing curricula based on the needs of the digital industry, organizing workshops, training, and seminars related to multimedia technology and digital marketing and collaboration in research and development of digital products to improve student competencies in the world of work.

The Multimedia Education Study Program stated that this collaboration is a crucial step in strengthening the implementation of linkages and matches between campuses and industry. Meanwhile, representatives from PT Inovindo Digital Media affirmed their readiness to support human resource development in the multimedia field through various collaborative activities.

Through this collaboration, it is hoped that students will gain real-world experience in the industry, while both parties can work together to deliver digital innovations relevant to the needs of the times. (Ibud, November 13, 2025)
