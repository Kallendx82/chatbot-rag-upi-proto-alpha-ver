Information on Submitting Final Exam Scores for Even Semester 2024/2025 for PGPAUD Lecturers

Cibiru, June 16, 2025 — Following up on the academic schedule of the Indonesian Education University (UPI), all lecturers of the Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the UPI Cibiru Campus are expected to pay attention to the Final Semester Examination (UAS) score submission schedule for the Even Semester of the 2024/2025 Academic Year for the D3, D4, S1, S2, and S3 levels. The following is complete information that needs to be considered:

Grade Checking and Entry
📅 Date: June 16 – 20, 2025
📌 By: Lecturers of each course
🌐 System: https://siak.upi.edu/sinndo

Remedial and Re-Exam
📅 Date: June 16 – 20, 2025
📍 Venue: Held in each study program

Re-examination Score Entry
📅 Date: June 20 – 26, 2025
🌐 System: https://siak.upi.edu/sinndo

Lecturers are expected to submit grades on time according to the established schedule to ensure the smooth running of the student academic process. For remedial and retake activities, coordination with the respective study programs is essential to ensure smooth implementation.

Let us work together to maintain the quality and order of academic administration for the sake of a smooth teaching and learning process and optimal student graduation.
