PGPAUD UPI Campus in Cibiru Wishes You a Happy New Year 2025!

Cibiru, January 1, 2025 — As the new year begins, the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) at the Indonesian University of Education campus in Cibiru wishes all academics, partners, alumni, students, and the wider community a Happy New Year 2025. The new year is a reflective moment to evaluate the achievements that have been achieved and plan future steps with renewed enthusiasm. PGPAUD UPI Cibiru welcomes 2025 with optimism and a commitment to continue providing quality, relevant education that has a real impact on the development of early childhood education in Indonesia. With the support of professional educators, dedicated students, and solid collaboration with various parties, PGPAUD UPI Cibiru hopes to continue to be a center for developing future educators who are superior, creative, and have character. May 2025 bring blessings, new enthusiasm, and more brilliant achievements for all of us. Let us welcome the future of education with collaboration, innovation, and a spirit of togetherness.
Happy New Year 2025!
Success and prosperity to us all.
