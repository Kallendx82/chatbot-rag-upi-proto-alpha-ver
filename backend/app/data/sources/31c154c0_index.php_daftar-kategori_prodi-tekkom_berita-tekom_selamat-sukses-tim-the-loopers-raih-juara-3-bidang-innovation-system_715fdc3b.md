Congratulations and Success! The "THE LOOPERS" Team Won Third Place in the Innovation Systems Category at INTELECTA 2025, Telkom University.

The students of the  “THE LOOPERS” Team , consisting of Dimastio Setiawan, Muhammad Azka Luthhansa, Mutiara Adhanie, and Muhammad Arizal Maulana , have once again achieved a proud achievement. This team won 3rd place in the INTELECTA 2025 competition at Telkom University in the Innovation System category .

This success was achieved through their innovative work entitled "KleroProfile: A Plant Monitoring and Profiling System with Raspberry Pi and Artificial Intelligence using Edge-Computing Methods ." This innovation offers a modern solution for real-time plant condition monitoring by utilizing AI technology and Raspberry Pi devices processed locally through edge-computing, making it more efficient, fast, and resource-efficient.

This brilliant achievement is certainly inseparable from the guidance of Dr. Eng. Munawir, S.Kom., MT , who consistently provided direction, support, and motivation to the team members so that their work could compete at the national level.

“Thank you for your dedication and hard work!” is the perfect expression to describe the team’s long struggle in developing this work.

We are very proud of the achievements of THE LOOPERS Team. We hope this success will inspire other students to continue creating, innovating, and presenting intelligent solutions for society and the future development of science and technology.
