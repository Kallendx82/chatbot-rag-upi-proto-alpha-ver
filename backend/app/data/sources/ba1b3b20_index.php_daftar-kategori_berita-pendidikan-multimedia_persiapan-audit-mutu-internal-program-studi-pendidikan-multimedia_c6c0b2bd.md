Preparation for Internal Quality Audit of Multimedia Education Study Program, UPI Cibiru

Cibiru, September 26, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru is currently preparing for an Internal Quality Audit (AMI) as part of its efforts to improve academic and managerial quality. This activity aims to ensure that all educational processes, administration, and support services within the study program are running according to established standards.

In preparation, the study program management team conducted a comprehensive evaluation of various aspects, such as the curriculum, learning systems, quality assurance, and administrative documentation. Every lecturer and staff member was actively involved to ensure the readiness of data and documents for the audit.

With thorough preparation, the Multimedia Education Study Program is optimistic that it can meet all the expected indicators in the internal quality audit. The audit results will serve as a reference in developing future study program development strategies to provide superior education for students.
