PKM Release of 2 UPI Cibiru Campus Teams: Towards Student Innovation and Creativity, in realizing SDGS 4 Quality Education, 9 Industries, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Tuesday, November 18, 2025

The UPI Cibiru campus launched two innovative applications, IRENE and SIGNOVA, which will compete in the National Student Science Week (PIMNAS). The launch event was attended by the Director of UPI Cibiru, Prof. Dr. H. Karim Suryadi, M.SI., who offered his support and best wishes to both teams and their supervisors.

IRENE and SIGNOVA are the work of UPI Cibiru students who have great potential to make a positive contribution to society. IRENE is an application designed to improve the quality of education, while SIGNOVA is an application focused on innovation and creativity.

In his remarks, the Director of UPI Cibiru stated that UPI Cibiru strongly supports student activities oriented toward innovation and creativity. "We believe that UPI Cibiru students have great potential to become future leaders and innovators," he said.

The two teams and their advisors competing in PIMNAS are expected to give their best and bring UPI Cibiru to glory. Hopefully, IRENE and SIGNOVA can serve as examples for other students to develop creative and innovative ideas.
