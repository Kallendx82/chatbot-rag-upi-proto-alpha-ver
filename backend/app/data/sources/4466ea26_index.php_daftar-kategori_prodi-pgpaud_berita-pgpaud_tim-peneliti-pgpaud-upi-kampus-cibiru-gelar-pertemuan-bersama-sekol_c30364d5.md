The UPI Cibiru Campus PGPAUD Research Team Holds a Meeting with Partner PAUD Schools to Discuss Research Implementation

Cibiru, September 4, 2025 — A research team from the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UIPI) Cibiru Campus held a meeting with representatives of partner PAUD schools to coordinate and discuss the research implementation. The meeting took place in the PGPAUD Study Program Room at UPI Cibiru Campus and was attended by research lecturers, leaders, and teachers from partner PAUD institutions.

The meeting aimed to align understandings regarding the research process, data requirements, and role allocation, as well as to ensure the research process ran smoothly and provided tangible benefits to partner schools. The research team presented the research objectives, technical implementation plan, and the potential contribution of the results to improving the quality of early childhood education services.

The partner PAUD schools welcomed this collaboration and expressed their readiness to support the research process, including teacher involvement, facilitation of data collection, and opportunities to implement research findings to improve teaching practices. The discussion was interactive, encompassing scheduling, school conditions, and strategies to maximize the research's impact in the field.

This collaborative activity aligns with SDG 4: Quality Education, specifically in efforts to improve the quality of early childhood education through partnerships between universities and early childhood education institutions. Through research based on school needs, PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Campus supports increasing educator capacity, strengthening learning practices, and ensuring equitable access to quality early childhood education. This collaboration also supports the principles of lifelong learning and continuous professional development for early childhood education teachers in Indonesia.
