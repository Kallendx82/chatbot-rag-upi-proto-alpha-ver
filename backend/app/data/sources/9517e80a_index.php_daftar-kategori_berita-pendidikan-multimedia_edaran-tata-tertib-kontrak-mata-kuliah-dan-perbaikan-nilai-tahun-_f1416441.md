Circular on Course Contract Regulations and Grade Improvements for the 2024/2025 Academic Year

Cibiru, September 10, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru has issued an official circular regarding the Course Contract Procedures and Grade Corrections for the 2024/2025 Academic Year. This circular aims to provide guidance for students so that the process of completing the IRS (Study Plan Form) and grade corrections can be carried out in an orderly manner and in accordance with academic regulations.

In the circular, there are several important points:

IRS Filling :

Students are required to complete the IRS online through the academic system on a predetermined schedule.
Students who do not complete the IRS within the specified time are deemed not to be attending lectures for the current semester.

Academic Guidance (Guardianship) :

Students are required to consult with their academic supervisor before completing the IRS.
Any changes to the course contract must be approved by the supervising lecturer.

Value Improvement :

Students who wish to improve their grades are required to submit a request for improvement in accordance with applicable procedures.
Grade corrections can only be made for courses that have been determined by the study program.

Students are expected to comply with this circular to ensure the smooth running of the academic administration process. For further information, students can contact the academic administration department or their respective supervisors.
