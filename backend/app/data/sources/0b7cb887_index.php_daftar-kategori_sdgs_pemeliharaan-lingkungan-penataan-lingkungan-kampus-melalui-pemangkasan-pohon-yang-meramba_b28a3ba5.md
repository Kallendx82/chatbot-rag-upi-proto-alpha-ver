Environmental Maintenance: Campus Environmental Management through Pruning of Trees that Grow Around the Special Needs School

UPI Cibiru Campus conducted environmental maintenance activities by pruning climbing trees around the SLB area, specifically behind the employee parking area. This activity was carried out by janitors as an effort to maintain the safety, comfort, and tidiness of the campus environment to continue supporting academic activities.

Tree pruning is carried out to prevent branches and vines from disturbing the surrounding area and to maintain the cleanliness of the campus environment. Through this activity, UPI Cibiru Campus continues to demonstrate its commitment to creating a safe, orderly, and comfortable environment for students, faculty, educational staff, and the surrounding community.

This environmental preservation activity aligns with UPI's "impact" program, which enhances the quality of campus facilities and environments, providing direct benefits to service users. Furthermore, the activity supports the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), specifically Goal 11 on Sustainable Cities and Communities and Goal 15 on Life on Land through clean and sustainable environmental management.
