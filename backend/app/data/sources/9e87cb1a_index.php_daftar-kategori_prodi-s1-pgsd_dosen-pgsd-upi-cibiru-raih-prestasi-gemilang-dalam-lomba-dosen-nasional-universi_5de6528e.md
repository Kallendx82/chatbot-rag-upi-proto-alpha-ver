UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Lecturer Achieves Brilliant Achievement in the 2025 Pelita Bangsa University National Lecturer Competition

The Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program, Faculty of Teacher Training and Education, Pelita Bangsa University, successfully held the 2025 National Lecturer Competition with the theme "Lecturer Innovation and Dedication for Quality Elementary Education." This activity aims to encourage lecturers' creativity and contribution in improving the quality of elementary education in Indonesia.

This competition is open to all active lecturers at universities in Indonesia with a National Institute of Health (NIDN) or National Institute of Health (NIDK) registration. The competition covers four categories: Scientific Articles, Learning Videos, Content Creators, and Book Chapters. The competition runs from May 29 to July 18, 2025, and registration is free.

In the competition, two PGSD lecturers (S1 and S2) from the UPI Campus in Cibiru won several categories. They are Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, S.Pd., M.Pd., MH as the first winner in the content creator competition category and Dr. Dede Trie Kurniawan, M.Pd., as the second winner in the learning video competition category and the consolation winner in the Content Creator competition category. Each winner received a certificate and prize money, while all competition participants received e-certificates as a form of appreciation.

This activity was enthusiastically received by lecturers from various universities across Indonesia. The competition served not only as a competitive platform but also as a collaborative space to share inspiration, innovation, and best practices in elementary education.

The success of the PGSD lecturers at the UPI Campus in Cibiru in winning the championship position in two categories proves the quality of the teaching resources at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, which continues to demonstrate real progress and contributions at the national level.
