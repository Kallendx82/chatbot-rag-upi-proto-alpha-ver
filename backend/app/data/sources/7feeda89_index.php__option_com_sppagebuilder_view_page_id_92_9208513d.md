Student Organization

HIMAPAUD

HIMAPAUD Cibiru is an abbreviation of the Early Childhood Education Student Association of the UPI Campus in Cibiru. In general, HIMAPAUD is a student organization at the study program level that accommodates all students of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD). This organization aims to:
1. Develop the potential, interests, and talents of PGPAUD students, both in academic, social, and creative fields.
2. Become a forum for aspirations and communication between PGPAUD students and the study program and institution.
3. Encourage active student participation in scientific development activities, community service, and character building as prospective early childhood educators.
4. Build leadership and cooperation among students.
