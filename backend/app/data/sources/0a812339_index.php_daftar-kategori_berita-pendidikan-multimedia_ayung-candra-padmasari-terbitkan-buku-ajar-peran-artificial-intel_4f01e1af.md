Ayung Candra Padmasari Publishes Textbook "The Role of Artificial Intelligence: Regulating Scientific Papers in Education"

Cibiru, August 25, 2024 — Ayung Candra Padmasari , a lecturer in the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru, has successfully published a textbook entitled "The Role of Artificial Intelligence: In Regulating the Creation of Scientific Papers in the World of Education." This book is an important contribution to understanding how artificial intelligence can influence and regulate the process of creating scientific papers in educational environments.

In this book, Ayung discusses various aspects related to the use of AI technology, including the benefits, challenges, and ethical implications that educators and students should consider. Furthermore, the book provides practical guidance on how to effectively utilize AI in research and scientific writing.

The textbook "The Role of Artificial Intelligence: In Regulating Scientific Paper Production in Education" is available at the UPI Cibiru library and will become part of the teaching materials in the Multimedia Education Study Program. Its publication is expected to stimulate further discussion and research on the role of technology in education.
