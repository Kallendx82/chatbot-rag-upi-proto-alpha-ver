Thirty-eight Students Take the August 2024 Thesis Defense Exam

Cibiru, August 24, 2024 — Thirty-eight students from the Multimedia Education Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru will take their thesis defense exam for the August 2024 period. This exam is the final stage that students must pass before earning a bachelor's degree.

The thesis defense provides an opportunity for students to present their research findings to a panel of examiners consisting of experienced lecturers. In this session, students will present various aspects of their research, including objectives, methodology, results, and conclusions.

This thesis defense also serves as a means of evaluating students' ability to communicate and explain their ideas effectively. With these skills, students are expected to be able to face the challenges of the professional world after completing their education.

The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru hopes that through this thesis defense exam, students can achieve satisfactory results and immediately continue their steps towards the world of work or further education.

Preparation of the 2024 Curriculum for the Multimedia Education Study Program

Cibiru, August 2, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru is currently developing its curriculum for the 2024 academic year. This process aims to ensure that the curriculum is relevant to industry needs, technological developments, and the ever-changing demands of the job market.

The curriculum development team, consisting of faculty and student representatives, is committed to developing high-quality study programs that are responsive to the dynamics of education and industry. In developing this curriculum, the study program will examine various aspects, including competency mapping, course alignment, and innovations in teaching and learning methods.

In addition, in developing this curriculum, the study program will also involve input from alumni and industry to gain a broader perspective on the skills and competencies required by graduates in the field.

With the systematic and planned curriculum development, the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru is committed to continuously improving the quality of education and producing competitive and innovative graduates.

Lima Mahasiswa Mengikuti Ujian Sidang Skripsi pada 18 Juli 2024

Cibiru, 19 Juli 2024 — Lima mahasiswa dari Program Studi Pendidikan Multimedia UPI Cibiru akan mengikuti ujian sidang skripsi pada 18 Juli 2024. Ujian ini merupakan langkah akhir bagi mereka untuk menyelesaikan studi sarjana dan memperoleh gelar di bidang pendidikan multimedia.

Sidang skripsi merupakan momen penting di mana mahasiswa akan mempresentasikan hasil penelitian mereka di hadapan dewan penguji yang terdiri dari dosen-dosen berkompeten. Dalam sidang ini, mahasiswa diharapkan dapat menjelaskan latar belakang penelitian, metodologi yang digunakan, serta hasil dan kesimpulan yang diperoleh dari penelitian mereka.

Proses sidang skripsi ini tidak hanya menguji kemampuan akademis mahasiswa, tetapi juga kemampuan presentasi dan komunikasi yang sangat penting dalam dunia profesional. Dengan menyampaikan ide dan hasil penelitian secara jelas, mahasiswa dapat menunjukkan pemahaman yang mendalam tentang topik yang diteliti.

Program Studi Pendidikan Multimedia UPI Cibiru berharap, dengan pelaksanaan ujian sidang skripsi ini, para mahasiswa dapat mencapai keberhasilan dan segera melanjutkan langkah mereka ke dunia kerja atau pendidikan yang lebih tinggi.

Intan Permata Sari Terbitkan Buku Ajar "Animasi Stop Motion"

Cibiru, April 14, 2024 — Intan Permata Sari , a lecturer in the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru , has published a textbook entitled "Stop Motion Animation ." This book is a comprehensive guide for students and practitioners who want to understand and master stop motion animation techniques, a creative method that combines art and technology.

In this book, Intan presents various basic animation concepts, practical steps for creating stop-motion animations, and useful tips and tricks for improving the quality of your work. With its easy-to-understand approach, this book is expected to be a useful reference for anyone interested in animation and multimedia.

With the presence of this book, it is hoped that students and other readers will be inspired to create innovative and high-quality animated works.
