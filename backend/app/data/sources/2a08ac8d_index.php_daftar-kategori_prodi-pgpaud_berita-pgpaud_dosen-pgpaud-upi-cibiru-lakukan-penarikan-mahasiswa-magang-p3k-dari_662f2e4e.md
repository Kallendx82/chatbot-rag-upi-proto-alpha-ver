UPI Cibiru PGPAUD Lecturers Withdraw First Aid Internship Students from Partner Schools

Cibiru, June 16, 2025 — Lecturers of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education Campus in Cibiru have conducted a withdrawal activity for the Introduction to the Teaching and Education Profession (P3K) intern students from various partner schools, on Monday, June 16, 2025. This withdrawal activity marked the end of a series of internship programs that the students had carried out for several weeks in the field. During the internship period, PGPAUD students were actively involved in various learning and managerial activities at PAUD institutions, accompanied by mentor teachers and field supervisors. The withdrawal was carried out directly by PGPAUD lecturers by visiting partner schools. During this activity, the lecturers also discussed and evaluated the internship process with the principal and mentor teachers, in order to obtain feedback for future improvements to the P3K program. The P3K internship program is an important part of the PGPAUD curriculum which aims to equip students with real-world experience in the workplace, strengthening pedagogical, professional, social, and personality competencies as prospective early childhood educators. The UPI Cibiru Early Childhood Education Center (PGPAUD) expresses its gratitude and appreciation to all partner schools for their cooperation and contributions in supporting the students' learning process. We hope this internship experience will be a valuable asset for the students on their journey to becoming outstanding and dedicated professional educators.
