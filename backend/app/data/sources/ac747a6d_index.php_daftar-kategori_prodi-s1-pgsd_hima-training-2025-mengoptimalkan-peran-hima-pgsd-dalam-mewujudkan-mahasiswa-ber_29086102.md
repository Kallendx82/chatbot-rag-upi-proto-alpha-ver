HIMA TRAINING 2025 'Optimizing the Role of HIMA PGSD in Creating Character-Based Students'

HIMA Training 2025 is a training activity for the Legislative Council and Executive Board of HIMA PGSD UPI Campus in Cibiru which was held at the beginning of the management. This HIMA Training activity was attended by all the Legislative Council and Executive Board administrators of HIMA PGSD UPI Campus in Cibiru. This activity was held on Friday, May 9, 2025, which took place at the 3rd floor of the Bumi Siliwangi Auditorium, Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia, campus in Cibiru. This activity is one of the annual agendas organized by the Elementary School Teacher Education Student Association (HIMA PGSD) as an effort to improve the leadership capacity, professionalism, and organizational competence of its members.

The implementation of the activity began with the arrival of the entire committee at the event location. Upon arrival at the location, the committee was immediately directed to attend a briefing session , technical preparation ( preparing ), and final checking ( final checking ). This session was guided directly by the Events Division, which ensured that all elements of the activity ran according to the rundown that had been prepared, and that all event equipment was ready to use and well-coordinated. After all technical preparations were completed, the participants began to arrive and were greeted by the Liaison Officer (LO) and the field commander (Danlap) on duty, assisted by the Public Relations Division. The participants were directed to fill out the attendance list and were orderly directed to enter the room and take their seats according to the previously designed arrangement, in order to create a conducive and organized atmosphere from the start of the activity.

The event was officially opened at 08.20 WIB by the Master of Ceremony (MC) on duty, namely Alpin Pauzi and Nilna Hawlatul Hamidah. The opening session began with the reading of the holy verses of the Qur'an and tilawah extract, followed by singing the national anthem Indonesia Raya and the UPI Hymn, and an activity report delivered by the Chairperson of the Executive Committee, Wening Larasati Dewi. The first speech was by the Chairperson of the HIMA PGSD Executive Board, M. Hanif Dhiyaulhaq, followed by the Chairperson of the HIMA PGSD Legislative Council, Moch Fitran Naufaldy, finally the foreword and opening by Mrs. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., and closed with a prayer together.

Setelah sesi pembukaan formal ditutup, acara dilanjutkan dengan kegiatan ice breaking yang dipandu oleh MC sebagai sarana interaksi ringan antar peserta dan penghangat suasana sebelum masuk ke inti acara. Suasana penuh antusiasme dan keceriaan tampak mewarnai sesi ini, yang dirancang untuk menumbuhkan semangat partisipatif. Memasuki inti kegiatan, dilakukan sesi pematerian pertama yang disampaikan oleh perwakilan dari Demisioner Dewan Legislatif HIMA PGSD, Abdul Aziz Sidiq. Sesi ini dibuka dengan pembacaan curriculum vitae (CV) pemateri oleh moderator Putri Vanda Divaura, dilanjutkan dengan pemaparan materi yang berkaitan dengan pengembangan kapasitas internal organisasi dan kaderisasi mahasiswa. Setelah sesi materi selesai, moderator menutup sesi pertama dan MC kembali memandu ice breaking sebagai transisi menuju sesi berikutnya.

Kegiatan kemudian berlanjut ke sesi pematerian kedua, yang kali ini menghadirkan pemateri dari Demisioner Badan Eksekutif HIMA PGSD, Aceng Fahmi Idris. Sesi ini dibuka oleh moderator Marisa Ayu Sundari yang memperkenalkan latar belakang pemateri. Materi yang disampaikan mencakup wawasan kepemimpinan, dinamika organisasi, serta tantangan dan strategi dalam menjalankan roda organisasi mahasiswa. Sesi ini ditutup dengan sesi tanya jawab interaktif yang memberikan ruang bagi peserta untuk menyampaikan pertanyaan, pandangan, serta berdiskusi secara langsung dengan pemateri. Usai sesi pematerian kedua, kegiatan dilanjutkan dengan penyerahan sertifikat penghargaan kepada para pemateri dan moderator sebagai bentuk apresiasi atas kontribusi mereka dalam kegiatan ini. Penyerahan dilakukan secara simbolis oleh Ketua Pelaksana, Ketua Badan Eksekutif, serta Ketua Dewan Legislatif, dan dilanjutkan dengan sesi dokumentasi foto bersama yang dibantu oleh Divisi Publikasi, Dokumentasi, dan Desain (PDD).

Sebagai penutup rangkaian acara, MC secara resmi menutup kegiatan HIMA TRAINING 2025, yang kemudian diikuti oleh kegiatan clean area oleh seluruh panitia sebagai bentuk tanggung jawab kolektif dalam menjaga kebersihan dan ketertiban tempat kegiatan. Dengan demikian, kegiatan HIMA TRAINING 2025 berjalan dengan lancar, tertib, dan penuh semangat kolaboratif. Harapannya, kegiatan ini mampu memberikan nilai tambah bagi peserta dalam aspek pengetahuan, pengalaman organisasi, serta memperkuat solidaritas dan semangat kepemimpinan di kalangan mahasiswa PGSD.
