UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Students Win Gold Medals at the National Airlangga Competition 12: Realizing SDGs 4

Bandung, 2025 — Another achievement was achieved by a student of the Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Cibiru Campus. Fitria Ichsan Eka Putri won a gold medal at the 12th national-level Airlangga Competition, a prestigious academic competition organized by PT. Lentera Pendidikan Indonesia on November 16, 2025.

Fitria, who participated in the Indonesian language competition, was named a Gold Medalist after a rigorous selection process alongside participants from various universities across Indonesia. Her final grade of A+ confirmed her superior academic quality and literacy skills.

Participants' Impressions and Statements

After being announced as the gold medalist, Fitria expressed her gratitude and hopes:

"I feel very grateful and proud to have won a gold medal in this competition. The process wasn't easy, but consistent effort and training ultimately paid off. I hope this achievement will inspire others to continue to try and develop their best abilities," said Fitria.

The Meaning of Achievement for PGSD UPI Cibiru

This achievement is a testament to the quality of learning at UPI Cibiru's Elementary School Teacher Education Program, which emphasizes strengthening students' literacy, creativity, and academic competence. National competitions like the Airlangga Competition provide a strategic opportunity for students to hone their potential, compete healthily, and prepare themselves as intelligent and visionary future educators.

Fitria's achievements also strengthen the commitment of PGSD UPI Cibiru in producing superior graduates and supporting the achievement of SDG 4 (Quality Education) by improving students' academic competencies in various fields.

With this success, Fitria Ichsan Eka Putri is expected to continue to work and inspire, not only for PGSD UPI Cibiru students, but also for the young generation of Indonesia who are committed to excellence and academic achievement.
