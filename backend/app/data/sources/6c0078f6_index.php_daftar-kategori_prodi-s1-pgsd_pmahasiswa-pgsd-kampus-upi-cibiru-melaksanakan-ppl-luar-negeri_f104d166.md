Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) students at UPI Cibiru Campus carry out overseas internships

UPI Cibiru Campus Elementary School Teacher Training students participated in the Overseas PPL activity held at the PERMAI Guidance Center in Penang and Kulim, Malaysia, on May 6–June 3, 2025. This activity was attended by 5 students and lasted for one month with various learning programs and character building for students. This PPL is part of the effort to develop the professional competencies of prospective teachers as well as a means of cross-cultural learning.
