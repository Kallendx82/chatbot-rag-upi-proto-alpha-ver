Strengthening Family: Regular Meetings of IIK UPI Cibiru Campus Support Harmony of the Academic Community in Strengthening Solidarity

(IIK) UPI Cibiru Campus held a regular meeting at Cawalaloka Building A, 2nd Floor, to strengthen solidarity and harmony among members. The event was attended by IIK Chair Ibutusti Setiwati, IIK Vice Chair Ibu Fuji, and all IIK UPI Cibiru Campus members. The routine meeting served as a forum for fostering camaraderie and strengthening a sense of family within the academic community.

During the event, Ms. Fuji shared her experiences and insights from her time in Japan, inspiring and motivating the members. She also presented souvenirs to all IIK members as a token of her appreciation and camaraderie. The warm and friendly atmosphere of the event further strengthened the bonds between IIK members.

This activity aligns with UPI's "impact" program, which strengthens social and cultural ties, benefiting the academic community. Furthermore, the activity supports the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly Goal 3 on Good Health and Well-being, by creating a healthy, harmonious social environment that supports shared prosperity.
