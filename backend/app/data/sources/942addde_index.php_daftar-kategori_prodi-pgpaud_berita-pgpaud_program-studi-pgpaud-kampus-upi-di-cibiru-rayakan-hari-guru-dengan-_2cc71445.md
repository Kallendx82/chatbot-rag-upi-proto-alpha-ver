The PGPAUD Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru Celebrates Teachers' Day with Festive Celebrations

Cibiru, November 25, 2024 – The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) of the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Campus in Cibiru celebrated Teacher's Day 2024 with joy and warmth on November 25, 2024. This special moment was marked by offerings from students and giving gifts as a form of appreciation to the lecturers who have guided them in the educational process.

The event began with a creative performance by PGPAUD students, who presented various artistic performances, including poetry, songs, and dances, focusing on the theme of appreciation for teachers. The students expressed their gratitude and appreciation for their lecturers' dedication to educating and inspiring them to become professional educators in the future.

As a token of gratitude, the students also presented symbolic gifts to the lecturers of the Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Program. "Teachers' Day is the perfect occasion to express our gratitude to the lecturers who have provided us with knowledge and provided extraordinary examples. We hope this can be a small token of our appreciation for their hard work," said one student representative.

This Teachers' Day celebration also serves as a moment to reflect on the crucial role of teachers in educating the nation. The Early Childhood Education Program (PGPAUD) at the UPI Campus in Cibiru is committed to continuing to produce professional teachers who can make a positive contribution to the development of early childhood education in Indonesia.
