Multimedia Education Journal (EDSENCE) Vol 6 No 1 Has Been Published

Cibiru, August 3, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru proudly announces the official publication of the Multimedia Education Journal (EDSENCE) Volume 6 No. 1. This latest edition presents a variety of quality scientific articles, research results, and studies relevant to the development of multimedia education.

The EDSENCE Journal is a publication platform for researchers, lecturers, and students to share knowledge and innovation in multimedia education. Every published article undergoes a rigorous review process to ensure quality and contribution to the field.

The latest edition of the EDSENCE Journal can be accessed online through the official website of the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru. Readers are encouraged to provide feedback and suggestions to improve the quality of future publications.

With the publication of EDSENCE Journal Vol 6 No 1, the Multimedia Education Study Program of UPI Cibiru continues to be committed to supporting the development of science and technology in education.
