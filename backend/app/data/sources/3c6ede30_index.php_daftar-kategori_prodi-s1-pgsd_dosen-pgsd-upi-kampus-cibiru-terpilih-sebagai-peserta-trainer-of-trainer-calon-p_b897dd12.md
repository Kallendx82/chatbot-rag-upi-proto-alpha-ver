UPI Cibiru Campus PGSD Lecturer Selected as Trainer of Trainer Participant for Prospective Teachers of PTP Training with Main Qualification in 2025

Proud news comes from the UPI Cibiru Campus Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program. One of its best lecturers, Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, S.Pd., M.Pd., was selected as a Trainer of Trainers (ToT) participant for the Prospective Teacher Training for the Main Qualification of PTP Class of 2025, which was held in Jakarta on July 8–10, 2025.

This ToT activity is part of a national training program that aims to prepare prospective PTP (Professional Education and Training) teachers with primary qualifications, to ensure the quality of training provided to educators throughout Indonesia.

Dr. Dinie's participation in this ToT is not only a personal achievement but also a source of pride for UPI Cibiru Campus, particularly the Elementary School Teacher Education Program. This demonstrates the institution's commitment to encouraging its lecturers to contribute to the development of national education quality.

The leadership of UPI Cibiru Campus and the entire academic community expressed their appreciation and support for this achievement. Going forward, it is hoped that the results of this training will directly contribute to improving the quality of teacher training in Indonesia and strengthen UPI's role in building superior and impactful education.
