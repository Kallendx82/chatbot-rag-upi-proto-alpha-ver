Congratulations on your return to duty: Chair and Secretary of the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program for the 2025-2030 period

The Indonesian Education University (UPI) officially inaugurated a number of structural officials for the 2025–2030 term. One of those inaugurated was Dr. Tita Mulyati, M.Pd., as Head of the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program for undergraduate and postgraduate levels and Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., MH as Secretary of the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program. The inauguration of UPI structural officials for the 2025–2030 period was held at the UPI Gymnasium Building, Jl. Dr. Setiabudhi, No. 229 Bandung, West Java, on Wednesday (20/8/25) and was attended by university leaders, lecturers, and representatives of the academic community.

In her remarks, Dr. Tita Mulyati, M.Pd., expressed her gratitude and the immense responsibility she carries in this new mandate. "This inauguration is not just a position, but also a commitment to further advance and excel the UPI Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program, both in terms of educational quality, research, and community service. I hope all lecturers and students can collaborate in realizing this grand vision," she said.

With the inauguration and confirmation of the new chair and secretary of the study program, it is hoped that the UPI PGSD Study Program can further strengthen its role as a leading study program that produces professional, globally competitive, and nationally characterized elementary school teachers.
