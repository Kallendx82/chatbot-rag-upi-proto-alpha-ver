Elementary School Teacher Education Students at UPI Campus in Cibiru Achieve West Java Provincial Level Achievements

Another proud achievement was achieved by a student at the Cibiru Campus of the Indonesian Education University (UPI). Destri Maryam Suci Mawati, a fifth-semester Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) student, won first place in the West Java-wide Bandung Open Pencak Silat Championship in the women's category.

This victory is not only a personal source of pride for Destri Maryam, but also for the entire UPI Cibiru campus community. This achievement demonstrates that UPI students excel not only academically but also in non-academic areas, particularly in traditional Indonesian martial arts.

The campus expressed its highest appreciation for the achievement. "We hope this achievement will inspire other students to continue striving, achieving success, and bringing honor to their alma mater," said a representative of the campus leadership.

With this achievement, it is hoped that Destri Maryam will continue to develop her abilities and maintain her fighting spirit, so that she is able to make a bigger contribution to the world of sports and education in the future.
