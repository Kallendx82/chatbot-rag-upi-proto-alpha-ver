Multimedia Education Alumni Reunion: Igniting the Spirit of Creativity and Collaboration in Realizing SDG 4 Quality Education.

The UPI Cibiru Multimedia Education Alumni Association (IEA) was officially established on Sunday, November 16, 2025, in the Hall of Building E, UPI Cibiru Campus, via Zoom. The event was attended by alumni from various classes and members of the Multimedia Education Study Program.

The meeting was chaired by Kiki Saepul Anam, S.Pd., and Azahra Putri Denytha P., S.Pd., as the meeting's secretary. The meeting began with the ratification of the meeting's rules of procedure, followed by a discussion of the Articles of Association (AD) and Bylaws (ART) as the basis for establishing the alumni organization.

At the plenary session, the forum agreed on the name of the alumni organization, KITAPEDIA (Family of the Multimedia Education Alumni Association). This name, previously proposed by several participants, received full support as it was deemed more solid, memorable, and reflected the togetherness of the alumni.

Discussions on the Articles of Association (AD/ART) proceeded dynamically. The forum ratified all chapters of the Articles of Association, principles and objectives, membership, code of ethics, and provisions for dissolution of the organization. Several important changes were proposed by participants, including refining the name of the Research and Development Division to encompass both education and industry, removing the basic colors of the organization's flag, establishing a digital Membership Card (KTA), and confirming alumni membership through an application system.

 

After the Articles of Association (AD/ART) were ratified, the session moved on to electing the KITAPEDIA Chairperson. Based on the previously compiled list of chairperson candidates, the forum agreed on two candidates and appointed Kang Farhan as Chairperson and Kang Furqaan as Vice Chairperson. The formation of the formators has not yet been determined at this session and will be formulated by the elected chairperson and vice chairperson.

With the ratification of the Articles of Association and the election of the first board, KITAPEDIA was officially established as a forum for Multimedia Education alumni from UPI Cibiru. This organization is expected to provide a space for collaboration, career development, and strengthening bonds between alumni and relationships with study programs.
