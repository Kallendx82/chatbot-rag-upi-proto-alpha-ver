ADJUSTMENT OF UPI CIBIRU CAMPUS LIBRARY SERVICES

ANNOUNCEMENT - UPI Library, UPI Cibiru Campus, will adjust its services from March 25-27, 2026. During this period, all services will be switched to an online platform. Users can continue to access library information through the website: https://kd-cibiru.upi.edu/index.php/perpustakaan-upi-cibiru  or contact the UPI Cibiru Campus Library via WhatsApp and social media.
