The UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Masters Program will conduct an Internal Quality Audit (AMI) in 2025.

Bandung, October 9, 2025 – The Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Master's Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Cibiru Campus conducted its 2025 Internal Quality Audit (AMI) on Wednesday, October 9, 2025, in the Cawala Loka Room, Building A, UPI Campus in Cibiru. The event began at 8:00 a.m. and continued until completion.

The implementation of the AMI was attended by the Head of the PGSD Masters Study Program, Dr. Tita Mulyati, M.Pd., Secretary of the Study Program, Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., MH, and the AMI S2 PGSD Team. The audit was carried out by two assessors, namely Prof. Dr. Dedy Achmad Kurniady, M.Pd. and Prof. Dr. Wiji, M.Si., who were appointed by the UPI Quality Assurance Institute (LPM).

AMI activities aim to ensure that internal quality assurance systems at the study program level operate optimally and sustainably. In the process, auditors review various aspects of academic administration, from the curriculum and learning process to research and publications, to study program governance.

The Head of the Elementary School Teacher Education Master's Program, Dr. Tita Mulyati, M.Pd., stated that AMI is a crucial part of the continuous quality improvement cycle. "Through this audit, we can reflect on and improve all academic aspects, so that the quality of education at the Elementary School Teacher Education Master's Program at UPI Cibiru continues to improve and is relevant to the needs of the educational world," she said.

Meanwhile, Prof. Dr. Dedy Achmad Kurniady, M.Pd., as assessor, appreciated the readiness and commitment of the PGSD Master's Program team in implementing the AMI. "This study program has demonstrated seriousness in maintaining academic quality and good governance. We hope that this audit result will serve as a strong foundation for sustainable development," he said.

The implementation of AMI 2025 in the PGSD Masters Study Program at UPI Cibiru is an important momentum to strengthen the culture of quality, increase accountability, and ensure that all academic and non-academic processes run in accordance with UPI's quality assurance standards.
