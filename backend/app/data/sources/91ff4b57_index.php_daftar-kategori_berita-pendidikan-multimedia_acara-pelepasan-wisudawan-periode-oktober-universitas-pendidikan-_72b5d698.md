Graduation Ceremony for the October Period of the Indonesian Education University, in Realizing SDGS 4

Tuesday, October 7, 2025

The UPI Cibiru campus will host a grand graduation ceremony on October 13th and 14th, 2025, at UPI Setiabudi. This event marks a significant moment for the graduates, who have completed their studies and are ready to step out into the wider world.

To prepare for this graduation ceremony, the UPI Cibiru campus has established a graduation ceremony committee to ensure the smooth running and success of the event. The graduation ceremony committee will work diligently to ensure that all aspects of the ceremony run smoothly and according to plan.

This graduation ceremony will be an unforgettable moment for the graduates, their families, and friends. Therefore, the UPI Cibiru graduation committee hopes to provide the best possible experience for everyone involved.
