Inauguration of BEM and DPM UPI Cibiru Campus 2026: A New Milestone for the Organization in Strengthening Visionary and Impactful Student Leadership

UPI Cibiru Campus affirmed its commitment to building a progressive student ecosystem through the Inauguration of BEM and DPM for the 2026 period at the Gedung Baru Field . This activity was led directly by the Deputy Director for Education, Student Affairs, and Quality Assurance, Dr. Eng. Munawir, MT , and attended by representatives of educational staff as witnesses and the Chair of the previous BEM period. This momentum is a strategic step for the campus in preparing the regeneration of student leadership that is visionary, has integrity, and is ready to present impactful work programs.

As part of student institutional development, the campus ensures that the leadership transition process is orderly, democratic, and educational. This inauguration not only marks the handover of authority but also strengthens students' capacity in organizational governance, decision-making, and collaborative culture.

This activity also supports SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 16 (Resilient Institutions) by strengthening leadership, democratic, accountable, and integrity-based organizational governance.

Through this inauguration, UPI Cibiru Campus hopes to produce active, innovative, visionary, and collaborative student leadership, capable of bringing good name to the campus at the local and national levels.
