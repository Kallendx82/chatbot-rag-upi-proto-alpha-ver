PGPAUD students at the UPI Campus in Cibiru were released for teaching internships at the Indonesian School in Kuala Lumpur.

Bandung, November 25, 2024 – The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Campus in Cibiru held a farewell ceremony for students who will undertake the Teaching Internship Program (PPL) at the Indonesian School Kuala Lumpur (SIKL), Malaysia. This activity is part of the implementation of the Merdeka Belajar Kampus Merdeka (MBKM) program, which provides international experience to students in teaching and managing education abroad. The Indonesian School Kuala Lumpur (SIKL) was chosen as a partner in this program because it is known as an educational institution that focuses on implementing national values ​​for Indonesian citizen (WNI) children in Malaysia. The students will undergo teaching practice and explore the curriculum implemented at SIKL during the program.

One of the students participating in the program expressed his enthusiasm. "This is our first experience teaching abroad. We hope to learn a lot from the education system at SIKL and make the best possible contribution to the children there," he said enthusiastically.

Through this internship program, PGPAUD students at the UPI Campus in Cibiru are expected to strengthen their competencies in carrying out teaching practices according to needs, so that after graduation they are expected to be able to become professional teachers.
