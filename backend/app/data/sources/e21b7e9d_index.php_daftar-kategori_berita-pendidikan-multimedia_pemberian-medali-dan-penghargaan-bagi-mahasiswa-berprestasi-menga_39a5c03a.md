Awarding Medals and Awards to Outstanding Students: Appreciating Excellence in Realizing SDG 4: Quality Education

Tuesday, October 17, 2025

The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Cibiru held a medal and award ceremony for outstanding students in both academic and non-academic fields. The event aimed to recognize the students' brilliance and dedication in achieving outstanding achievements.

UPI Cibiru Campus Director, Prof. Dr. H. Karim Suryadi, M.Sc., personally presented the awards to the outstanding students. In his remarks, he stated that the awards were a token of the institution's appreciation for the students' hard work and dedication.

"Outstanding students are a valuable asset for UPI Cibiru. We are proud to have students who are not only brilliant in academics, but also active in non-academic activities. Hopefully, this award can motivate other students to achieve higher achievements," said Prof. Dr. H. Karim Suryadi, M.Si.

The award-winning students also expressed their gratitude to UPI Cibiru for the opportunity. They hoped this award would motivate other students to continue achieving and bringing honor to UPI Cibiru.
