UPI Cibiru PGPAUD Participates in Aspiration Activities and Joint Meetings with the Campus Senate

Cibiru, December 4, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru participated in the Aspirations and Joint Senate Meeting, a strategic forum held to strengthen academic governance and ensure alignment with the institution's development direction. This meeting provided a crucial dialogue space for study programs to share input, needs, and ideas related to improving the quality of education.

During the event, PGPAUD representatives actively expressed their concerns regarding various aspects, ranging from strengthening the curriculum based on field needs, developing academic services for students, increasing support for Tridharma activities, to strategies for meeting accreditation standards and quality standards for study programs. The discussion was constructive, demonstrating a strong commitment between the Campus Senate and study programs to developing adaptive and responsive higher education.

This activity also serves as a momentum to strengthen internal communication between study programs and the Senate, while ensuring that the decision-making process is carried out in a participatory, transparent manner, and in line with the institution's vision.

Contribution to SDGs

PGPAUD's participation in this aspiration meeting also supports several sustainable development goals, especially:

SDG 4: Quality Education, through encouraging curriculum improvement, improving academic services, and strengthening the quality of early childhood education.

SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions, because this activity reflects participatory, transparent, and inclusive higher education governance.

SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals, through internal collaboration that strengthens cross-unit synergy in achieving common goals.

By attending this forum, PGPAUD UPI Cibiru affirmed its commitment to continue contributing to the development of strong governance, as well as improving the quality of education that has an impact on society and the world of early childhood education.
