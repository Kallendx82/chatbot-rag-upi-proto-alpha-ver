Vision

To become a pioneering and superior study program in the development of educational science in Multimedia expertise competencies in Indonesia by 2028.

Mission

1. Organizing educational and teaching services in the field of expertise in Multimedia education to produce graduates who have high academic abilities, are creative, innovative,

2. Implement research programs to support high-quality learning processes and contribute to the development of science, applications, and multimedia-based products.

3. Utilizing multimedia knowledge and products in carrying out community service based on research results that can contribute to advancing a science and technology-based society.

4. Develop, implement and disseminate technology in the field of Multimedia based on the needs of society, the business world and industry.

What is Multimedia Education?

Multimedia Education Study Program of Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia UPI Cibiru Campus was officially established on April 2, 2018, with the Decree of the Rector of Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia after the application form for the new study program was officially accepted and received minimum accreditation from the Ministry of Research, Technology and Higher Education on March 28, 2018, Multimedia Education Study Program of Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia aims to be a pioneer educator who produces graduates who can apply knowledge and skills in the field of education and multimedia in facing the very rapid technological advances in the current digital era.

EXPERTISE GROUP
DESIGN

The multimedia design expertise group is a field that studies the concepts, techniques, and application of digital technology-based design which includes graphics, animation, audio, video, and interactivity to produce modern visual communication media that are attractive, informative, and interactive.

DIGITAL MEDIA

The Digital Media and Multimedia Expertise Group is an expertise area that focuses on the development and application of digital technology in the creation, processing, and presentation of various forms of visual, audio, text, and interactive media for communication, education, and entertainment purposes.

COMPUTING

Komputasi Multimedia adalah rumpun keilmuan yang berfokus pada penggabungan antara teknologi komputasi dan media digital untuk merancang, mengembangkan, serta mengelola sistem dan konten multimedia yang interaktif, informatif, dan inovatif.

ANIMASI

Kelompok bidang keahlian Animasi dan Multimedia merupakan bidang yang mempelajari konsep, teknik, dan penerapan teknologi digital dalam pembuatan animasi 2D dan 3D, efek visual, serta media multimedia interaktif.

BROADCASTING

Broadcastingmerupakan bidang yang mempelajari konsep, teknik, dan penerapan teknologi dalam proses produksi siaran, meliputi penulisan naskah, pengambilan gambar, penyuntingan video dan audio, hingga distribusi konten melalui media platform digital seperti YouTube, podcast, dan media sosial.
