Congratulations and Success! Computer Engineering Students from UPI Cibiru Campus Selected as Recipients of the 2025/2026 Djarum Scholarship Plus

UPI Cibiru Campus has once again achieved a proud achievement. We congratulate and wish success to Muhammad Zaki Fadilah and Nur Adi Supriyantomo , students of the Computer Engineering Study Program , who have been successfully selected as Recipients of the Djarum Beasiswa Plus (Djarum Beasiswa Plus) for the 2025/2026 Academic Year . The selection of Zaki and Adi through a rigorous selection process demonstrates the quality, dedication, and great potential that both of them have, both in academics and self-development activities. Djarum Beasiswa Plus is known as one of the prestigious scholarship programs in Indonesia that not only provides financial support, but also soft skills development, leadership training, and various character development activities. Once again, congratulations to Muhammad Zaki Fadilah and Nur Adi Supriyantomo! May this achievement be the first step towards a bright future.

The news has been linked to the SDGs:

DG 4 – Quality Education

The Djarum Plus Scholarship Program provides access to higher-quality education through financial support and competency development. This reinforces our commitment to improving the quality of higher education, particularly in developing 21st-century skills needed in the workplace.

SDG 8 – Decent Work and Economic Growth

Providing soft skills and leadership training helps produce human resources who are ready to compete in the world of work, are productive, and able to contribute to sustainable economic growth.

SDG 10 – Reducing Inequality

This scholarship provides opportunities for high-achieving students from diverse backgrounds to receive quality education and training without financial constraints. This helps reduce disparities in access to education and career opportunities.

SDG 17 – Partnerships to Achieve the Goals

Collaboration between universities, scholarship institutions, the industrial world, and students is a concrete manifestation of multi-stakeholder partnerships to support the development of superior human resources in Indonesia.
