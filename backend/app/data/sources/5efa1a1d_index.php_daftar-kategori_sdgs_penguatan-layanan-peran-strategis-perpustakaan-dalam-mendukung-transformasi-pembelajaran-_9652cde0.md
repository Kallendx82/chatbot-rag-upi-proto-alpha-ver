Strengthening Services: The Strategic Role of Libraries in Supporting Learning Transformation at UPI Cibiru Campus

Strengthening the quality of academic services by optimizing the library's function as a center for student literacy and learning. Located on the 4th floor of the New Building library, two library service managers, Dadang and Mamat Rupawan, demonstrated their commitment to providing friendly, responsive service and supporting the academic needs of the academic community. The presence of optimal library services is an important part of supporting the transformation of learning on campus. The library functions not only as a place to store book collections, but also as a space for study, discussion, and student literacy development. Through active and professional service, students gain easy access to learning resources, academic references, and other learning support facilities.

The library management's commitment to providing excellent service has a positive impact on student comfort and productivity during academic activities. UPI, through strengthening library services, continues to encourage the creation of a culture of literacy and continuous learning that can improve the quality of human resources on campus. This activity is also in line with UPI's "impact" program through improving academic services that provide direct benefits to students. Furthermore, strengthening library services supports the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly Goal 4 on Quality Education by providing inclusive and quality access to education and learning resources.
