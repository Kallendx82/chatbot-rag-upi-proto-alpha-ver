Coordination of Initial Preparations for Odd Semester Lectures for the 2024-2025 Academic Year

Cibiru, August 15, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru held a coordination meeting for initial lecture preparations for the odd semester of the 2024-2025 academic year. This activity aims to ensure that all academic and administrative aspects are ready before classes begin.

During this coordination meeting, lecturers and study program managers discussed various matters related to the curriculum, class schedules, and the readiness of facilities and infrastructure. The agenda also included discussions on the teaching methods to be implemented and an evaluation of the previous semester for improvements in the upcoming semester.

On this occasion, it is hoped that every lecturer can contribute to creating an interactive and innovative learning atmosphere, as well as improving the quality of education in the Multimedia Education Study Program.

With this coordination, the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru is committed to providing the best learning experience for its students in the 2024-2025 academic year.
