UPI Cibiru - Assistance in Completing the Evaluation Worksheet (LKE) of the UPI Integrity Zone – Towards WBK/WBBM. Friday, November 14, 2025 – The Integrity Zone (ZI) Team of the UPI Campus in Cibiru received an invitation to participate in Assistance in Completing the Evaluation Worksheet (LKE) of the Integrity Zone towards a Corruption-Free Area (WBK) and a Clean and Serving Bureaucracy Area (WBBM) . This activity is part of the effort to strengthen bureaucratic reform within the Indonesian University of Education.

In this activity, the UPI Campus in Cibiru sent 12 representatives who were divided into six areas of Integrity Zone development, namely:

The mentoring activity took place in the Auditorium of the New Postgraduate Building, 7th Floor, Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) . The event began at 8:00 a.m. and ended at 4:00 p.m. , with representatives from all ZI areas across UPI's work units in attendance.
