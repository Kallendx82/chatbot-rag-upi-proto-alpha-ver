Welcome to the Indonesian Education University Cibiru Campus

As nearly every aspect of civilization is controlled by smart technology, the need for character education (morals) is growing. The Cibiru Campus exists as a technologically adaptive, socially beneficial ,  open learning ecosystem . We not only provide knowledge and technology, but also strengthen character and uphold religious, cultural, and humanitarian values. 

With the support of global research and collaboration, in the moral anchor and national identity, UPI Cibiru Campus is committed to sowing the seeds of human potential, cultivating good talents for the birth of a superior generation that is ready to answer challenges, and contribute to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Director of UPI Campus in Cibiru
ABOUT US

UPI Cibiru Campus plays a role as an academic implementing element that organizes the Tridharma of Higher Education. Currently, UPI Cibiru Campus has seven study programs, namely the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program (PGSD), Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD), Multimedia Education Study Program, Software Engineering Study Program, Computer Engineering Study Program, Elementary School Teacher Professional Education Program (PPG), and the Postgraduate Program S2 PGSD. Several leading study programs, such as PGSD and PGPAUD, have achieved A accreditation from BAN-PT, thus strengthening the campus's position as a center for education and development of quality educators.
