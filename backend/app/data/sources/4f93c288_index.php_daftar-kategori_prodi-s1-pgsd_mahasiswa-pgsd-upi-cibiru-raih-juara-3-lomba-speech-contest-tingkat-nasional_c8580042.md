UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Students Win Third Place in National Speech Contest

Bandung – Another proud achievement was achieved by a student at the Cibiru Campus of the Indonesian Education University (UPI). Azhar Pangestu, a third-semester Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) student with the student ID number 2403235, won third place in the National Speech Contest.

This competition was part of the Language Festival organized by UIN Sunan Kalijaga, Yogyakarta. In the competition, Azhar delivered a speech titled "Language is a Bridge for Our Growth," emphasizing the importance of language as a means of building bridges of growth, collaboration, and cross-cultural understanding.

This achievement is proof of the quality and competitiveness of UPI Cibiru PGSD students at the national level, while also demonstrating the students' potential in the field of communication and language skills.

The study program expressed its highest appreciation for Azhar's achievement. "We are proud of Azhar's accomplishments. We hope this achievement will inspire other students to continue working and competing at the national and international levels," said Winti Ananthia, Azhar's advisor and academic mentor. 

With this achievement, Azhar not only brings good name to his personal life, but also brings honor to his alma mater, UPI Cibiru Campus, through his contributions in the academic field and language competitions.
