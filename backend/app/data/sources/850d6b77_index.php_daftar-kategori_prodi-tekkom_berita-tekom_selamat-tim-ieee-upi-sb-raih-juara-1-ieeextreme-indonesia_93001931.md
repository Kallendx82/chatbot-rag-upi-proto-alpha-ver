Congratulations! The IEEE UPI SB Team Wins First Place at IEEEXtreme Indonesia

Bandung,  – Proud news has arrived from the UPI Dictionary in Cibiru. The team representing the IEEE UPI Student Branch (SB), known as TRIOSTRONG, achieved a brilliant achievement by winning first place in the IEEEXtreme 19.0 Programming Competition for the Indonesian Section.

The IEEEXtreme 19.0 marathon programming competition is a prestigious annual event that challenges students from around the world to solve a series of complex programming problems in 24 hours non-stop.

The TRIOSTRONG team, consisting of three high-achieving students—Ghayrie Habsya Alghifary, Yaris Akbar Rivaldi, and Nur Adi Supriyantomo—showed an outstanding performance. Their solid teamwork and mental resilience in facing challenges for 24 hours led them to the top ranking in Indonesia (Rank 1).

To further enhance our pride, one of the team members, Nur Adi Supriyantomo, is an active student in the Computer Engineering Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus. This achievement is a clear testament to the quality of education and the extraordinary potential of UPI students, particularly in the fields of computer engineering and programming.

This achievement not only brings honor to UPI on the national stage but also demonstrates the capacity of Indonesian students to compete in global technology competitions. Congratulations to the TRIOSTRONG Team and the entire IEEE UPI SB team for this inspiring achievement!
