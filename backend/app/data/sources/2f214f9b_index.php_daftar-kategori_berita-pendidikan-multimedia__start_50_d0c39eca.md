25 Multimedia Students Take Proposal Seminar Exam on February 15-16, 2024

Cibiru, February 17, 2024 — Twenty-five students from the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru participated in a proposal seminar exam held on February 15-16, 2024. This activity is an important part of the academic process before students proceed to the thesis research stage.

The proposal seminar exam is held to present the research plans developed by each student to the examining lecturer. Each student is given the opportunity to explain the research objectives, the methodology to be used, and the expected contribution of the research.

Over the two days of the program, students presented on a variety of topics, including innovations in learning media, multimedia application development, and research on the impact of technology on learning. Examiners provided constructive feedback to help students refine their proposals before moving on to more in-depth research.

By successfully completing this proposal seminar exam, students are expected to be well-prepared for the research process that will form part of their thesis. The Multimedia Education Study Program remains committed to supporting student academic and research development as part of its efforts to produce quality graduates.

Agus Juhana Publishes Textbook "Algorithms and Basic Programming Using C++"

Cibiru, January 30, 2024 — Agus Juhana, a lecturer in the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru, has just launched a textbook entitled "Basic Algorithms and Programming Using C++ ." This book is intended to help students and general readers understand the basic concepts of algorithms and programming using the C++ programming language.

This textbook is based on Agus Juhana's experience teaching programming courses at universities, as well as various trusted references and sources in the field. In this book, Agus explains in detail the fundamentals of algorithms, data structures, and programming techniques needed to build simple applications.

Peluncuran buku ini mendapat sambutan positif dari kalangan mahasiswa dan dosen di Program Studi Pendidikan Multimedia. Dengan terbitnya buku ajar ini, Agus Juhana berharap dapat memberikan kontribusi yang signifikan dalam pengembangan pendidikan di bidang pemrograman dan teknologi informasi.

Perawatan Rutin Laboratorium Pendidikan Multimedia dalam Rangka Menyambut Semester Baru

Cibiru, 15 Januari 2024 — Dalam rangka menyambut semester genap tahun akademik 2023-2024, Program Studi Pendidikan Multimedia UPI Cibiru melaksanakan perawatan rutin laboratorium untuk memastikan fasilitas laboratorium siap digunakan oleh mahasiswa dan dosen dalam kegiatan praktikum. Perawatan ini meliputi pengecekan perangkat keras, pembaruan perangkat lunak, serta perbaikan sarana pendukung lainnya.

Tim teknisi dan dosen yang terlibat dalam perawatan ini fokus pada pengecekan perangkat komputer, peralatan audio-visual, jaringan internet, serta software-software yang digunakan untuk proyek multimedia seperti desain grafis, animasi, dan editing video. Selain itu, beberapa peralatan baru juga dipasang untuk meningkatkan kualitas fasilitas laboratorium.

Mahasiswa diharapkan dapat memanfaatkan fasilitas yang telah diperbarui ini dengan maksimal dalam mendukung pengembangan keterampilan praktis mereka di bidang multimedia dan teknologi. Dengan laboratorium yang terawat dengan baik, program studi berkomitmen untuk terus menyediakan lingkungan belajar yang kondusif dan inovatif.

Mahasiswa Pendidikan Multimedia Melaksanakan Perwalian dan Kontrak Kuliah Semester Genap 2023-2024

Cibiru, 26 Januari 2024 — Memasuki semester genap tahun akademik 2023-2024, mahasiswa Program Studi Pendidikan Multimedia UPI Cibiru mulai melaksanakan perwalian dan kontrak kuliah sebagai bagian dari persiapan akademik. Kegiatan ini berlangsung dari 22 Januari 2024 hingga 31 Januari 2024, dengan melibatkan dosen wali dan seluruh mahasiswa aktif di program studi.

Dalam proses perwalian, mahasiswa berkonsultasi dengan dosen wali mengenai pemilihan mata kuliah yang akan diambil selama semester genap. Dosen wali memberikan bimbingan terkait rencana studi yang disesuaikan dengan capaian akademik mahasiswa, serta memastikan bahwa setiap mahasiswa dapat mengikuti perkuliahan sesuai dengan kurikulum yang berlaku.

Setelah perwalian, mahasiswa melanjutkan dengan proses kontrak kuliah melalui sistem akademik online UPI, di mana mereka resmi mendaftarkan mata kuliah yang akan diambil pada semester genap. Kontrak kuliah ini menjadi tahap krusial yang memastikan setiap mahasiswa terdaftar pada mata kuliah yang sesuai dengan rencana akademik mereka.

With the ongoing guardianship and study contract process, it is hoped that all Multimedia Education students can prepare themselves well to face the even semester, and maximize the available learning opportunities to achieve optimal academic results.

Multimedia Education Study Program Lecturers Develop Employee Performance Targets for 2024

Cibiru, January 24, 2024 — As 2024 approaches, lecturers at the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru held a meeting to develop the 2024 Employee Performance Targets (SKP) . This meeting aimed to formulate performance targets aligned with the study program's vision and mission and to support improvements in the quality of education and academic services at UPI.

In a meeting held on January 23, 2024 , the Head of the Multimedia Education Study Program, Ayung Candra Padmasari, S.Pd., MT , led a discussion on the main priorities that must be achieved by every lecturer and education staff in 2024. The focus of the SKP that was prepared included improving the quality of research, community service, and developing digital technology-based teaching methods.

Furthermore, the drafting of the SKP also discussed lecturer professional development programs, such as international training, scientific journal publications, and research collaborations with various institutions both domestically and internationally. This aligns with the study program's efforts to enhance its academic reputation at the national and international levels.

With the preparation of the 2024 SKP, it is hoped that all lecturers and staff of the Multimedia Education Study Program can work in a structured and focused manner, and contribute maximally to achieving the set targets.
