HEALTHY FAMILY EXERCISE ON UPI CAMPUS IN CIBIRU

UPI Cibiru - The Healthy Exercise program, held regularly every Friday at the UPI Cibiru campus, including on Friday, November 15, 2024, aims to promote a healthy lifestyle among the academic community. This exercise program is one of the campus' flagship programs aimed at maintaining physical fitness and increasing enthusiasm for work and study. Involving students, lecturers, and educational staff, this activity also serves as a forum for strengthening togetherness and solidarity within the campus environment. 

 

The main goal of this activity is to foster a culture of healthy and active living within the campus community. Healthy exercise not only helps maintain physical health but also reduces stress caused by busy academic and work schedules. Through simple yet effective exercise movements, participants can improve their physical fitness and maintain emotional stability, enabling them to perform their duties more optimally. 

 

The hope of this activity is to create a healthy, productive, and harmonious campus environment. With consistent participation, this activity is expected to raise awareness of the importance of maintaining health amidst the busyness of daily life. Furthermore, through interactions in a relaxed atmosphere, it is hoped that relationships among the entire academic community will be strengthened, creating strong solidarity to support the progress of the UPI Cibiru Campus as a whole. Ibud, 11/14/2024.
