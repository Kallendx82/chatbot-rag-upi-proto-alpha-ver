Wilujeng Retired Dra. Hj. Tin Rustini, M.Pd.

The extended family of the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program (PGSD) of the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Cibiru Campus expresses its highest appreciation and respect to Mrs. Dra. Hj. Tin Rustini, M.Pd., for her dedication and devotion for almost 40 years.

In the honor ceremony held at the UPI Cibiru Campus, Mrs. Tin Rustini received a certificate of appreciation as a form of gratitude for her services and significant contributions in educating, guiding, and being a role model for the academic community.

During her tenure, Ms. Tin was known as an exemplary educator, highly committed, and consistently upholding the values ​​of scholarship, integrity, and dedication to her students and the institution. Her legacy of service is an inspiration that will be remembered and passed down to future generations.

"May Allah reward all of Mrs. Tin's kindness and sacrifice with blessings, health, and happiness in her retirement," said the representative of the leadership of the UPI Cibiru Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program on that occasion.

This moment is not only a symbol of farewell, but also a reminder of the importance of an educator's dedication in shaping the nation's next generation.
