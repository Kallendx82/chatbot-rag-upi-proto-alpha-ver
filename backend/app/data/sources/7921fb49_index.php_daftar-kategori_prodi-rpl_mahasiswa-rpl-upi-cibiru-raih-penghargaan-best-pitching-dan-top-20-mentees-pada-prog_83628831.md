UPI Cibiru RPL Students Win Best Pitching and Top 20 Mentees Awards in the 2025 Telkom Digistar Program

The Software Engineering Study Program of the Indonesian Education University (RPL UPI) is proud to announce the achievements of Akwan Cakra Tajimalela (class of 2022) in the Digistar Internship Program 2025 event organized by Telkom Indonesia.

Akwan managed to win two awards at once, namely:

Best Pitching – Digistar Telkom 2025

Top 20 Mentees with Outstanding Projects

This achievement demonstrates the ability of UPI RPL students to develop innovative ideas, conduct professional presentations, and produce projects that are considered superior among program participants from various universities.

This success demonstrates UPI's RPL commitment to producing competent, adaptable digital talent ready to contribute to the challenges of the technology industry. The study program hopes this achievement will motivate other students to continue working, innovating, and creating digital works that positively impact society.
