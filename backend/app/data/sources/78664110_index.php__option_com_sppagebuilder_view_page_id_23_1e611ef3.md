Multimedia Education
Vision and mission

Vision

To become a pioneering and superior study program in the development of educational science in Multimedia expertise competencies in Indonesia by 2028.

Mission

Providing educational and teaching services in the field of expertise in Multimedia education to produce graduates who have high academic abilities, are creative, innovative,
Implementing research programs to support high-quality learning processes and contributing to the development of scientific knowledge, applications, and multimedia-based products.
Utilizing multimedia knowledge and products in carrying out community service based on research results that can contribute to advancing a science and technology-based society.
Developing, implementing, and disseminating technology in the field of Multimedia based on the needs of society, the business world, and industry.
