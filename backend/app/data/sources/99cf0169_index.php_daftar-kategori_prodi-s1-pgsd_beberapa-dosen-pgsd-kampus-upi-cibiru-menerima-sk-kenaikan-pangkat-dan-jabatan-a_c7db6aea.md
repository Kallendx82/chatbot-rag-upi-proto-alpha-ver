Several Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Lecturers at UPI Cibiru Campus Received Decrees for Promotion and Academic Position in 2025

Bandung, April 29, 2025 — The Indonesian Education University (UPI) held an event for the handover of Decrees (SK) for Promotions for the April 2025 Period and Decrees for Promotions in Academic Positions in 2025 for Civil Servants and Permanent University Employees working within the UPI environment.

The event, which took place on Tuesday, April 29, 2025, at 9:00 a.m. WIB (Western Indonesian Time), was held in the Auditorium of the New Graduate School Building, 7th Floor, UPI Bandung Campus. The event was led by the Vice Rector for Finance, Infrastructure, and Human Resources, Prof. Adang Suherman.

More than 180 lecturers and educational staff from various faculties and regional campuses at UPI are listed as recipients of the decree. They come from various work units, including the Faculty of Education (FIP), Faculty of Social and Political Sciences (FPPIPS), Faculty of Social and Political Sciences (FPBS), Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences (FPMIPA), Faculty of Social and Political Sciences (FPTI), Faculty of Social and Political Sciences (FPOK), Faculty of Education (FPEB), Faculty of Humanities and Social Development (FPSD), Graduate Schools (SPs), as well as UPI campuses in areas such as Cibiru, Sumedang, Purwakarta, Tasikmalaya, and Serang.

Among the recipients of the Academic Promotion Decree, there were four names recorded from the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program at the UPI Cibiru Campus. They are Hana Yunansah, M.Pd., M Ridwan Sutisna, S.Pd., M.Pd., Dr. Yusuf Tri Herlambang, S.Pd., M.Pd., and Dr. Rendi Restiana Sukardi, S.Pd., M.Pd. In addition, there are also several other study program lecturers from the UPI Cibiru Campus, namely Deden Pradeka, ST, M.Kom., Muhammad Taufik Dwi Putra, S.Tr. Kom., MTI, Indira Syawanodya, M.Kom., Dian Anggraini, MT, Intan Permata Sari, S.ST., M.Ds., and Maya Purnama Sari, S.Pd., M.Ds. 

The awarding of this decree is a token of appreciation from the institution for the academic and administrative achievements of lecturers and staff who have met the requirements and criteria for promotion to higher positions and ranks. This activity also serves as a crucial part of strengthening human resource governance within UPI.

The Vice Chancellor emphasized that promotions in rank and position are not only administrative achievements, but also serve as motivation to continue to actively contribute to education, research, and community service.
