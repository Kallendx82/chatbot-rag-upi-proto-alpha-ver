Implementation of the Multimedia Education Study Program Proposal Seminar Examination for the January 2025 Period

CIBIRU (10/01/2025) – The Multimedia Education Study Program, Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Cibiru Campus, once again held an important stage for final-year students: the Thesis Proposal Seminar Examination. This activity was held for two consecutive days, from January 8 to 9, 2025 .

This academic agenda aims to test the readiness, methodological feasibility, and originality of research ideas proposed by students before moving on to the field research and thesis writing stage.

Utilization of Superior Laboratory Facilities

Unlike conventional exams, this proposal seminar maximized the technological facilities available at the Multimedia Education Study Program. The exams were held simultaneously at two main locations:

Multimedia Computer Laboratory

Game and Innovation Laboratory

These two laboratories were chosen for a reason. In addition to their adequate capacity, both rooms are equipped with presentation equipment and high-spec computers, making it easy for students to demonstrate multimedia product designs or application prototypes that form part of their research proposals.

Conducive Academic Atmosphere

Over the two days of the exam, the atmosphere was observed to be conducive yet critical. The students presented their research designs before a panel of examiners consisting of expert lecturers in educational technology, visual communication design, and software development.

Head of the Multimedia Education Study Program, Ayung Candra Padmasari, S.Pd., MT , expressed his appreciation for the implementation of this period's exam.

"We've seen an improvement in the quality of the proposals submitted by students for the January 2025 term, particularly in the integration of new technologies into learning media. We hope that the input from today's examiners will refine their research plans so that the resulting theses will have a real impact on the world of education," he said.

First Steps Towards Graduation

The Proposal Seminar exam is a crucial first step. Students who pass will immediately receive a Thesis Advisor's Decree (SK) to proceed to the data collection and system development stages.

With the end of the January 2025 exam period, the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus continues to be committed to producing graduates who are not only pedagogically competent but also excel in multimedia technology innovation.
