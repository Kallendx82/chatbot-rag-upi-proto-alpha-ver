Lecturers of PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Campus Participate in Technical Guidance and Certification for the KKNI Level 6 Master Program

Cibiru, December 10, 2024 – Four lecturers from the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) in Cibiru participated in the Technical Guidance (Bimtek) and Certification for the Indonesian National Qualifications Framework (KKNI) Level 6 Master Program. This activity was held online and aimed to prepare participants for the certification exam. During the Bimtek session, participants received a comprehensive presentation regarding the material they must master and prepare for the exam. The material covered competencies in accordance with the KKNI Level 6 standards, which emphasize mastery of knowledge, practical skills, and critical thinking skills in the field of early childhood education. In the following session, the committee explained the technical implementation of the certification exam in detail. This explanation covered the online exam procedures, the collection of supporting documents, and the assessment criteria used to evaluate participant eligibility. Participants were also given the opportunity to ask questions and have direct discussions regarding the technical preparation and substance of the exam.

"This program is very important to improve the competence of lecturers to comply with national and international standards, especially in the field of early childhood education," said one participant from PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Campus.

By participating in the KKNI Level 6 Technical Guidance and Certification, lecturers are expected to improve their capabilities as educators and mentors, while also contributing to strengthening the quality of education in the Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Program. This activity also serves as a strategic step to support the implementation of a curriculum oriented towards KKNI-based learning outcomes.
