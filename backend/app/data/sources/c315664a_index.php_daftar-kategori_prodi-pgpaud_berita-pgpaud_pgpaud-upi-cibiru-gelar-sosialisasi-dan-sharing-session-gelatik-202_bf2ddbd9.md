UPI Cibiru Early Childhood Education Center Holds GELATIK 2025 Socialization and Sharing Session to Encourage Student Creativity

Cibiru, February 24, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) UPI Cibiru Campus took part in the Socialization and Sharing Session of the 2025 Data Science, Digital Innovation, Technology and Information (GELATIK) Exhibition held by the UPI Cibiru Campus Achievement Center on Friday, February 14, 2025.

This event served as a strategic platform to introduce students to various national-level digital innovation competitions, including the Student Digital Innovation Competition (LIDM). The session featured LIDM finalists from previous years, sharing their experiences during the competition, from preparation to challenges, to innovative strategies for creating impactful work.

The enthusiasm of the PGPAUD students was high, especially after listening to the finalists' inspiring stories, which provided immediate motivation to start developing their own creative ideas. The socialization also provided technical insight into the types of competitions available, the judging criteria, and the importance of collaboration and technology exploration in education.

Through this activity, the PGPAUD UPI Cibiru hopes to foster a competitive spirit among students and encourage them to actively participate in innovative events. Participation in GELATIK and similar competitions is expected to provide a platform for developing PGPAUD students' academic, practical, and professional competencies in today's digital era.
