The Multimedia Education Study Program Accreditation Task Force Team Holds Online Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E)

Cibiru, October 28, 2024 — In preparation for accreditation, the Accreditation Task Force Team of the Multimedia Education Study Program at the Indonesian Education University (UPI) Cibiru held an online Monitoring and Evaluation (Monev) on October 28, 2024. This activity aims to ensure that all aspects of accreditation have been fulfilled and are running according to plan.

During implementation, the team held interactive discussions, identified strengths, and sought solutions to challenges encountered. The results of this monitoring and evaluation will serve as a reference for further improvements before the accreditation visitation process takes place.

It is hoped that this effort will lead the Multimedia Education Study Program to achieve superior accreditation results, strengthening its reputation as a study program oriented towards academic and professional excellence.
