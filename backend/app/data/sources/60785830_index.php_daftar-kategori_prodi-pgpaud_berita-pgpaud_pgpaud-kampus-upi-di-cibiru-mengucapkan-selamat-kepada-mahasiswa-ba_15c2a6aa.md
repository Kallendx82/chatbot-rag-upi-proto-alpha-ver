UPI Campus PGPAUD in Cibiru Congratulates New Students Who Passed the 2025 SNBP

Cibiru, March 18, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education Campus in Cibiru congratulates and wishes success to all participants who have passed the 2025 National Achievement-Based Selection (SNBP) and have been officially accepted as new students at the PGPAUD Study Program at UPI Cibiru. This achievement is the result of dedication, hard work, and an extraordinary enthusiasm for learning. PGPAUD UPI Cibiru is proud to welcome you as part of an academic community committed to producing professional and competitive educators in the field of early childhood education. With UNGGUL accreditation, qualified lecturers, a curriculum based on the needs of the times, and complete supporting facilities, the PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Study Program is ready to be the best place to grow and develop together. Welcome to the big family of PGPAUD UPI Cibiru! May this academic journey be a beautiful start towards a bright and meaningful future.
