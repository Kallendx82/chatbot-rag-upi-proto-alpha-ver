Information for Students and Lecturers of the PGPAUD Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru regarding the Implementation of Final Semester Exams and the Submission of Grades for the Odd Semester of 2024/2025

In connection with the academic calendar of the Indonesian Education University (UPI) for the 2024/2025 year, we would like to inform all students of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) at the UPI Campus in Cibiru regarding the implementation of the Final Semester Exam (UAS) for the Odd Semester of 2024/2025 which will be held from December 26, 2024 to January 12, 2025. The following is important information that all students need to pay attention to:

1. Final Exam Schedule for Odd Semester 2024/2025

The final exams for General (MKU), Basic Education (MKDK), and University Specialization (MKKU) courses will be held online using a Computer Based Test (CBT) from December 26 to 30, 2024. Meanwhile, the exams for expertise courses (MKKIPS, MKKPBS, MKKPPS, MKKF, and MKKSPs) will be held offline by the respective lecturers.

2. CBT Final Exam Trial

Students enrolled in MKU, MKDK, and MKKU courses are required to take the online CBT UAS trial, which will be held on December 23, 2024 , from 10:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. WIB. This trial aims to ensure readiness for the online exam.

3. Online Final Exam Provisions
The online CBT UAS for MKU/MKDK/MKKU will be held on the following schedule:
Thursday, December 26, 2024 : Islamic Religious Education, Indonesian Language Education, Civics Education, and Pancasila Education.
Friday, December 27, 2024 : Educational Foundations, Educational Psychology and Guidance, Curriculum and Learning, and Physical Education and Sports.
Monday, December 30, 2024 : Educational Management, Introduction to Education, Protestant Christian Religious Education, and Catholic Christian Religious Education.
Students are expected to arrive on time. No additional time will be given to those who are late.
4. Value Input
Entry of UAS scores by lecturers will be carried out from January 8 to 12, 2025, via the SIAK platform.
Remedial and Re-examination will be held from January 8 to 12, 2025 .
Re-examination score entries can be made until January 16, 2025 .
5. Important Reminder
The odd semester of 2024/2025 will end on December 20, 2024.
Make sure you have followed the academic guidance and completed the IRS/PRS completely before the exam is held.
Problems during the exam can be reported via WA CS ICT Services at 0811-2011-007 or 0811-2291-229 .
There will be no online make-up final exams for MKU, MKDK, and MKKU. If you cannot take the exam on schedule, please contact your instructor.

Please pay close attention to all of this information to ensure the smooth running of the final exam and the submission of grades. Ensure you follow the procedures accurately and on time.

This information is conveyed, hopefully the exam goes smoothly.
