Stakeholder Satisfaction Survey on Facilities and Infrastructure in 2024

Cibiru, September 18, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru conducted a Stakeholder Satisfaction Survey on Facilities and Infrastructure in 2024. This survey aims to measure the level of satisfaction of students, lecturers, staff, and related parties with the available facilities and identify areas that need improvement.

The survey covers various aspects, such as the availability of classrooms, laboratories, technology facilities, and the comfort of the campus environment. Active participation from all stakeholders is highly recommended to obtain accurate and representative data.

The survey results will be analyzed and used as a basis for planning improvements to facilities and infrastructure in the coming years. The Study Program is committed to providing a comfortable, modern learning environment that meets the needs of developing educational technology.

Students and other stakeholders are invited to participate by completing the survey through the link provided. We hope this effort will positively impact the quality of education in the Multimedia Education Study Program.
