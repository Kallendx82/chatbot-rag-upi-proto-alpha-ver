2025 Doctoral Program Scholarship Opportunities for PGPAUD Lecturers at UPI Cibiru

Cibiru, June 13, 2025 — In order to support the improvement of human resources quality and strengthening academic capacity, lecturers of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education Campus in Cibiru are encouraged to take advantage of the opportunity to register for doctoral program scholarships (S3) in 2025. Various scholarship schemes are offered by the government through the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology (Kemendikbudristek), LPDP, and partner universities in Indonesia and abroad. This program is part of the national commitment to producing competent doctoral-qualified lecturers who are ready to compete at the global level. This program is very relevant for PGPAUD lecturers who wish to continue their studies in the fields of early childhood education, curriculum and learning, developmental psychology, or other related fields. The Head of the PGPAUD Study Program stated that participation in this program will not only enrich individual academic insight, but also have a direct impact on the quality of learning and scientific development within the PGPAUD UPI Cibiru environment.

Let's realize the vision of PGPAUD lecturers who are superior, competitive, and impactful through further studies at the doctoral level.
