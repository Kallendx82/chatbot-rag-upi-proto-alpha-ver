University of Malaya

PGPAUD Cibiru has established an international partnership with Universiti Malaya (UM), Malaysia, to enhance academic quality and broaden global perspectives in the field of early childhood education. This collaboration is implemented through various activities, including guest lectures, visiting professors, summer programs, and curriculum benchmarking between institutions. Through this partnership, PGPAUD students and lecturers gain opportunities to expand their academic experiences, deepen cross-cultural understanding, and access best practices in early childhood education at the international level. Additionally, one of the lecturers from the PGPAUD Study Program is currently pursuing a doctoral (Ph.D.) degree at Universiti Malaya as part of efforts to strengthen human resource capacity and global academic networking.

Universitas Muhammadiyah Surabaya

PGPAUD Cibiru has established a collaboration with Universitas Muhammadiyah Surabaya through guest lecture activities and joint book chapter writing. In the guest lecture sessions, lecturers from both institutions shared insights and experiences in the field of early childhood education, enriching the students' learning process. Additionally, the joint book chapter writing project reflects a strong academic synergy aimed at producing meaningful and relevant scholarly work. This collaboration highlights PGPAUD's commitment to strengthening national academic networks and enhancing the quality of education and scientific publications.
