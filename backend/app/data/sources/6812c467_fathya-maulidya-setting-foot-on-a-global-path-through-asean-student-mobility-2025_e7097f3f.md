Fathya Maulidya, Setting Foot on a Global Path Through ASEAN Student Mobility 2025.
February 14, 2025
Berita UPI Tasikmalaya, Bisnis Digital, Prestasi Unit Kerja dan Mahasiswa

Continuing to make an impact on the international stage, the Digital Business Program proudly delegates Fathya Maulidya as its representative for ASEAN Student Mobility 2025, a program designed to connect students across Southeast Asia through discussions, idea exchanges, and cross-border collaborations to strengthen the role of youth in shaping ASEAN’s future.

This program will take place from February 11–25, 2025, in a hybrid format, combining online discussions with on-site activities in Kuala Lumpur. Throughout the program, participants will explore various challenges and opportunities in the ASEAN region while engaging in interactive discussions on the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The program’s key focus areas include quality education, environmental sustainability, social innovation, and economic inclusivity, which are critical topics in building a better future for ASEAN.

As a representative of UPI, Fathya will participate in a series of activities aimed at developing leadership skills, fostering cross-cultural understanding, and enhancing critical thinking in addressing global challenges. Furthermore, interactions with students from various countries will create opportunities for international collaboration and provide valuable insights into how young individuals can contribute to meaningful change.

Participation in ASEAN Student Mobility 2025 highlights UPI students’ readinessto be part of an active, innovative, and socially responsible global community. This program is not just an academic exchange but also a platform for students to contribute to real-world solutions for a more sustainable future.

By engaging in international programs like this, students can expand their perspectives, build professional networks, and bring the spirit of change to a global stage. Congratulations and success to Fathya Maulidya! May this journey bring valuable experiences and open even greater opportunities at the international level. (FM)
