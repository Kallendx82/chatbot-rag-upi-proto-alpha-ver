PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Congratulates the Inauguration of the UPI Rector for the 2025–2030 Period

Cibiru, July 1, 2025 — The extended family of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) of the Indonesian University of Education Campus in Cibiru conveys congratulations and success on the inauguration of the Rector of the Indonesian University of Education for the 2025–2030 period. We congratulate him on the mandate and responsibility he has assumed in leading UPI into the future. Hopefully, under the new leadership, UPI will progress further, excel, and continue to be a pioneer in educational innovation at the national and international levels. This inauguration is an important momentum to strengthen our shared commitment to building an inclusive, adaptive, and globally competitive higher education ecosystem. PGPAUD UPI Cibiru is ready to support the Rector's vision and strategic programs to realize UPI as a world-renowned university while upholding the values ​​of science, humanity, and culture. Congratulations on carrying out your mandate, and may you be successful in serving the advancement of Indonesian education.
