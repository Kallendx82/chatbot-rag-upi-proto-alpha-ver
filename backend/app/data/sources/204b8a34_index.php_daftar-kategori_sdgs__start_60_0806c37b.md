First Aid Training for Sixth Semester Students: Building Mental Resilience and Professionalism in Partner Schools

Friday,   February

In order to prepare students for the implementation of the P3K Program, the Study Program held a briefing and guidance activity for Semester VI students on Thursday, January 5, 2026. This activity was held in the dormitory room of the UPI Cibiru Campus and was guided directly by Dr. Asep Rudi Nurjaman, M.Pd.I. This activity aims to provide direction and strengthen student readiness before going directly to partner schools that have been designated as locations for the implementation of P3K.

In his remarks, he emphasized the importance of mental preparedness, discipline, and responsibility in carrying out academic tasks within the school environment. Students are expected to maintain a professional attitude, adapt to the school culture, and demonstrate commitment as prospective educators with integrity. He also emphasized that first aid activities are a crucial part of the learning process, providing students with real-world experience in implementing the pedagogical and scientific competencies they have acquired throughout their studies.

This briefing activity serves as a crucial opportunity to strengthen students' confidence and readiness to face challenges in the field. It also reflects the Study Program's commitment to ensuring students are well-prepared, both academically and mentally, before undertaking practical work in a real-world educational environment.

Preparation of Employee Performance Targets (SKP) for Lecturers in the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program

Friday, February 6, 2026

To improve lecturer performance and professionalism, the Employee Performance Targets (SKP) development program for lecturers in the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program has been implemented. This activity is a crucial part of the effort to organize and align lecturer performance targets to align with the study program's vision, mission, and academic achievements.

The discussion on the preparation of the SKP was conducted by the Head of the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program for the undergraduate and postgraduate levels, Dr. Tita Mulyati, M.Pd., which took place in the Smartclass Room. This activity focused on reviewing, aligning, and formulating lecturer performance targets, both in the fields of education and teaching, research, community service, and supporting elements of the tridharma of higher education.

Melalui kegiatan ini, diharapkan penyusunan SKP dosen PGSD dapat menjadi instrumen yang efektif dalam mendorong peningkatan kinerja, akuntabilitas, serta kualitas layanan akademik. Selain itu, kegiatan ini juga mendukung terciptanya tata kelola program studi yang lebih terstruktur, profesional, dan berorientasi pada peningkatan mutu pendidikan.

Merancang Masa Depan Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia melalui Renstra 2026

Jum’at, 06 Februari 2026

Dalam rangka merumuskan arah kebijakan dan pengembangan institusi ke depan, Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia menyelenggarakan Rapat Rencana Strategis (Renstra) Tahun 2026. Kegiatan ini menjadi forum penting untuk menyelaraskan visi, misi, serta program strategis universitas dalam menghadapi tantangan dan peluang di masa mendatang.

Rapat Renstra 2026 dilaksanakan di Aula Gedung E Kampus UPI Cibiru dan dihadiri oleh para pimpinan serta dosen sebagai pemangku kepentingan utama dalam perencanaan dan pelaksanaan kebijakan institusi. Kehadiran unsur pimpinan dan sivitas akademika ini mencerminkan komitmen bersama dalam membangun perencanaan yang partisipatif, komprehensif, dan berorientasi pada peningkatan mutu pendidikan.

Kegiatan berlangsung mulai  dengan agenda pembahasan arah strategis, prioritas program, serta penguatan tata kelola universitas. Melalui rapat ini, diharapkan Renstra 2026 mampu menjadi landasan yang kokoh dalam mewujudkan Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia sebagai institusi pendidikan tinggi yang unggul, adaptif, dan berkelanjutan.

UPI Cibiru Perkuat Tata Kelola Digital melalui Monitoring Pengembangan Aplikasi WBS

Sebagai bagian dari upaya peningkatan efisiensi dan integrasi proses kerja di Kampus UPI Cibiru, telah dilaksanakan kegiatan Monitoring Pembuatan Aplikasi WBS. Kegiatan ini bertujuan untuk meninjau progres pengembangan aplikasi, mengidentifikasi kendala teknis, serta memastikan bahwa aplikasi dapat mendukung manajemen kerja secara optimal.

Rapat monitoring dilaksanakan di Ruang Wakil Direktur Bidang Pendidikan, Kemahasiswaan, dan Penjaminanan Mutu, dengan kehadiran 8 dosen dan 5 mahasiswa sebagai tim pengembang dan evaluator. Pertemuan ini menjadi wadah untuk berbagi masukan, mengevaluasi progres pembuatan aplikasi, serta menyusun strategi penyelesaian masalah teknis yang muncul selama pengembangan.

This activity affirms UPI Cibiru's commitment to utilizing technology to support more effective, transparent, and integrated institutional governance. With regular monitoring, it is hoped that the WBS application will be completed soon and serve as a strategic tool for all campus units, creating more structured, efficient work processes that positively impact the academic community.

Recruiting Outstanding Students through the 2026 UPI Cibiru Campus Presidential Election

Friday, February 6, 2026

In an effort to capture and develop the potential of superior students, the UPI Cibiru Campus held the 2026 Outstanding Student Selection (PILMAPRES) activity. This activity is a strategic forum to appreciate students' academic and non-academic achievements while encouraging the strengthening of students' character, leadership, and competitiveness at a higher level.

The PILMAPRES (Presidential Election) activities were attended by students, both in person and online via Google Meet, providing an inclusive and flexible space for participation. The offline activities were centered in the Cawalaloka Room on the UPI Cibiru Campus, which served as the primary location for student selection and presentations.

This activity was attended in person by the Deputy Director for Education, Student Affairs, and Quality Assurance, Dr.Eng.Munawir, MT. Head of the Academic and Student Affairs Section, Annas Fachruddin, S.Pd. as well as the Heads of Study Programs and lecturers, who acted as a team of companions and assessors. The presence of the leadership and academic community emphasized the institution's commitment to supporting the development of student achievement and ensuring an objective, transparent, and quality selection process.

Through the implementation of the 2026 PILMAPRES, it is hoped that the best students will be selected who are able to represent the UPI Cibiru Campus, as well as become an inspiration for other students to continue to excel and contribute positively to the institution and society.
