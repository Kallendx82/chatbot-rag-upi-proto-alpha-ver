Withdrawal of Gumilang PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Internship Students from Kidzania Surabaya

Cibiru, January 15, 2025 - The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru held a withdrawal activity for Gumilang Internship Students from Kidzania Surabaya on Wednesday, January 15, 2025. The withdrawal activity was carried out online and was attended by the Head of the PGPAUD Study Program at UPI Cibiru, representatives of HRD at Kidzania Surabaya, and two students participating in the Gumilang Internship.

This activity marked the end of a series of internships, part of strengthening students' competencies in the edutainment workforce. During the withdrawal process, all parties provided evaluations, appreciation, and reflections on the internship experience.

The Head of the Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at UPI Cibiru expressed his gratitude to Kidzania Surabaya for their partnership in providing hands-on experience for students. This collaboration not only benefits students' professional skills but also aligns with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), specifically:

·       SDG 4 (Quality Education) through providing meaningful, inclusive, and relevant learning experiences to the needs of the world of work.

·       SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) by providing opportunities for students to understand the professional work environment and increase their readiness to enter the world of work.

·       SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals) through strategic partnerships between universities and the industrial world to support the achievement of quality education goals.

In their remarks, the HR department at Kidzania Surabaya also expressed their appreciation for the students' contributions during their internships. They noted that they demonstrated professionalism, strong communication skills, and creativity in assisting child visitors.

Both students also shared their reflections on their experiences, including their understanding of vehicle operations, strengthening of service skills, and their ability to adapt to a dynamic work environment.

At the end of the activity, all parties hoped that the collaboration between PGPAUD UPI Cibiru and Kidzania Surabaya could continue to be expanded as a form of joint commitment to supporting quality and sustainable education.

Thus, the Gumilang Internship students officially completed their entire internship program in the 2024/2025 academic year, while simultaneously strengthening the campus's contribution to achieving the SDGs through education and productive partnerships.
