Welcome New Students of the 2025 SNBT Pathway

On Thursday, May 29, 2025 is the announcement day for new students through the SNBT (UTBK) pathway in 2025 which was announced on the official page of the national selection for new student admissions (SNPMB) at 15.00 WIB which can be accessed at the following link https://pengumuman-snbt-snpmb.bppp.kemdikbud.go.id/ . After opening the announcement, students who are accepted through the SNBT pathway will look for advocacy contacts from the study program departments and universities where they passed. Each association from all study programs and universities usually has prepared a poster welcoming new students through the SNBT pathway in 2025 which contains contact person information that students need to contact to confirm their graduation along with a gform link that they need to fill in regarding their personal data.

The student association conducts a screening of new students through the SNBT pathway in order to collect data on the number of acceptances this year, which will then be created a special WhatsApp group for recipients of the SNBT pathway, as has been done by HIMA PGSD. This screening is carried out by the Advocacy Department in its work program called PEMBARU 6.0. In this PEMBARU 6.0 work program, after conducting a screening of new students by entering new students into their respective study program groups, then a briefing will be carried out for new students in 2025. The new student intelligence is carried out with the aim of socializing about PGSD Campus UP I in Cibiru and the Intelligence steps are carried out to support the knowledge of new students during the lecture period.
