Vision and Mission of the Study Program
Vision

Pioneer and Excellence in PGPAUD Development through the Tri Dharma of Higher Education to Produce Graduates who are Competent, Creative, Globally Competitive, Have Noble Morals and Have an Entrepreneurial Spirit

Mission

Developing educational programs in the field of PGPAUD that are able to produce graduates who have competitive advantages and high dedication;

Developing research programs that contribute to the development of innovative early childhood education theories and practices rooted in local wisdom;

Develop community service programs based on research results based on PAUD expertise;

Develop partnerships with various institutions that contribute to improving the quality of PGPAUD services.
