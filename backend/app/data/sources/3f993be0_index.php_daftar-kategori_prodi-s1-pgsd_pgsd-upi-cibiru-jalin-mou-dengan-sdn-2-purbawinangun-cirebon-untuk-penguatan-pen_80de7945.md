UPI Cibiru's Elementary School Teacher Education Department Signs MoU with SDN 2 Purbawinangun Cirebon to Strengthen Elementary Education

The Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Cibiru Campus officially established a partnership with SDN 2 Purbawinangun, Cirebon, through the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU). This MoU represents a concrete form of collaboration between academics and elementary schools to improve the quality of education. The scope of the collaboration includes:

Assistance with curriculum development and active learning in elementary school classes

Involvement of PGSD students in community service activities and teaching practice

Provision of teacher training and improvement of professionalism of teaching staff

At the MoU signing, Dr. Yona, representing the Elementary School Teacher Education Department of UPI Cibiru, and the principal of SDN 2 Purbawinangun shook hands to mark the beginning of the collaboration. This strategic move aligns with the spirit of the Independent Campus (Kampus Merdeka), where universities play a direct role in advancing basic education through synergy with partners in the surrounding area.

With this collaboration, UPI Cibiru PGSD students can apply theories and innovations in real-world contexts, while SDN 2 Purbawinangun gains access to more up-to-date academic resources, research, and educational practices.

It is hoped that this MoU will not only strengthen the partnership, but also encourage the transformation of learning at SDN 2 Purbawinangun so that it becomes more inspiring, effective, and relevant for the development of students.
