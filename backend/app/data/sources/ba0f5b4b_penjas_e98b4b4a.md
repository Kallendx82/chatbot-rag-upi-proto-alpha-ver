Rizal Bahtiar, M.Pd.
Penjas
Kedutaan Besar Republik Indonesia di Malaysia

<p>The academic path of Rizal Bahtiar, M.Pd., stands as a story of perseverance, commitment, and a lifelong pursuit of knowledge. A proud alumnus of the Primary School Teacher Education (PGSD) in Physical Education Program (2015-2019) and the Master’s Program in Physical Education (2019–2021) at Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Sumedang Campus, Rizal exemplifies how a solid educational foundation can lead to global academic achievement.</p><p>His time at UPI Sumedang Campus provided a transformative learning experience – guided by dedicated lecturers, immersed in a nurturing academic environment, and enriched by opportunities to strengthen critical thinking and research capabilities. These experiences shaped not only his intellectual growth but also his character: resilient, inquisitive, and driven to contribute meaningfully to the world of education.</p><p>Today, Rizal continues his academic journey as a doctoral candidate at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI), Malaysia, supported by the Beasiswa Unggulan Scholarship from Indonesia’s Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology. This achievement marks a significant step in his mission to engage in educational research and policy development that benefits the broader community.</p><p>His field, Physical Education, serves as more than just an academic discipline, it is a philosophy of balance between physical vitality, discipline, and intellectual pursuit. It is through this perspective that Rizal seeks to inspire future generations to view education as a lifelong process of growth, reflection, and contribution.</p><p>From Sumedang’s classrooms to Malaysia’s research halls, Rizal Bahtiar’s story embodies the enduring spirit of UPI Sumedang’s alumni: educators who are globally minded yet deeply rooted in their mission to advance education in Indonesia. The campus continues to stand as a cradle for future leaders, those who teach, inspire, and redefine the meaning of learning across borders.</p>

Karisdha Pradityana
Penjas
Dosen Universitas Terbuka

<p>Karisdha Pradityana, a graduate of the Primary School Teacher Education (Physical Education) Program at Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) Sumedang Campus, completed his undergraduate studies in 2015. He is now a lecturer in the Primary School Teacher Education Program at Universitas Terbuka and has completed his doctoral studies in Physical Education. His academic journey reflects a strong dedication to professionalism and a lifelong commitment to advancing education in Indonesia.</p><p>For Karisdha, studying at UPI Sumedang Campus was more than the pursuit of a degree. It was a transformative experience that shaped his pedagogical insight, professionalism, and integrity. The comprehensive curriculum, the guidance of skilled lecturers, and the supportive academic atmosphere nurtured him into a reflective and innovative educator capable of addressing the evolving challenges of education.</p><p>“During my time at UPI, I gained a comprehensive and meaningful learning experience – academically, pedagogically, and in terms of character development,” he reflects.</p><p>He believes that the knowledge, values, and sense of purpose he acquired at UPI have formed the foundation of his professional growth. These experiences have enabled him to fulfill the <i>tridharma perguruan tinggi</i> – teaching, research, and community service with integrity and vision.</p><p>Now as a lecturer, Karisdha continues to embody the spirit of excellence and innovation fostered at UPI Sumedang Campus. For him, being part of this academic community is both an honor and a lifelong mission to strengthen the quality of physical education in Indonesia.</p>
