UPI Cibiru Campus and Langlang Buana University Discuss and Sign Cooperation Agreement

Bandung, April 25, 2025 — In an effort to expand the institutional cooperation network, the Indonesian Education University (UPI) Cibiru Campus held a discussion and signing of the Cooperation Agreement (PKS) with Langlang Buana University (UNLA) on Friday, April 25, 2025, at the Hall of Building E, 1st Floor, UPI Cibiru Campus.

The event, which started at 13.00 WIB, was attended by the leaders and structural officials within the UPI Cibiru Campus, including the Director, Deputy Director, Heads of Study Programs (PGSD, PGPAUD, Multimedia Education, Software Engineering, and Computer Engineering), as well as section heads and other supporting staff.

The activity agenda includes three main points: discussion of the contents of the cooperation document, signing of the PKS document between the two institutions, and follow-up discussions regarding the implementation of joint programs designed to support the Tri Dharma of Higher Education.

The Director of the UPI Campus in Cibiru, Deni Darmawan, expressed his hope that this collaboration would strengthen synergy between institutions in the fields of education, research, and community service, especially in the era of digital transformation and the industrial revolution 4.0 which demands cross-institutional collaboration.

This signing marks an important milestone in establishing a strategic partnership between UPI Cibiru and UNLA, which is expected to have a positive impact on developing institutional capacity and improving the quality of graduates.
