The PGPAUD Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus Holds a Practical Activity for the Entrepreneurship Course with a Digital Marketing Theme

Cibiru, December 6, 2024 – The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) in Cibiru held a practical activity in the Entrepreneurship course with the theme Digital Marketing . This activity lasted for three days, starting from December 6, 8, and 9, 2024, and was attended by all students of the 2024 intake. This activity featured experienced practitioners from industry partners who have long collaborated with the study program. In the opening session, practitioners provided in-depth insights into effective digital marketing strategies to support small and medium enterprises (SMEs), especially in the field of early childhood education.

"In this digital era, the ability to utilize technology to market products and services is crucial, including for early childhood education educators who want to develop creative, education-based businesses," said the speaker in his presentation.

During the activity, students not only received theory about digital marketing, but also involved in simulations and case studies to understand the implementation of marketing strategies through digital platforms such as social media, marketplaces, and other technology-based applications. The Head of the PGPAUD Study Program at the UPI Cibiru Campus said that this activity aims to equip students with entrepreneurial skills relevant to the needs of the times. "We want students not only to be educators, but also to be able to become creative and innovative entrepreneurs. This competency strongly supports the PGPAUD Study Program's vision of producing graduates who are adaptive and ready to compete in the world of work," he explained.

The students' enthusiasm was evident throughout the event. They actively asked questions, discussed discussions, and participated in various activities prepared by the speakers. One student said, "This activity is very beneficial. I now understand better how to utilize digital media to market my ideas or products." With the success of this practical activity, the PGPAUD Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus further demonstrated its commitment to integrating theory and practice, as well as forging close collaboration with industry partners to support student learning.
