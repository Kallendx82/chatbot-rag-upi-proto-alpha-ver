Two PGSD Lecturers Participate in PEKERTI Activities

 

Bandung, April 25, 2025 – In order to improve basic competencies and instructional techniques, two lecturers from the PGSD Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus participated in the Basic Skills and Instructional Techniques Improvement Training (PEKERTI) which was held on April 21-25, 2025 at the 6th Floor of the FPEB Building Auditorium. The two lecturers are Yayang Furi Furnamasari, M.Pd. and Mega Laeni, M.Pd.

This activity represents UPI's effort to improve the quality of teaching and the professionalism of lecturers, particularly in designing, implementing, and evaluating effective learning. PEKERTI training is a strategic step in developing lecturer capacity, particularly in facing the challenges of learning in the digital era and the Independent Learning and Independent Campus (MBKM) curriculum.

In a spirited and friendly atmosphere, the training participants appeared enthusiastic throughout the entire series of activities. One special moment was captured in a group photo taken after the closing session, where the lecturers posed together with enthusiastic smiles and carrying gifts as a token of appreciation and a memento of their participation.

With this training, it is hoped that UPI lecturers can continue to develop learning innovations that are adaptive, creative, and relevant to student needs and current developments.
