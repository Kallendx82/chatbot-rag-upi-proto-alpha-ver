EDUHUMANIORA JOURNAL TEAM PARTICIPATES IN JOURNAL ACCREDITATION IMPROVEMENT WORKSHOP

On Tuesday, April 22, 2025, a workshop on improving journal accreditation was held. The Eduhumaniora journal team also contributed to the event. The workshop was held in the hall of building E, 1st floor, UPI Campus in Cibiru. The speakers who attended were Prof. Dr. Eng. Asep Bayu Dani Nandiyanto, ST, M.Eng. and the KJP team. In the workshop, several inputs and training were reviewed to improve the quality of journals nationally and internationally. This event was attended by the journal team at the UPI Campus in Cibiru, which consists of a team of lecturers and students.

The workshop is expected to improve the accreditation and quality of the Eduhumaniora journal. The Eduhumaniora journal team is currently preparing for journal accreditation in 2027, requiring a review of the journal's administration and management to ensure it meets Sinta 2 and Scopus standards.
