UPI Cibiru PGPAUD Lecturers Involved in Student RPL Activities in Collaboration with the Adiluhung Nusantara Foundation

Cibiru, July 15, 2025 — Three lecturers from the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) in Cibiru participated in the coordination and assessment of Prior Learning Recognition (RPL) for PGPAUD students in collaboration with the Adiluhung Nusantara Foundation. This activity was held for two days, on July 14 and 15, 2025, as part of the process of recognizing learning achievements obtained by students through non-formal channels and previous work experience. The lecturers were responsible for coordinating and assessing student portfolio documents in accordance with established standards and procedures. This activity is a form of real support from the PGPAUD Study Program at UPI Cibiru in expanding access to higher education through the RPL scheme and strengthening strategic partnerships with institutions outside the campus. It is hoped that the implementation of this RPL can provide real benefits for students in continuing their studies in a more structured and efficient manner.

UPI Cibiru Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) Attends Second Quarter Achievement Report Meeting with Campus Leadership

Cibiru, July 10, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) Campus in Cibiru participated in the second quarter achievement report meeting, which was held on July 10, 2025. This meeting was attended by the Director of the UPI Cibiru Campus, Deputy Directors, and all Heads of Study Programs on campus. This activity aimed to monitor and evaluate the implementation of programs and the performance achievements of each study program until the end of the second quarter of 2025. On this occasion, the PGPAUD Study Program reported a number of encouraging achievements. Some of the achievements that have been achieved include:

Lecturer involvement in various activities outside the campus, both as resource persons, collaboration partners, and in community service programs.
Students who have completed 20 credits of off-campus learning as part of the implementation of the Independent Learning – Independent Campus (MBKM) program.
The increase in the number of citations of lecturers' publications, which reflects the increasing productivity and quality of research.
As well as various other achievements that support the achievement of Key Performance Indicators (KPI) and improving the quality of education.

Rapat ini menjadi forum strategis untuk saling berbagi praktik baik, menyamakan persepsi, serta merumuskan langkah-langkah tindak lanjut guna mendorong pencapaian target kinerja hingga akhir tahun.

PGPAUD Kampus UPI di Cibiru Ikuti Rapat Revisi RKAT Bersama Pimpinan dan Para Ketua Program Studi

Cibiru, 10 Juli 2025 — Program Studi Pendidikan Guru Pendidikan Anak Usia Dini (PGPAUD) Kampus UPI di Cibiru turut berpartisipasi dalam kegiatan rapat revisi Rencana Kerja dan Anggaran Tahunan (RKAT) yang dilaksanakan pada 10 Juli 2025. Rapat ini dilaksanakan bersama dengan Direktur Kampus UPI di Cibiru, Wakil Direktur, Satuan Kendali Mutu (SKM), serta seluruh Ketua Program Studi di lingkungan kampus. Kegiatan ini bertujuan untuk melakukan peninjauan dan penyesuaian terhadap RKAT masing-masing program studi agar selaras dengan arah kebijakan, kebutuhan prioritas, serta perkembangan terbaru di lingkungan kampus. Melalui rapat ini, setiap program studi diberikan kesempatan untuk menyampaikan revisi dan usulan strategis terkait pelaksanaan kegiatan akademik maupun non-akademik tahun berjalan, dengan mempertimbangkan efektivitas, efisiensi, serta dukungan terhadap pencapaian Indikator Kinerja Utama (IKU). Keterlibatan aktif PGPAUD dalam rapat ini menjadi bentuk komitmen untuk mewujudkan tata kelola program studi yang lebih terarah, akuntabel, dan responsif terhadap dinamika pendidikan tinggi.

Program Studi PGPAUD UPI Kampus Cibiru Gelar Rapat Evaluasi Akhir Semester dan Persiapan Awal Semester Ganjil 2025/2026

Cibiru, 8 Juli 2025 — Program Studi Pendidikan Guru Pendidikan Anak Usia Dini (PGPAUD) Kampus UPI di Cibiru menyelenggarakan kegiatan rapat evaluasi akhir semester dan rapat awal semester pada hari Selasa, 8 Juli 2025. Kegiatan ini berlangsung secara luring dan diikuti oleh seluruh dosen di lingkungan Prodi PGPAUD. Rapat ini dipimpin langsung oleh Ketua Program Studi PGPAUD, yang mengarahkan jalannya diskusi dan koordinasi. Fokus utama rapat meliputi evaluasi penilaian kinerja dosen selama semester genap tahun akademik 2024/2025, persiapan perkuliahan untuk semester ganjil 2025/2026, serta penyusunan dan penyesuaian jadwal perkuliahan. Dalam rapat ini, para dosen saling berbagi refleksi mengenai pelaksanaan pembelajaran, capaian luaran mata kuliah, serta tantangan yang dihadapi selama satu semester terakhir. Selain itu, disepakati pula beberapa strategi penguatan kualitas pembelajaran yang akan diterapkan pada semester mendatang. Kegiatan ini menjadi momen penting bagi Prodi PGPAUD UPI Cibiru untuk menjaga kesinambungan mutu akademik, meningkatkan kolaborasi antar-dosen, serta menyelaraskan agenda perkuliahan dengan kebijakan institusi dan kebutuhan mahasiswa.

PGPAUD Kampus UPI di Cibiru Laksanakan Akreditasi Internasional ACQUIN

Cibiru, July 5, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education (UPI) in Cibiru has completed its international accreditation process from the ACQUIN (Accreditation, Certification, and Quality Assurance Institute) on July 3 and 4, 2025. The two-day online accreditation process is an important part of efforts to improve the quality of education within the PGPAUD environment. The activity began with an interview session with the head of the study program and faculty, followed by interviews with educational staff (tendik) on the first day. On the second day, the accreditation session continued with interviews with lecturers, students, and alumni. The entire process ran smoothly and was attended enthusiastically by all parties involved. This international accreditation has been prepared for a long time, involving intensive cooperation and coordination from the accreditation team and the entire academic community. It is hoped that the results of this process will provide international recognition for the quality of education at PGPAUD UPI Cibiru and encourage continuous quality improvement.
