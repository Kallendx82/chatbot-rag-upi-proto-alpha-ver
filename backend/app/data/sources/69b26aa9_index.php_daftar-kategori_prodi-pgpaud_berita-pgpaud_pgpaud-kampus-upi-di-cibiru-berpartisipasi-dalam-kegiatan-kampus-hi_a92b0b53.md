UPI Campus PGPAUD in Cibiru Participates in Green Campus 2025 Activities

Cibiru, November 7, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education (PGPAUD) study program at the UPI campus in Cibiru participated in the Green Campus program, organized by the UPI campus in Cibiru, as part of its efforts to create a healthy, clean, and sustainable campus environment. The event involved lecturers, educational staff, and students from various study programs, including PGPAUD, who attended enthusiastically.

The Green Campus 2025 program included a fun walk, a campus cleanup, and a communal meal as a demonstration of togetherness and solidarity across the academic community. Participants worked together to clean the park, pedestrian paths, open spaces, and parking areas, demonstrating a collective commitment to maintaining a clean and comfortable campus environment.

This activity is not only a forum for togetherness, but also an educational tool to strengthen environmental awareness for the academic community, including PGPAUD students who will later become educators and agents of change in society.

PGPAUD's participation in the Green Campus activity aligns with SDG 13: Climate Action and SDG 15: Life on Land, as it focuses on concrete actions to preserve the environment, reduce pollution, and create healthy and sustainable learning spaces. Furthermore, this activity also supports SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being through a walk that promotes a healthy lifestyle.

Through this activity, PGPAUD UPI Cibiru affirms its commitment to continue supporting sustainable campus programs and building a culture of environmental care among students.
