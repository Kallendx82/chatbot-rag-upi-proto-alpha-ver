Circular on the Prohibition of Hazing and Pick-Up of New Students at MOKAKU 2024

Cibiru, September 19, 2024 — The Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru has issued an official circular regarding the prohibition of hazing and the pick-up of new students during the 2024 MOKAKU (Campus and College Orientation Period) . This step was taken as part of the commitment to creating a safe, comfortable, and educational orientation environment for new students.

In the circular, the study program emphasized that any form of hazing, intimidation, or degrading treatment of new students will not be tolerated. Furthermore, any activities involving the pickup or conditioning of new students that do not comply with official regulations are also prohibited.

Senior students and the MOKAKU committee are expected to comply with this circular responsibly. Violators will be subject to sanctions in accordance with the regulations of the Indonesian University of Education (UPI).

With this circular, the Multimedia Education Study Program hopes that MOKAKU 2024 can run smoothly, conducively, and become a positive, memorable moment for all new students.
