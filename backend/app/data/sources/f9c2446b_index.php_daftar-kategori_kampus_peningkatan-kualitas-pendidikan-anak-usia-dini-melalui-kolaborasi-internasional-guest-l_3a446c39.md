"IMPROVING THE QUALITY OF EARLY CHILDHOOD EDUCATION THROUGH INTERNATIONAL COLLABORATION: GUEST LECTURE WITH DR. ZAMZAMI ZAINUDDIN" FLINDERS UNIVERSITY, ADELAIDE

UPI Cibiru - The Guest Lecture activity held by the PGPAUD Undergraduate Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru on Thursday, November 21, 2024, aims to enrich students' academic insights through international collaboration. The presence of Dr. Zamzami Zainuddin, a guest lecturer from Flinders University, Adelaide, South Australia, provides an opportunity for students and lecturers to explore current topics in early childhood education from a global perspective. This activity reflects the PGPAUD Undergraduate Study Program's commitment to providing science-based learning that is up-to-date and relevant to global educational needs.

The purpose of this Guest Lecture is to expand international academic networks and motivate students to gain a broader perspective in preparing themselves as professional educators. Furthermore, this activity also opens up opportunities for collaborative research and educational innovation between the UPI Cibiru Campus and international academic institutions, particularly Flinders University. Dr. Zamzami's presence inspired a constructive and insightful discussion, which is expected to strengthen students' analytical and critical skills.

It is hoped that this activity will not only serve as a forum for knowledge transfer, but also become a strategic step in realizing UPI's vision of going global. By continuing to present international collaborations, the UPI Cibiru Campus PGPAUD Undergraduate Study Program is expected to produce a generation of educators who are competent, innovative, and ready to compete at the global level. This Guest Lecture also affirms UPI's existence as an educational institution that is dynamic and adaptive to global developments. Ibud, 21/11/2024.
