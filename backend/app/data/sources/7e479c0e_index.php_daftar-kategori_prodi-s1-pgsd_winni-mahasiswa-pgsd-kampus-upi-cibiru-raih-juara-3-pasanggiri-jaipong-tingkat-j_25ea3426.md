Winni, a PGSD Student at UPI Cibiru Campus, Won Third Place in the Pasanggiri Jaipong Competition at the West Java Level

On April 27, 2025, the prestigious Pasanggiri Jaipong West Java 2025 event was held at Teras Sunda, Bandung. This activity was organized by the CEO of Gatotkaca Production and attended by 75 general participants from various regions in West Java. This competition is an important platform to channel traditional dance talents, especially Jaipong, as well as a form of preserving local culture that is full of values ​​and identity. The performances of the participants were judged not only on technique and aesthetics, but also on the soul and strength in displaying the unique character of Jaipong movements.

Winni, a Class of 2024 student from the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program, won third place in this competition. She expressed her gratitude and pride for this achievement, especially for being able to bring honor to her alma mater in a provincial-level event through arts and culture. "This activity gives me the opportunity to continue developing and maintaining my passion for the arts. I hope this achievement can inspire my friends to continue exploring their talents," she said. She advised, "Stay motivated, keep trying, and don't be afraid to try, because every small step towards success begins with courage."
