The Internal Quality Audit Visit to the Computer Engineering Study Program at UPI Cibiru Was a Success

The Computer Engineering Study Program of the Indonesian Education University (UPI) Cibiru Campus received a visit from the Internal Quality Audit (AMI) on Friday, October 17, 2025. This activity is part of the academic quality assurance cycle that is routinely carried out by UPI to ensure that all study programs implement education, research, and community service systems in accordance with the quality standards set by the university.

During the activity, the audit team evaluated various aspects, including academic planning, learning implementation, assessment of learning outcomes, research and community service activities, administrative governance, and study program quality documentation . In addition, interviews were conducted with representatives of lecturers, students, and educational staff to obtain a comprehensive overview of the implementation of the quality assurance system at the study program level.

The audit team commended the study program's readiness and performance, demonstrating a strong commitment to implementing a quality assurance system. The audit results are expected to form the basis for developing a follow-up action plan (RTL) that will strengthen the quality culture and support future study program accreditation.
