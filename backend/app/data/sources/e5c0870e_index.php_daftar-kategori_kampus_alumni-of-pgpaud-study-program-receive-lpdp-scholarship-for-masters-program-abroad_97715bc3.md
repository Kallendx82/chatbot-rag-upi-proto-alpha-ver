ALUMNI OF PGPAUD STUDY PROGRAM RECEIVE LPDP SCHOLARSHIP FOR MASTERS PROGRAM ABROAD

UPI Cibiru - Congratulations to Dhiya Adzkia Fadhila Haidar, alumni of the 2018 PGPAUD Study Program, for her extraordinary achievement in obtaining an LPDP Scholarship to continue her studies to the Masters level at Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand, in the Master of Educational Psychology program. This achievement is real evidence of dedication, hard work, and extraordinary fighting spirit in the world of education.

We are very proud and inspired by your achievements. This success is not only a source of pride for yourself, but also for your alma mater and all your colleagues in the PGPAUD Study Program. We are sure that the knowledge you will gain there will make a major contribution to the development of education, especially in the field of educational psychology in Indonesia.

May your study journey at Victoria University of Wellington bring you many valuable experiences and pave the way for more successes in the future. Stay enthusiastic and keep achieving. Ibud, 7/10/2024.
