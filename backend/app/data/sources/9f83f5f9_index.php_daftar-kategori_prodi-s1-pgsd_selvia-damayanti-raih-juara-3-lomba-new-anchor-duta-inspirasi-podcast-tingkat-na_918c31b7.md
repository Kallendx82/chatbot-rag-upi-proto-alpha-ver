Selvia Damayanti Wins Third Place in the National Podcast Inspiration Ambassador New Anchor Competition, Supporting Youth's Role in the SDGs

Selvia Damayanti achieved a proud achievement by winning 3rd place in the New Anchor Competition at the 19th National Podcast Inspiration Ambassador Batch event held on January 16–28, 2026.
This event carried the theme "Youth Voice in Action: Communication for Digital Impact", which is in line with the implementation of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), especially SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 16 (Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions) through strengthening communication literacy and participation of the younger generation in the digital space.
In the competition, Selvia demonstrated her ability as a new anchor who is communicative, confident, and able to convey messages effectively and responsibly. Her role as a young communicator also supports efforts to disseminate educational, inclusive, and ethical information, in line with the spirit of sustainable development.
This achievement is not only a personal achievement, but also reflects the contribution of the younger generation in supporting the achievement of the SDGs through the use of digital media as a means of spreading inspiration and positive social change.
