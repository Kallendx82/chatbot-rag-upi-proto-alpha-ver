Circular: Make-up Course Contracts for Students Who Have Paid UKT for the Odd Semester of 2024/2025

Bandung, October 10, 2024 — The Indonesian University of Education (UPI) issued an official circular regarding the implementation of follow-up course contracts for students who have completed the Single Tuition Fee (UKT) payment for the odd semester of the 2024/2025 academic year.

In the circular, it is explained that students who have not had the opportunity to complete a course contract on the previously determined schedule can take a follow-up contract according to the following provisions:

Follow-up Contract Period :

The follow-up course contract will be implemented from October 10, 2024 to October 15, 2024 .

Condition :

Students are required to show proof of UKT payment.
Fill out the application form for a follow-up course contract through the academic system or faculty administration counter.

Contract Procedures :

Students can choose courses according to schedule availability and class capacity.
Contract submission must obtain approval from the supervisor.

Contract Completion Deadline :

The entire process must be completed by October 15, 2024 .

Further information can be accessed through the university's official website or through the academic section of each faculty.
