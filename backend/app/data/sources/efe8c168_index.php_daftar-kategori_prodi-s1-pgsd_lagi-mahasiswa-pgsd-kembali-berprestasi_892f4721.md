Again! Elementary School Teacher Education Students Achieve Another Success

Three PGSD students have again achieved brilliant achievements. They are Aura Aurelya Putri Andhien, a student from the Class of 2024, Selvia Damayanti, a student from the Class of 2023, and Endah Parawangsa, a student from the Class of 2021. Aura Aurelya Putri Andhien won 2nd place in the National Short Story Competition on Character Education - 9th Anniversary of BSO, which was held online by Madura Pintar. Meanwhile, Selvia Damayanti and Endah Parawangsa became the Finalists (Top 10) of the Reading Ambassadors of Bandung Regency. This achievement adds to the treasure trove of achievements of PGSD students at the UPI Cibiru Campus. This proves that in addition to focusing on academics as prospective elementary school teachers, the students have succeeded in proving that they are able to compete in various competencies.

Thank you for your efforts in bringing honor to the Elementary School Teacher Education Study Program and the UPI Cibiru Campus Institution. Congratulations!
