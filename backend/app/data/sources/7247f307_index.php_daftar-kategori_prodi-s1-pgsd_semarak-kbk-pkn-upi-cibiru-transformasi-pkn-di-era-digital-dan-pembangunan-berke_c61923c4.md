The Excitement of the UPI Cibiru Civics KBK: Civics Transformation in the Digital Era and Sustainable Development

The Civics Education Expertise Group (KBK) of the Elementary School Teacher Education Program at the Indonesian University of Education, Cibiru Campus, held a Webinar on the Excitement of the KBK for Civics Education on February 25, 2026, via Zoom Meeting. The event, which took place from 1:00 PM to 4:40 PM WIB, raised the theme "Civics Transformation in the Digital Era and Sustainable Development" and was attended by more than 200 participants, including students, academics, and education practitioners.

This webinar presented five speakers, namely Nur Asifin, M.Pd., Dr. Edwin Nurdiansyah, M.Pd., Nasran, S.Pd., M.Pd., Dr. Dinie Anggraeni Dewi, M.Pd., and Dr. Agil Nanggala, M.Pd. The speakers discussed various important issues related to the development of Civic Education in the digital era, such as political literacy on social media, the role of Civic Education in building environmental awareness, the relationship between Civic Education and digital literacy, and the contribution of Civic Education in addressing various social pathologies in society.

Through this activity, the Civics Education KBK (Citizenship Education KBK) of the Elementary School Teacher Education Department (PGSD) at UPI Cibiru Campus strives to provide a space for academic discussion that encourages the strengthening of Civics as a discipline that adapts to current developments. It is hoped that this activity will enrich participants' insights and strengthen their commitment to developing a critical, integrated young generation that cares about sustainable development in the digital age.
