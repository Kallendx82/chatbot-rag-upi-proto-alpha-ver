Multimedia Education Students from UPI Cibiru Participate in Job Readiness Training Themed "Green Skills and Green Jobs"

Bandung, November 20, 2024 — Students of the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus participated in a job readiness training themed "Green Skills and Green Jobs" held on November 20, 2024, at the 2nd Floor of the FPMIPA Auditorium Building. This training aims to equip students with skills and insights related to sustainability and career opportunities in the environmentally friendly sector.

In addition to the material sessions, the training also included interactive workshops, such as job simulations in green technology and discussions on career opportunities in the multimedia sector. Participants gained hands-on experience integrating sustainability principles into digital media design, project management, and technological innovation.

This program is expected to prepare students to be part of the future workforce that is not only competitive, but also cares about sustainability.

With activities like this, the Multimedia Education Study Program at UPI Cibiru continues to demonstrate its commitment to producing graduates who are adaptive to the needs of the ever-evolving job market.
