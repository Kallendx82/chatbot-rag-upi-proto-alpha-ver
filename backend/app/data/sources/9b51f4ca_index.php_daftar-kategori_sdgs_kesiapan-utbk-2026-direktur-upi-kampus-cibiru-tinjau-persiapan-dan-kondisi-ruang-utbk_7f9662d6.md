UTBK 2026 Readiness: UPI Cibiru Campus Director Reviews Preparations and Conditions of UTBK Room

UPI Cibiru Campus ensured the readiness of the 2026 UTBK implementation through a direct inspection by the Director of UPI Cibiru Campus, Prof. Karim Suryadi, on campus in April 2026. This activity aimed to thoroughly ensure that the exam rooms, including the readiness of computers, internet networks, lighting, ventilation, and environmental cleanliness were in optimal condition. Consequently, technical readiness could be measured properly so that potential obstacles during the exam could be minimized. This inspection is part of the institution's commitment to providing professional and quality educational services.

This activity also relates to SDG 4 (Quality Education) by providing adequate examination facilities and supporting a quality higher education selection process, as well as SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure) by utilizing computer-based technology in exam administration. This demonstrates UPI Cibiru Campus' commitment to supporting sustainable educational development.
