Service Information

The UPI Public Information Disclosure Unit (PPID) was established as a strategic step in implementing Law Number 14 of 2008 concerning Public Information Disclosure (UU KIP). The PPID's function is to ensure openness, transparency, and accountability in the delivery of public information within the Indonesian University of Education.

As the primary mandate holder,  the UPI PPID is led by the University Secretary (SU)  , who reports directly to the UPI Chancellor. The Chancellor acts as  the UPI PPID's superior  , ensuring that all public information activities and policies are managed in accordance with applicable regulations.

To carry out its functions,  PPID UPI collaborates with five main work units  , each of which has a specific role in supporting information and documentation management, namely:

Public Relations Office : Responsible for public information services. Public Relations is at the forefront of conveying information to the public and ensuring transparent and accurate access to information.

Archives Office : Manages archiving and documentation professionally, ensuring that all information produced by UPI is documented and stored properly for future public access needs.

Legal Office : Handles legal aspects related to the dissemination of public information and ensures that UPI PPID activities are carried out in accordance with applicable legal provisions, including the protection of confidential information.

Directorate of Information Technology Systems (STI) : Develops and maintains information technology systems that support transparency and digital information dissemination. STI ensures that UPI's online platforms are adequate to support public access to information.

Directorate of Planning and Organization (Renor) : Responsible for preparing reports and evaluating the performance of UPI's PPID. Renor ensures that the reporting process complies with established standards, both for internal and external needs.

These five work units not only work within their respective fields but also collaborate in communication and coordination to ensure effective and efficient public information management. They also act as  coordinators for other work units, acting as the Implementing Public Information Officer (PPID) , coordinating all units within UPI to support public information transparency efforts.

With this organized structure, PPID UPI is committed to providing open and accessible information to the wider public, in line with the principles of information transparency mandated in the KIP Law.

Memberikan layanan informasi publik yang cepat, tepat, dan sederhana yang sesuai dengan ketentuan peraturan perundang-undangan;

Menetapkan prosedur operasional penyebarJuasan Informasi Publik;
