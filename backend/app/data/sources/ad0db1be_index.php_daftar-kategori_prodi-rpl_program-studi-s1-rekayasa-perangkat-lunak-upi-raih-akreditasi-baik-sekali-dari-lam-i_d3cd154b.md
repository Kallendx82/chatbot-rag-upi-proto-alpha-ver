UPI's Undergraduate Software Engineering Program Receives "Excellent" Accreditation from LAM-INFOKOM

The Software Engineering Undergraduate Program at the Indonesia University of Education (UIE) has successfully earned an Accreditation Certificate with a "Very Good" rating from LAM-INFOKOM . This achievement demonstrates the program's commitment to providing quality education, developing student competencies, and providing superior academic governance in the fields of software technology and engineering.

This accreditation status reflects UPI's commitment to improving the quality of learning, research, and contributions to human resource development in the era of digital transformation. This achievement also marks a significant step in strengthening the position of the UPI RPL Study Program as an educational institution ready to produce innovative, character-driven, and globally competitive technological talent.

UPI Cibiru Campus expresses its appreciation to all academics, industry partners, and stakeholders who have collaborated in supporting this achievement. We hope this achievement will encourage the development of higher quality education and academic services.
