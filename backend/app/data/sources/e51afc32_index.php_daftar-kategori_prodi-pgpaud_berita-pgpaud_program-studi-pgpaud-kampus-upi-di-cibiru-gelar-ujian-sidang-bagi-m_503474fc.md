The PGPAUD Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru Holds a Final Exam for Final Year Students

Cibiru, April 28, 2025 — The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) UPI Cibiru Campus held a Thesis Defense Examination for final year students on Monday, April 28, 2025. This academic activity is the final stage before students are declared to have graduated from the undergraduate program and are ready to step into the professional world.

This year's final exam was attended by 13 students, each presenting their research findings in the field of early childhood education. The students were assessed by the examining faculty to assess their ability to design, implement, and analyze scientific research in accordance with academic standards.

The trial proceeded smoothly, orderly, and enthusiastically. The students demonstrated strong academic readiness and ability, reflecting the learning process they have undergone during their studies at the PGPAUD UPI Cibiru Campus.

The Early Childhood Education Program (PGPAUD) expresses its appreciation and congratulations to all participants. It is hoped that this achievement will serve as a stepping stone for students to continue their work, contribute to society, and become professional early childhood educators with integrity.
