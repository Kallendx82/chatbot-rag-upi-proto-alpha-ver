UPI Campus PGPAUD in Cibiru Holds Final Exams for Final Year Students

Cibiru, August 28, 2025 - The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) of the Indonesian University of Education, Cibiru Campus, has successfully held a Thesis Defense Examination for final year students on Thursday, August 28, 2025. This activity was attended by students who have completed the entire series of academic processes and are ready to enter the final stage of completing their studies.

The final exam proceeded in an orderly manner, with examiners and supervisors present to assess the substance of the research, academic ability, and students' readiness to present their work. This exam was a crucial opportunity for students to demonstrate their academic competence, research skills, and understanding of early childhood education issues.

The PGPAUD Study Program expresses its appreciation to all students who have demonstrated dedication and hard work in completing their research. Successfully passing the final exam is a significant achievement and the first step toward a professional role as a competent and competitive PAUD educator.
