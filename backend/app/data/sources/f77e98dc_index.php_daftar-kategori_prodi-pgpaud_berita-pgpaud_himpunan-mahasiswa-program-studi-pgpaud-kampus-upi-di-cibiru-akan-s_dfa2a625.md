The Student Association of the PGPAUD Study Program at the UPI Campus in Cibiru Will Hold the 6.0 PAUD Celebration Activity

Cibiru, December 10, 2024 – The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Student Association at the UPI Cibiru Campus is ready to host the upcoming Gebyar PAUD 6.0 event. Gebyar PAUD is an annual event held by HIMAPAUD UPI Cibiru and aims to provide a platform for students, the community, and all parties involved in the field of early childhood education to participate in various exciting activities.

This year, Gebyar PAUD 6.0 carries the theme "Diving into Diversity and Curiosity to Continuously Innovate Children's Creativity." This theme was chosen to encourage innovation in early childhood learning and introduce diversity as an important part of their creative development.

The Gebyar PAUD 6.0 event will consist of several activities, including a Student Competition , Fun Fest , and a Parenting Seminar . The Student Competition will provide an opportunity for participants to showcase their creativity and abilities in various fields, while the Fun Fest will be a fun and entertaining recreational event. The Parenting Seminar, which will also be held, aims to provide knowledge and tips for parents and educators in supporting the growth and development of early childhood.

"The 6.0 PAUD Celebration is a highly anticipated event for students and the community. Through this event, we want to encourage everyone to participate in a collaborative effort to develop children's creativity and potential from an early age," said one of the event's organizers.
