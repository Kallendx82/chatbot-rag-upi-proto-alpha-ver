Isra Mi'raj 1447 H Commemoration at UPI Cibiru: Restructuring Life Orientation and Strengthening the Character of the Academic Community

In order to commemorate the Isra Mi'raj of the Prophet Muhammad SAW, the Indonesian Education University (UPI) Cibiru Campus held a religious activity on Thursday, January 23, 2026. This activity was held at the UPI Cibiru Campus and was attended by the academic community of the UPI Cibiru Campus.

This year's Isra Mi'raj commemoration carries the theme "Mi'raj in Restructuring Life's Orientation." This theme was chosen to encourage the academic community to reflect on the meaning of the Isra Mi'raj event as a spiritual momentum in restructuring the direction and purpose of life, both in academic, professional, and social contexts. This reflection aligns with the university's commitment to supporting the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly in developing human resources with character, integrity, and social awareness.
