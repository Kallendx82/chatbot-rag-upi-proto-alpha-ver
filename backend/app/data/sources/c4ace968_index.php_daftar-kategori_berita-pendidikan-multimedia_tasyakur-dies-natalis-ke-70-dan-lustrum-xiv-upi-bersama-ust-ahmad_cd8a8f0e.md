Tasyakur 70th Anniversary and 14th Lustrum of UPI with Ustaz Ahmad Abu Bakar Al-Habsyi

Bandung, October 30, 2024 — To commemorate the 70th anniversary and 14th Lustrum of the Indonesian Education University (UPI), a Tasyakur (gathering ) event was held on Friday, November 1, 2024, featuring a religious sermon by Ustaz Ahmad Abu Bakar Al-Habsyi . The event took place solemnly at the Al Furqaan Mosque and was attended by all UPI academics, alumni, and invited guests.

This thanksgiving event served as a moment of reflection and gratitude for UPI's long journey in producing quality educators. In his sermon, Ustaz Ahmad Abu Bakar Al-Habsyi emphasized the importance of maintaining integrity, instilling the value of sincerity in pursuing knowledge, and continuing to contribute to society.

In addition to the sermon, the event also included a communal prayer, a recitation of verses from the Quran, and the cutting of a tumpeng (rice cone) as a symbol of gratitude. The sense of togetherness was palpable throughout each event, affirming UPI's commitment to continued advancement as a leading educational institution in Indonesia.

Hopefully UPI will continue to be successful and contribute positively in building the world of education in the future.
