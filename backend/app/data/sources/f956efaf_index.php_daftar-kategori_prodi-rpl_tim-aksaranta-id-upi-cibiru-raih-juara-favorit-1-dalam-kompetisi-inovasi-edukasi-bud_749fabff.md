The Aksaranta.id Team from UPI Cibiru Won 1st Place in the Cultural Education Innovation Competition (SDGs 4 & 11)

The Software Engineering (RPL) Study Program at UPI Cibiru Campus has once again achieved a proud achievement.
The Aksaranta.id team won first place in the cultural education innovation competition through a digital learning project focused on exploring Batak script, language, and culture using a structured and accessible learning approach.

This innovation is a real contribution from students in supporting the achievement of sustainable development goals, in particular:

SDGs 4 – Quality Education:
through the development of innovative and inclusive learning media to expand access to culture-based education.

SDGs 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities:
by promoting the preservation of local culture as an important part of community identity and community sustainability.

This achievement shows that UPI Cibiru RPL students are not only competent in technology development, but also have a commitment to supporting social and cultural sustainability through educational innovation.

Congratulations to the Aksaranta.id team on this extraordinary achievement. May they continue to have a positive impact and inspire the development of educational technology based on the preservation of national culture.
