INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION BETWEEN TERMEZ STATE UNIVERSITY, UZBEKISTAN AND UPI CIBIRU CAMPUS

UPI Cibiru Campus has once again established an international partnership with TERMEZ STATE UNIVERSITY in Uzbekistan. This collaboration aims to improve the quality of education, particularly at both universities. Five study programs are collaborating: Elementary School Teacher Education (PGSD), Early Childhood Teacher Education (PGPAUD), Multimedia Education, Software Engineering (RPL), and Computer Engineering (Tekkom).
