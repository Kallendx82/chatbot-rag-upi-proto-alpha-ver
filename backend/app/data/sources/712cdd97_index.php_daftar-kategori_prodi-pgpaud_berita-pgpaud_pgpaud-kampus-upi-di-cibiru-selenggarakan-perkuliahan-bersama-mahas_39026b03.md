UPI Campus PGPAUD in Cibiru Holds Joint Lectures with RPL East Kutai Students for the Odd-Number Academic Year 2025/2026

Cibiru, September 9, 2025 - The Early Childhood Education Teacher Education (PGPAUD) Study Program of the Indonesian University of Education, Cibiru Campus, officially started lectures with students of the Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) of East Kutai on Monday, September 1, 2025, as part of the implementation of the Odd Semester of the 2025/2026 Academic Year. This activity is a form of ongoing collaboration between UPI Cibiru Campus and the East Kutai Regency Government in improving the quality of human resources, especially PAUD educators in the region.

Lectures are conducted online, allowing students in East Kutai to participate in learning more flexibly without geographical constraints. Early childhood education (RPL) students, who are educators and practitioners of early childhood education, have the opportunity to continue their formal education and strengthen their academic understanding through structured learning experiences with UPI Cibiru PGPAUD lecturers.

This activity focuses not only on strengthening the theory and practice of early childhood education but also on developing professional competencies relevant to field needs. Students are given a space to discuss, reflect on best practices, and improve the quality of education services in their respective regions.

The implementation of the East Kutai RPL program directly contributes to the achievement of SDG 4: Quality Education, specifically the target of increasing access to inclusive and quality higher education. The RPL program provides lifelong learning opportunities for local communities, expanding access for educators who previously faced barriers to continuing their studies.

By facilitating distance learning and recognizing work experience, PGPAUD UPI Cibiru helps create equal opportunities, strengthens the competency of educators, and supports the realization of better and more sustainable early childhood education services.
