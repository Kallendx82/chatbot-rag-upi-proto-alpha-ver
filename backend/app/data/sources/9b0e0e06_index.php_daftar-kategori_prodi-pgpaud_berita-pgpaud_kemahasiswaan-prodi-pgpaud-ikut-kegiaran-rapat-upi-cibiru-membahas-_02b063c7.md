The PGPAUD Study Program Student Affairs participated in the UPI Cibiru Meeting to discuss strengthening student services and achievements in the 2025 Student Affairs Meeting.

Cibiru, February 10, 2025 — The Cibiru Campus of Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) held a Student Meeting on Monday, February 10, 2025, attended by the leadership and academic community. Also present at the meeting were the Director of the UPI Campus in Cibiru, representatives from the UPI Bumi Siliwangi Directorate of Student Affairs, the Deputy Director, lecturers, and educational staff.

This meeting served as a strategic forum to discuss various issues and develop more inclusive, progressive, and aligned student services between regional and central campuses. Key points discussed included:

Assistance for students, both in the form of financial support and psychological services, to support student welfare during their education.

Strengthening student activities so that students are more active in organizations, self-development activities, and social contributions.

Talent scouting to identify and support students who have the potential to participate in national level competitions.

Official scholarship information, including scholarships that have a Decree (SK) and access to UPI's endowment fund.

Equalizing recognition of regional and central campuses, in order to strengthen institutional identity and equal access to services and achievements.

Development of student applications, which function to monitor student activities, achievement, competency development, and awards for active students.

Strengthening the Independent Learning and Independent Campus (MBKM) program through the integration of student activities and the development of student potential across fields.

Through this meeting, UPI Cibiru affirmed its commitment to creating a supportive, equal campus ecosystem and empowering students to develop academically and non-academically.
