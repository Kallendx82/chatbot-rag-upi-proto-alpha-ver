The UPI Cibiru PGPAUD PkM Team Improves PAUD Teacher Competence Through Environmentally Based APE Development Training

Cibiru, August 9, 2025 – The Community Service (PkM) Team of the Early Childhood Education Teacher Education Study Program (PGPAUD) at the UPI Cibiru Campus held a PAUD Teacher Competency Strengthening Program in the Development of Environmentally Based Educational Game Tools (APE) . The activity, which was carried out at the UPI Cibiru Campus, was attended by PkM partner PAUD teachers from the Regency and City of Bandung. The PkM team consisted of M. Helmi Ismail, M.Pd., Ririn Despriliani, S.Pd., M.Si., Mirawati, M.Pd., Ayu Hopiani, M.Pd., along with PGPAUD students. This training was packaged in the form of interactive discussions, workshops, and mentoring to encourage teachers to be more creative in utilizing simple materials in the surrounding environment as children's learning media.

This program is oriented towards improving the quality of education in line with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) point 4: Quality Education , which is to ensure that early childhood receives quality education with inclusive and meaningful learning experiences. One of the participating teachers said, "Through this training, we have become more confident in designing innovative and child-friendly learning media by utilizing the surrounding environment." Through this activity, it is hoped that the competence of PAUD teachers in creating innovative APE will increase so that they can support the optimal growth and development of early childhood.
