Recap INNOMOTION: The Evolution of Car Design – Innovation in Style and Sustainability
November 1, 2024
Berita UPI Tasikmalaya, Desain Produk Industri

Friday, October 18, 2024 – Studio Gambar, In the rapidly advancing world of car design, INNOMOTION returned to showcase innovations that blend style with sustainability, marking a promising evolution. Under the theme The Evolution of Car Design: Innovation in Style and Sustainability, the event presented a series of insightful discussions led by automotive design expert Prof. Hariri Abdullah.

Prof. Hariri Abdullah opened the session by discussing the evolution of car design over the past decade. Through both visual and technical analysis, he highlighted transformations in car design, now more energy-efficient, aerodynamic, and aesthetically refined. Prof. Hariri emphasized that sustainability is no longer just a trend but has become a new standard that guides automotive companies to produce environmentally-friendly vehicles.

The event did not only focus on the technical aspects of car design but also explored the values these designs embody. Sustainable living has now become an integral part of modern car design. By reducing carbon emissions and enhancing fuel efficiency, these designs reflect the aspiration for a greener future.

The INNOMOTION seminar became an inspiring platform for designers, engineers, and automotive enthusiasts to explore new possibilities that could reshape the future of the automotive industry. The event underscored that in this era of boundless innovation, car design is not just about form or aesthetics; it’s a commitment to our planet. Prof. Hariri Abdullah closed the event with a powerful statement: “Innovation without sustainability is no longer an option. Through future-focused design, we can create meaningful change.” (Dilardi Ramadhan)
